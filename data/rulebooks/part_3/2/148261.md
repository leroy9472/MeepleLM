# SeaFall – Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective  
SeaFall is a legacy campaign game set in a world of exploration, trade, and conflict. Players are governors of provinces, seeking to expand their influence through exploration, building, raiding, and diplomacy. The goal is to earn the most **Glory** by the end of the campaign. Glory is earned through successful endeavors, claiming milestones, building structures, hiring advisors, and plundering during raids.

### 2. Components  
- Province boards  
- Ships (flagship and support)  
- Advisors (in forum and council rooms)  
- Goods (wood, spice, linen, iron)  
- Treasure cards  
- Research cards  
- Upgrade cards  
- Enmity tokens and stickers  
- Fortune and Reputation tokens  
- Event deck  
- Uncharted Waters deck  
- Captain’s Booke  
- Astrolabe (first player marker)  
- Dice (custom: blank, weak success, standard success, strong success)  
- Garrison markers, province markers, hold goods  

*(Note: Physical components are assumed based on standard SeaFall setup.)*

### 3. Setup  
- Each player selects a province and takes matching components.  
- Place starting ships in home harbor.  
- Shuffle Advisors into the Forum.  
- Shuffle Event, Treasure, and Research decks.  
- Players sit clockwise by title (lowest to highest).  
- Lowest title receives the Astrolabe (first player).  

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
Each round consists of the following steps:

1. **Reveal Event Card** (by first player)  
2. **Player Turns** (each player takes one turn)  
3. **Resolve Event Card**  
4. **Check Glory Track** for win condition  
5. **Advance Astrolabe** (check if Winter begins)  

#### Your Turn – 5 Steps:
1. **Hire Advisor or Buy Treasure**  
   - *Hire Advisor*: Choose one from the Forum, pay gold or Reputation, place face-up in Council Room, refill Forum. Gain printed Glory.  
   - *Buy Treasure*: Choose one from the deck, pay gold, discard one matching warehouse good for −8 gold discount. Gain printed Glory.  

2. **Activate an Advisor**  
   - Move one unexhausted (face-up) Advisor from Council Room to Active Advisor space. Gain all abilities until end of turn.  

3. **Hire a Guild & Perform 2 Actions**  
   - Choose one guild. Do any two of its three actions, in any order.  
   - After any action, **claim one Milestone** (only once per turn).  
   - May offer 1 Reputation to another player to use powers in a region they control.  

4. **Claim a Milestone** (if applicable, only once per turn)  
   - If a milestone condition is met, claim it and follow instructions.  

5. **Exhaust Active Advisor**  
   - Turn the active Advisor face-down in your Council Room.  

### 5. Core Mechanics  

#### Guild Actions  

**All Guilds – A) Sail**  
- Move both ships up to their Sail value (minimum 1 space, even if 0).  
- Ships may rearrange goods between them if together near land or in same region.  
- If ending in an **empty sea space west of the first 4 columns**, draw one **Uncharted Waters card** (only one card even if both ships end in separate empty spaces).  
- Entering **Dangerous Waters**: Attempt a **Sail Endeavor**. May use second ship as support.  
- **Currents**: May exit in current direction without spending movement.  
- **Atolls**: Regions where ships may rearrange holds without being in uncharted waters.  

**Merchants Guild – B) Buy / C) Sell**  
- **Buy**: Buy up to 2 goods where your ship is present. Cost: 3 gold + 1 gold per **enmity** on the island.  
- **Sell**: Sell up to 2 goods in a region with matching **sell market** and color. Sell for 6 gold each.  

**Builders Guild – B) Repair or Upgrade / C) Build**  
- **Repair**: Refresh all upgrades, dismiss all damage cards. Sunken ships return to home harbor.  
- **Upgrade**: Buy one upgrade matching dock color. Spend 8 gold, or 1 matching resource for −8 gold discount. Gain 1 Glory (0 if returning an upgrade).  
- **Build**:  
   - Build structure: Use 1 matching resource for −8 gold discount. Gain 1 Glory (0 if returning).  
   - Build colony: Spend 6 goods (at least 1 of each type) at an island. Choose colony from chest.  
     - Gain 3 Glory (inactive) or 4 Glory (active, place province marker).  
     - Governor controls sea space.  

**Explorers Guild – B) Explore / C) Research**  
- **Explore**:  
   - Perform **Explore Endeavor** on unexplored site.  
   - Success: Gain 1 Glory, cross off symbol in Captain’s Booke, read entry.  
   - **Island in column without island**: Defense = column number. Success = gain Glory, take random island sticker, place according to instructions.  
   - **Tomb**: See Tombs! section.  
- **Research**: Pay 3 gold, draw 1 Research card. Keep one in Treasure Room (face-up), discard rest.  

**Soldiers Guild – B) Raid / C) Collect Taxes or Build**  
- **Raid**:  
   - Raid island site, ship, or colony.  
   - Use **Raid Endeavor**.  
   - Plunder if successful.  
- **Collect Taxes**: Gain +3 gold.  
- **Build**: Same as Builders Guild.  

#### Endeavors  
- **Dice Pool** = Flagship’s stat (Explore/Raid/Sail)  
  + Support ship at same location (+1 die)  
  + Upgrades, Advisors, Buildings, Research cards  
  − Damage cards, Garrisons, Enmity  
- **Raid Endeavor Adjustments**:  
  - − Garrison value  
  - − 1 die per **your enmity** on defender’s site or in their region  
  - − 1 die per **defending support ship**  
  - +1 die per **defender’s enmity** in your **At War With** section  
  - Ship raids: +1 die if attacker controls space, −1 die if defender controls  
- **Roll Dice**: Count successes (non-blank unless modified).  
- **Fortune Tokens**: Spend to convert blank → standard success (cannot use at dangerous sites).  
- **Success**: At least one success and flagship not sunk.  
- **Damage** = Defense − # of successes. Applied to **flagship only**.  

#### Applying Damage  
- For each point of damage:  
  - Exhaust a ship upgrade, **or**  
  - Draw a damage/curse card  
- Ship sinks if:  
  - 3 damage cards drawn, **or**  
  - 3 curse cards drawn  
- **Sunken Ship**:  
  - Return upgrades (exhausted), lose 1 Glory per upgrade  
  - Return all goods to supply  
  - Ship removed until repaired  

#### Plunder  
After successful **Raid**, plunder a site. Successes must equal or exceed **Plunder Value** (blue number).  

**Island Sites**:  
- **Good**: Take good (from supply if empty)  
- **Mine**: Take gold = mine value  
- **Dock**: Take ship upgrade (color = 10 gold value)  
- **Market**: Take up to 2 matching goods  

**Province Sites**:  
- **Field**: Take gold = field value. Cannot produce in next Winter if enmity present.  
- **Structure**: Remove to sideboard (exhausted). Owner loses 1 Glory. Take matching good or ½ cost in gold.  
- **Vault**: Take ½ gold (round up)  
- **Council Room**: Take any Advisor (refreshed) or dismiss. Adjust Glory.  
- **Warehouse**: Take ½ goods (round up)  
- **Treasure Room**: Take 1 card (refreshed). Adjust Glory.  

**Ship Raids**:  
- Each success: Apply 1 damage **or** plunder 1 item from flagship’s hold  
- Place 1 enmity token in defender’s **At War With** per damage/plunder  
- If attacker controls region, place only **1 total enmity**  

#### Enmity  
- **On Islands**:  
  - +1 gold cost per token for goods, repairs, upgrades  
  - −1 die per token when raiding  
  - Prevents further raids on site for year  
- **On Provinces**:  
  - Same as above  
  - Fields with enmity don’t harvest  
  - Building sites with enmity can’t be built on  
- **At War With**:  
  - Each token gives +1 die when raiding that player  
  - Return tokens before placing your own when raiding  
- **Winter**:  
  - Move island enmity → cover garrison  
  - Move province enmity → At War With  
- **End of Game**:  
  - Roll die for each unused Fortune/Reputation  
  - 1 success = remove 1 enmity **token**  
  - 2 successes = cross out 1 enmity **sticker**  
  - Remaining tokens become stickers  

#### Tombs!  
- May explore open tombs. Read entry from back of Captain’s Booke.  
- Defense on tomb sticker.  
- May require non-Explore actions. Map hints at solution.  
- Always a second way to gain dice.  
- If explored with **no damage**, cross off entry (cannot repeat).  
- Some successes permanently close tomb.  

#### Relics and Tablets  
- **Tablets**: Stored in province’s Treasure Room. Carry over between games.  
- **Relics**: Carried on ships (take 1 hold space).  
- If ship sinks or no space, write **LOST** on relic, return to chest.  

#### Ker! (Special Rules)  
- **Enmity**: While Ker is with Emperor, province enmity on an island = max of that island or **Patmos**.  
- **Portals of the Ancients**: Transfer between any two portals without movement cost. Dangerous Waters check at origin only.  
- **Hiring Patmos Advisors**: Only if you have a ship at Patmos. Each enmity at Patmos increases cost by 1.  
- **Treasure Maps**: Two matching research cards (same title) form a map. Go to location, use Explore action, read Captain’s Booke entry.  
- **Conquering Colony**: Raid colony’s defense. If no damage, take card, structures, goods. Place enmity = defense number.  
- **Conquering Ker**: Immediately take colony card, flip, name new colony. You are governor (always active). Place 4 permanent enmity on Patmos, 2 on any other island.  

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
- **Game ends** when a player reaches the Glory threshold (varies by campaign).  
- **Endgame Steps** (in order of lowest to highest current Glory):  
  1. **Winner improves Province**: Improve Field, Garrison, Reputation, Fortune, or take Appellation.  
  2. **Train Advisors**: Each player picks a different Retired Advisor. Must train if slot available.  
  3. **Keep Advisor**: Winner may keep one Advisor only if its gold value < all others’ picks.  
  4. **Improve Ships**: Fill in one empty square on ship.  
  5. **Determine Enmity**: Roll for unused Fortune/Reputation. Remove tokens/stickers as above.  
  6. **Record Glory** in Historical Record.  
  7. **Assign Titles**: Based on campaign Glory. Tiebreaker: lowest score this game.  

#### Glory Sources  
- **Permanent Glory**:  
  - Successful Explore or Raid Endeavor: 1 Glory  
  - Claim Milestone: printed Glory  
- **Stealable Glory**:  
  - Hire Advisor: printed Glory  
  - Buy Treasure: printed Glory  
- **Losable Glory**:  
  - Building: 1 Glory (lost if plundered)  
  - Upgrade: 1 Glory (lost if ship sinks)  

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Can I split the Sail action?**  
  No. Move one ship, rearrange goods (if applicable), then move the other.  

- **Can I use multiple Research cards during an Explore?**  
  Yes. You may use as many as you want.  

- **Do I get Glory for discovering an island?**  
  Yes: 1 Glory for successful Explore endeavor, and 1 Glory for the island (if in column).  

- **What happens to enmity on a conquered colony?**  
  Enmity tokens move to former owner’s At War With during Winter or endgame.  

- **Can I raid a site twice in one year?**  
  No. Sites can only be raided once per year.  

- **Can I use Fortune tokens in Uncharted Waters?**  
  No. Uncharted Waters endeavors award no Glory and cannot use Fortune tokens.  

- **What if I don’t have enough enmity tokens to place?**  
  You cannot complete the action (e.g., raid, conquer). Lose 1 Glory per token you can’t give.  

- **Do goods stay on Smuggler after exhausting?**  
  Yes. Goods remain until hold size changes via upgrade/damage.
