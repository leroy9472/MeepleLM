### 1. Lore & Objective  
You are the last survivors of an ancient civilization, tasked with preserving and passing on millennia-old knowledge through monumental constructions. Build cities, megaliths, and pyramids, and equip them with artifacts and technologies to maximize your legacy. The game ends when any player has at least 14 monument cards in their Past. Victory is determined by scoring victory points (å–œ) while minimizing lost knowledge (å½“). The player with the most victory points wins.

### 3. Setup  
1. **Each player chooses a Builder Board** and places it in front of them.  
   - **Timeline (top)**: Zone for active monuments.  
   - **Past (left)**: Where declined monuments go.  
   - **Artifact Spaces (middle)**: 5 slots for artifact cards.  
   - **Technology Zone (right)**: Sort Technology cards by type (Ancient, Writing, Secret).  
   - **Lost Knowledge Zone (bottom left)**: Place lost knowledge tokens here.  

2. **Prepare the center**:
   - Shuffle all **Builder Cards** into a draw deck; place near table with discard pile space.
   - Deal **10 cards face down** to each player. Players look, keep **6**, discard **4**.
     - 3rd and 4th players draw **1 extra card** before discarding (not in 2-player).
   - Place **3 Technology Tiles** in center: 2 Level I, 1 Level II.
   - Place **3 face-up Level I Technology Cards** on each Level I tile.
   - Place **3 face-up Level II Technology Cards** on the Level II tile.
   - Form **Level I and Level II Technology Card decks** from remaining cards.
   - Place **tokens** in a central reserve.
   - Randomly choose **First Player**, give them the **First Player Token** and **Scorepad**.

*Optional First Game Rule*: Use starting hands (6 cards per symbol). Separate and distribute one random starting deck per player before shuffling the rest.

5. Stacking:  
   - Max 2 cards per space.  
   - New card can go **under or above**, but **cannot be reordered later**.  
   - If only one card remains in a space, it **slides down** to be just above Timeline.  
   - Diagonals are **not adjacent**.  

**Adjacent Cards**:  
- Same space (stacked) or side-by-side in same row.  
- Used for card effects.

**Create an Artifact (V)**:  
- Place on one of 5 artifact spaces.  
- Cannot be covered or moved.  
- Max 5 artifacts per player.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Card vs Rule Conflict**: Card text **overrides** rulebook.  
- **Past Cards**: Only used for:  
  - Fulfilling **Technology requirements**  
  - Triggering **other card effects**  
  - Being **rotated during EXCAVATE**  
  - **Not active** otherwise.  
- **Effect Resolution**:  
  - Must resolve **all possible parts** of an effect.  
  - If multiple effects trigger simultaneously, **choose order**.  
- **Artifacts**:  
  - Never slide.  
  - Max 5.  
  - Only affected by own playerâ€™s effects unless specified.  
- **Token Values**:  
  - 1-value (grey), 5-value (gold). Interchangeable in reserve.  
- **Creating on Non-Starting Space**:  
  - Allowed via discarding extra cards (1 per space).  
  - Cannot move later.  
- **Locked Cards (ðŸ”’)**: Must be played on starting space only.
