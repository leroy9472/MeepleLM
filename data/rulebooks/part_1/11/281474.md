## Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective  
Lands of Galzyr is set in Daimyria, a world of intelligent animalfolks where civilizations thrive amid the Age of Trade. Steam power and invention have transformed society, but adventure still calls to bold souls.  

Four adventurers—Bumir the polecat, Mor the frilled lizard, Aysala the kingfisher, and Keridai the newt—journey to Galzyr, a region where daring deeds earn fame. Each has a unique origin and a once-per-game special ability.  

**Objective**: Earn prestige by completing quests and performing notable feats. In competitive mode, the player with the most prestige wins. In solo or co-op mode, success is measured collectively and culminates in a shared narrative resolution.

### 2. Components  
- **Game board** (two-sided: summer/winter)  
- **12 Location cards** (forming 8 spaces)  
- **Card trays** with dividers:  
  - Library (7 brown dividers, cards 000–299)  
  - Vault (black divider)  
  - Quest slot (purple divider)  
  - Global Save (no divider)  
  - 5 Save slots (teal dividers, one per adventurer)  
- **Cards** (807 total):  
  - Quest cards  
  - Item cards  
  - Companion cards  
  - Adventurer status cards  
  - Local status cards  
  - Global status cards

- **Player components**:  
  - 4 Adventurer boards  
  - 4 Adventurer figures (meeples)  
  - 4 Prestige tokens  
  - Gold dials (on boards)  
  - Skill marks (24 small, 24 large, 6 colors)  
- **Dice**:  
  - 5 base skill dice (black)  
  - 12 advanced skill dice (6 colors)  
  - 1 regular 12-sided die  
- **Tokens**:  
  - 44 Timer tokens (22 pairs)  
  - 6 Quest tokens  
  - 1 Day token  
  - 1 Starting Player token  
  - 1 Souvenir token (no gameplay use)  
- **Digital component**: Book of Adventures (web app at stories.daimyria.fi)  
- **Other**: Travel guide, welcoming letter, achievement sheet, rulebook

### 3. Setup  
1. **Prepare card trays**:  
   - Place brown dividers (000, 050, ..., 250) in a tray.  
   - Place cards 000–299 in ascending order behind matching dividers (library).  
   - Place purple divider in front of library. Take cards 127–148 (22 cards), shuffle, place behind purple divider (quest slot).  
   - Place black divider behind library. Take cards 296–299, place behind it in order (vault).  
   - In second tray, place 5 teal dividers (one per adventurer).  

2. **Assign adventurer cards**:  
   - Each player chooses an adventurer.  
   - Take all cards with that adventurer’s icon (000 and 111 cards) from library and place behind matching teal divider.  
   - Roll 12-sided die; take card with that number from library, place behind teal divider with globe icon.  
   - Take cards 064–089 (26 cards); shuffle the 12 with red back and comet icon, then place all 26 behind same teal divider.  
   - Take 3 frontmost cards from quest slot (behind purple), place behind globe teal divider.  

3. **Game board setup**:  
   - Place board in center. Use summer or winter side per Global Status.  
   - Place Location cards on correct spaces.  
   - Place any Local Status cards with icon touching their affected space.  
   - Place Event deck (face down) nearby.  
   - Place Quest cards (public side up) as noticeboard; place Quest tokens on pickup locations.  
   - Place Global Status cards face up.  
   - Place Day token on randomized starting day (via Book of Adventures).  
   - Place Prestige tokens on 0.  

4. **Player setup**:  
   - Each player takes matching Adventurer board and figure.  
   - Take all cards from their Save slot.  
     - Place Item cards below board.  
     - Place Status and Companion cards beside board.  
     - Keep Quest cards in hand (or public).  
   - Set gold to 10 (unless otherwise specified).  
   - Place Timer tokens: for each card with timer, place one token X days ahead of Day token, and its pair on the timer box.  

5. **Start player**:  
   - Player who most recently completed a task for another takes Starting Player token.  

6. **Starting positions**:  
   - Players (except those with Quest 111 or Isolated status) place meeples on any location. May pick up matching quests (refill noticeboard to 3 each time).

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
Each round has two phases:

#### Adventure Phase (clockwise order)  
Each player takes one turn:
1. **Travel**: Move up to 2 spaces (± movement effects).  
   - Can trade or pick up quests between moves.  
2. **Story**: Resolve one scene from Book of Adventures.  
   - Must choose a mandatory scene (lightning bolt) if available.  
   - Otherwise, choose from:  
     - Quest, Status, Companion, Location, Local Status, or Event card.  
   - Story master (determined by player count) reads scene aloud.  
   - Active player makes decisions and performs skill checks.  
   - May have a **partner** (same location, agrees to assist), who lends:  
     - Skill marks, items, companions, statuses, gold, tags.  
     - Max 2 of each advanced die type.  
     - Can trade during scene.  

**Skill Check Process**:  
- Roll 5 base dice.  
- Swap up to 4 with advanced dice using skill marks (matching or adjacent skills preferred).  
- May reroll all dice once.  
- Apply verb box effects (mandatory if verb matches option; optional otherwise).  
- Story master reveals outcomes, selects leftmost matching.  

**During turn (not in scene)**:  
- Trade gold/items with players in same space.  
- Pick up quests (if at pickup location).  
- Abandon quests (resolve abandon effects).  

#### Calendar Phase  
1. Advance Day token to next day.  
2. Advance day in Book of Adventures.  
3. Resolve Timer tokens (top to bottom) on new day.  
4. Start new round. Start player remains the same.

### 5. Core Mechanics  
- **Prestige**: Earned by completing quests and feats. Tracked on prestige track.  
- **Gold**: Max 20. Tracked on Adventurer board.  
- **Skill Marks**: 4 total per player. Each allows use of one advanced die. Can be swapped during game.  
- **Tags**: On cards and boards. Used in Book of Adventures to unlock special options (starry).  
- **Timers**: Blue timer boxes trigger X days later. Use Timer tokens to track.  
  - **Delay**: Move token further ahead.  
  - **Hasten**: Move token closer; resolve immediately if reached.  
- **Cards**:  
  - **Discard**: Return to library.  
  - **Abandon** (Quests): Resolve effects on private side.  
- **Quests**: Max 3 at a time. Excess must be abandoned.  
- **Items**: Max 3. Discard freely during turn.  
- **Companions/Statuses**: No limit. Reset if gained again.  
- **Story Master**:  
  - 1p: Active player  
  - 2p: Other player  
  - 3p: Player to the right  
  - 4p: Player across

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
**Game End**: After predetermined rounds (based on player count and Discord/Harmony Global Status).  

**Competitive Mode**:  
- Highest prestige wins. Tie = shared victory.  
- Play **Scene 0150** (Discord). Winner is active player.  

**Solo/Co-op Mode**:  
- Total prestige determines success level.  
- Play **Scene 0200** (Harmony). Group chooses most valuable player to read scene.  

**End-of-Game Steps**:  
1. Remove all Timer tokens (do not resolve).  
2. Resolve all Save effects (bottom of cards and boards).  
3. Choose: Pack up or continue.  

**Pack Up**:  
- Players: Place their cards (Quests, Items, Statuses, Companions) in their Save slot.  
- Global: Place remaining cards (noticeboard, Event deck, Locations, Statuses) in Global Save.  
- Store components; preserve gold dials and skill marks.  

**Continue Playing**:  
- Take all from Global Save.  
- Apply Global Statuses.  
- Place Local Statuses.  
- Set prestige to 0.  
- Randomize new starting day.  
- Place Timer tokens (adventurers’ last).  
- Randomize start player.  
- Place meeples (unless Quest 111 or Isolated), pick up quests.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Wrong card taken?** Can cause story inconsistencies. Always verify number and icon.  
- **Multiple same-number cards?** Relative order doesn’t matter.  
- **Timer token stack?** Resolve top-down when day advances.  
- **Partner in scene?** Active player makes decisions. Partner lends resources but does not act.  
- **Starry option?** Must have Tag. Cannot discard source card before/during option.  
- **Skill check limits?** Max 2 of each advanced die type, even with partner.  
- **Event card draw?** Story master draws top card, picks first eligible scene. Card placed as instructed after.  
- **Resetting**:  
  - **Individual**: Return all personal cards to library, reset gold/skills, re-add 000/111 cards.  
  - **Full game**: Return all cards to library, re-randomize quest/event/vault cards, reset all adventurers.
