### Comprehensive Reference Manual

#### 1. Lore & Objective  
Tidal Blades: Heroes of the Reef is set in Naviri, a city under threat from Monsters emerging from The Fold, a dimensional barrier created by past Tidal Blades. Players are aspiring heroes competing in a Tournament to prove their worth and become the next generation of Tidal Blades. The objective is to score the most points by completing Challenges, advancing Traits, fighting Monsters, and achieving personal or public goals.

#### 2. Components  
- **Island Boards (5)**: Citadel of Time, Chronosseum, Lamara Stadium, Droska Ring, The Fold  
- **Champion Board (1)**: Tracks player rankings and advancement  
- **Resource Tokens**: 35 Fruit, 45 Shells  
- **Dice**:  
  - 14 Novice (Level 1, white)  
  - 16 Initiate (Level 2: 8 blue, 8 red)  
  - 16 Elite (Level 3: 8 blue, 8 red)  
  - 12 Guild (Level 4, black)  
- **Danger Dice (3)**: White (Level 1), Yellow (Level 2), Orange (Level 3)  
- **Monster Invasion Die (1)**: Light blue d8  
- **Monster Cards (6 base, 10 Advanced)**  
- **Challenge Cards (42)**: Standard, Starting, and Legendary variants  
- **Stunt Cards (30)**  
- **Market Cards (25)**  
- **Character Components per Player**:  
  - 1 Character board  
  - 1 Character standee  
  - 18 Hit tokens  
  - 4 Action discs  
  - 13 Character cards (including Shell Shield)  
  - 3 Secret Goal cards  
  - 3 Starting Challenge cards  
  - 2 Novice dice  
- **Additional Components**:  
  - Judge standee  
  - Boat standee  
  - First Player marker  
  - Current Round marker  
  - Rival board (Solo/Two-player)  
  - Plot Effect cards (Solo)  
  - Rival Action disc (Solo)

#### 3. Setup  
- Place **Citadel of Time** in the center; arrange other Island boards around it.  
- Set up **Monster Deck**: Shuffle 3 Hard and 3 Easy Monsters (marked with compass) and place face-down at The Fold. Reveal the top Monster at The Fold’s Edge. Place Invasion die nearby.  
- **Challenge Deck**: Shuffle standard Challenge cards and place face-down at Citadel of Time. Reveal 5 to form the Challenge pool.  
- **Stunt Deck**: Shuffle and place at Chronosseum.  
- **Market Deck**: Shuffle Market cards and place at Droska Ring. Reveal 3 to form Market pool.  
- Place **Danger Dice** on Round tracker. Set **Round marker** to Round 1.  
- Place **Judge** on a randomly chosen Arena (Lamara, Droska, or Chronosseum).  
- Place **Boat** on Start space at Lamara Stadium.  
- Each player receives:  
  - Character board, standee, 4 Action discs (2 under standee, 2 on Round tracker)  
  - 2 Novice dice, 2 Fruit, 2 Shells (plus 2 Shells on Shell Shield)  
  - 3 Secret Goal cards (choose one, keep face-down)  
  - 3 Starting Challenge cards  
  - Character deck (13 cards; place Shell Shield and Starting Character card in play)  
  - Set Trait dials to starting position (green circle)  
- Randomly assign **First Player**; give bonus Fruit/Shells to others.  

#### 4. Gameplay Flow  
Each round consists of a **Day Phase** (turns) and **Night Phase** (cleanup).  
- **Day Phase**: Players take turns in clockwise order, starting with First Player.  
  1. Move Character and one Action disc to an Island.  
  2. Take Action space reward (if available).  
  3. Take Island Bonus.  
  4. Attempt a Challenge (if at matching Arena) or fight a Monster (if at The Fold).  
  5. Play one Stunt card during turn.  
- **Night Phase**: After all players exhaust Action discs:  
  1. Roll Invasion die (1–5: Inner Reef Monster invades; 6–8: Fold’s Edge Monster invades).  
  2. Advance all Monsters one space; reveal new Monster.  
  3. If Monster flees from Inner Reef, players without a Hit token move –1 on Champion board.  
  4. Refresh and Upgrade dice equal to Resilience Trait level.  
  5. Return Characters and Action discs.  
  6. Place Hit token on Champion board if leading.  
  7. Move Judge clockwise to next Arena.  
  8. Advance Round marker; at start of Round 2 and 4, gain one Action disc and upgrade Danger die.  

#### 5. Core Mechanics  
- **Action Spaces**:  
  - **Single-use**: Solid line; only one player per round.  
  - **Open/Multi-use**: Dashed circle; unlimited use.  
  - **Island Bonuses**:  
    - **Chronosseum**: Draw 1 Stunt card.  
    - **Lamara Stadium**: Move Boat one space; gain reward.  
    - **Droska Ring**: Buy Market card or gain 1 Fruit.  
    - **Citadel of Time**: Choose or draw 2 Challenge cards.  
    - **The Fold**: Fight Monster.  
- **Challenges**:  
  - Play matching Challenge card at correct Arena.  
  - Roll dice (up to Focus level; eat Fruit for extra dice).  
  - Roll Danger die each time. Block × symbols with Shells (1 Shell per ×, 2 for ××).  
  - Match all required symbols to complete; Push It symbols are optional but grant extra Trait advancement.  
  - On success: Advance Traits, tuck card under board (for Fighting Style), gain Champion board bonuses.  
  - On failure: Advance matched Traits, discard card, draw replacement.  
- **Fighting Monsters**:  
  - Gain Bonus die based on Fighting Style level (1+ Challenges completed in that Arena).  
  - Roll dice + Danger die; block × symbols.  
  - Assign hits to uncovered Monster symbols. Armored spots require Level 2+ (solid color) or Level 3+ (black edge) dice.  
  - Re-roll until satisfied or out of dice.  
  - Deal damage with Hit tokens; advance matched Traits.  
  - **Lose all dice used** in battle.  
  - Kill Monster when all spots are covered; gain reward and Kill Bonus.  
- **Shell Shield**:  
  - Holds max 6 Shells.  
  - Block × symbols during rolls.  
  - Spend 4 Shells: Set one die to any face.  
  - Spend 6 Shells: Refresh 2 dice (no Upgrade).  
- **Character Cards**:  
  - Play when Synergy dial advances to card icon.  
  - Draw 2, choose 1, place other on bottom of deck.  
  - Remain in play; abilities active on your turn.  
  - Some can be discarded for one-time effects.  
- **Advanced Play (Optional)**:  
  - **Legendary Challenges**: 3–4 sets mixed into Challenge pool. Types: Hazardous, Mystic, Show-off, Heroic, Split, Circuit, Swarm, Judge.  
  - **Advanced Market Cards**: 1–2 sets shuffled into Market deck. Types: Strange Wares, Precious Wisdom, Black Market.  
  - **Advanced Monsters**: Mixed with base Monsters; significantly harder.  

#### 6. Scoring & End Game  
Game ends after Round 4. Score:  
1. **Completed Challenges**: Points as marked on card.  
2. **Trait Dials**: Highest point value reached on each dial.  
3. **Champion Board Rank**: 3 (1st), 2 (2nd), 1 (3rd). Ties split points (round down).  
4. **Champion Board Advancement**: Highest value passed (1, 2, 4, 6, 8, 10). Only one player claims 10.  
5. **Leader Bonus**: 1 point per Hit token on Champion board.  
6. **Secret Goals**: Points if completed (7 Easy, 10 Difficult).  
7. **Monster Hits**: 1 point per Hit token beside The Fold.  
- **Winning**: Highest score wins. Tiebreakers: Champion board position, then Challenge points, then shared victory.  

#### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Can I visit an Island with full Action spaces?** Yes; gain Island Bonus and may attempt Challenge.  
- **Can I re-roll dice that already match?** Yes, even if Challenge is complete (but completing ends it). For Monsters, re-rolls allowed freely.  
- **Do I lose all dice when fighting Monsters?** Yes; returned to general supply.  
- **Can I save Bonus die?** No; must use it in the fight.  
- **How does Push It affect scoring?** Push It symbols count toward Goal and Set completion, even if not fulfilled.  
- **Can I use Shell Shield during Monster fight?** Yes; same as Challenges.  
- **What if I can’t pay Invasion penalty?** Pay what you can; no additional penalty.  
- **Can I use Character card abilities during opponent’s turn?** No; only on your turn.  
- **Can I use Stunt cards before moving?** No; only after moving.  
- **Can I stay on the same Island?** Yes; must place new Action disc on available space or outskirts.
