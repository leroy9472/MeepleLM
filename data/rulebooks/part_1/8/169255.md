## Comprehensive Reference Manual  

### 1. Lore & Objective  
The game is set in the world of *A Song of Ice and Fire*, where rival noble houses vie for control of the Iron Throne. Players represent one of the great Houses of Westeros, using characters, locations, and attachments to gain power and outmaneuver opponents.  

**Objective**: Be the first player to gain **15 power** (on your House card or characters you control) to win the game.  

---

### 2. Components  
- **Faction Card**: Represents your House (e.g., Stark, Lannister).  
- **Agenda Card (Optional)**: Adds special rules or abilities.  
- **Plot Deck (7 cards)**: Determines income, claim, initiative, reserve, and special abilities each round.  
- **Draw Deck (60+ cards)**: Contains characters, locations, attachments, and events.  
- **Gold Tokens**: Used as currency to play cards.  
- **Power Counters**: Track progress toward victory.  
- **Influence Tokens**: Track game states (if needed).  
- **First Player Token**: Indicates turn order.  
- **Title Cards (Melee only)**: Used in 3–6 player games for special abilities and rivalries.  

---

### 3. Setup  
1. **Choose Decks**: Each player selects a faction and optional agenda.  
2. **Random First Player**: Determine starting player randomly.  
3. **Place Factions**: Reveal faction and agenda cards.  
4. **Create Treasury**: Place gold, power, and influence tokens in a shared pool.  
5. **Plot Deck**: Place facedown next to faction card.  
6. **Shuffle Decks**: Shuffle draw and plot decks.  
7. **Draw Setup Hand**: Draw 7 cards. May take **one mulligan** (shuffle and redraw 7).  
8. **Place Setup Cards**:  
   - In player order, each player may place up to **8 total gold cost** worth of character, location, or attachment cards facedown.  
   - No more than **one Limited** card.  
   - Attachments must have a legal target.  
   - Duplicates of unique cards may be placed at no cost.  
9. **Reveal Setup Cards**: All players reveal simultaneously. Attachments are attached; duplicates placed under matching unique cards.  
10. **Replenish Hand**: Draw cards until you have 7 in hand.  

---

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
The game proceeds in rounds. Each round has seven phases:  

#### 1. Plot Phase  
- Each player chooses **1 plot card** from their plot deck and places it facedown.  
- All plots are revealed simultaneously.  
- **Initiative**: Player with highest initiative value chooses **first player**.  
  - Tiebreaker: Lowest total power wins; if still tied, random choice.  
- Resolve **"When Revealed"** abilities (order chosen by first player).  
- **(Melee only)**: Players select **title cards** from a shared pool (see Title Rules).  

#### 2. Draw Phase  
- Each player draws **2 cards**.  

#### 3. Marshaling Phase  
- In player order (starting with first player), each becomes the **active player** in turn.  
- **Collect Income**: Add gold from your plot card + any modifiers to your gold pool.  
- **Marshal Cards**: Play character, location, or attachment cards by paying gold cost.  
  - Only active player may marshal.  
  - May play **one Limited card per round**.  
  - May play a second copy of a unique card as a **duplicate** (no cost).  
- Other players may use **actions** during special action windows.  

#### 4. Challenges Phase  
- In player order, each player becomes active player.  
- Active player may initiate **one military**, **one intrigue**, and **one power challenge** (in any order).  
- **Challenge Resolution**:  
  1. Declare challenge type and opponent.  
  2. **Declare attackers**: Standby characters with matching challenge icon. Kneel them.  
  3. **Declare defenders**: Opponent kneels standing characters with matching icon.  
  4. **Compare STR**: Total STR of attackers vs. defenders.  
     - **Tie**: Attacker wins.  
  5. **Unopposed Bonus**: If defender has **0 STR**, attacker gains **1 power**.  
  6. **Claim Effect** (if attacker wins):  
     - **Military**: Defender kills **claim value** number of characters (any, not necessarily in challenge).  
     - **Intrigue**: Defender discards **claim value** cards at random from hand.  
     - **Power**: Defender moves **claim value** power from their House card to attacker’s.  
  7. **Resolve Keywords**: Insight, Pillage, Intimidate, Renown, etc.  
  8. Challenge ends.  

#### 5. Dominance Phase  
- Each player counts:  
  - Total **STR of standing characters** +  
  - **1 per unspent gold** in pool.  
- Player with highest total gains **1 power**.  
- **Tie**: No power awarded.  

#### 6. Standing Phase  
- All **kneeling cards** are stood (returned to upright position).  

#### 7. Taxation Phase  
- **Return unspent gold** to treasury.  
- **Discard down to reserve**: In player order, each player discards down to hand size equal to **reserve value** on revealed plot card.  
- **(Melee only)**: Return all **title cards** to the pool.

### 5. Core Mechanics  

#### Unique Cards & Duplicates  
- Only **one copy** of a unique card (⊕) may be in play at a time.  
- May play a second copy as a **duplicate** (no cost). Place it under the original.  
- **Duplicate Ability**: "Interrupt: When the overlapping unique card would leave play, discard this duplicate to save it. (Cannot be canceled.)"  
- Cannot play a unique card if one is in your **dead pile**.  

#### Attachments  
- May only attach to eligible cards (character, location, etc.).  
- Remain under **owner’s control** even if attached card changes control.  
- When attached card **leaves play**, return attachment to hand—**unless** it has **Terminal**, then discard.  

#### Keywords  
- **Ambush (X)**: Pay X gold during challenges phase to play card.  
- **Insight**: After winning a challenge, draw 1 card.  
- **Intimidate**: After winning as attacker, kneel a losing opponent’s character with STR ≤ your margin of victory.  
- **Limited**: Max 1 per round (including setup).  
- **No attachments**: Cannot have attachments.  
- **Pillage**: After winning, discard top card of loser’s deck.  
- **Renown**: After winning, gain 1 power on this character.  
- **Stealth**: When declared as attacker, choose one defender without stealth; they cannot defend.  
- **Terminal**: If attached card leaves play, discard this (do not return to hand).  

#### Discard & Dead Piles  
- **Dead Pile**: Killed characters go here. Cannot reuse unique cards from dead pile.  
- **Discard Pile**: Discarded or sacrificed cards.  
- Both piles are public.  

#### Titles (Melee Only)  
- Selected each round from a shared pool.  
- **Supports**: Cannot initiate challenges against the player whose title you support.  
- **Rivals**: Gain **1 extra power** when winning a challenge (as attacker or defender) against a rival. Max 1 per rival per round.  
- **Crown Regent**: +2 STR in dominance. Can **redirect one challenge** per round to a new target.  
- **Hand of the King**: +1 STR in challenges with participant. May initiate **one extra challenge** (must be against new opponent).  
- **Master of Laws**: Draw +1 card. +1 reserve.  
- **Master of Ships**: +1 STR in challenges with participant. +1 claim when attacking rival.  
- **Master of Whispers**: When winning an intrigue challenge as attacker, may apply claim effect to **multiple opponents**.  
- **Master of Coin**: +2 gold income.  

---

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
- **Victory**: First player to have **15 or more power** on cards they control **immediately wins**.  
- **Power Placement**:  
  - From **Power Challenge**: Placed on attacker’s **House card**.  
  - From **Unopposed Bonus**: Placed on attacker’s **House card**.  
  - From **Renown/Effects**: Placed on **character card**.  
- **Instant Loss**: If a player’s **draw deck is empty**, they **lose immediately**.  

---

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Tied Challenges**: Attacker wins.  
- **Unopposed Challenge**: Defender has **0 STR** (no defenders or STR-reduced to 0). Attacker gains 1 power.  
- **Claim on Non-Participating Characters**: Military claim may target any character, even if not in challenge.  
- **Attachments on Killed Characters**: Return to owner’s hand (unless Terminal).  
- **Duplicates Saving from Kill/Discard**: Can save from "kill" or "discard" unless effect says "cannot be saved."  
- **Stealth**: Prevents one defender without stealth from participating.  
- **Multiple Titles**: In 3–6 player games, all titles are returned to pool at end of round.  
- **First Player Eliminated**: Player to the left becomes first player.  
- **Constant Abilities**: Active as long as card is in play. Multiple copies stack.
