### 1. Lore & Objective

For over a century, the city-state of Thandar has thrived as a beacon of peace through trade. Born from the once-warlike Empire‚Äôs shift toward diplomacy, Thandar now draws people from every nation and species. Yet beneath its prosperity lies constant political and physical conflict.

In **Hero Realms**, players take on the roles of powerful heroes building personalized decks of actions, items, and champions to gain influence, defeat rivals, and dominate through strength or cunning.

### 3. Setup

1. **Market Setup**:
   - Shuffle the Market Deck and place it face-down.
   - Reveal the top 5 cards to form the **Market** in a row.
   - Place the 16 Fire Gem cards face-up in a stack next to the Market.
   - Reserve space for the **Sacrifice Pile**.

2. **Player Setup**:
   - Each player creates a personal deck: 7 Gold, 1 Shortsword, 1 Dagger, 1 Ruby.
   - Shuffle and place the deck face-down. Place a discard pile next to it.
   - Determine first player randomly.

3. **Starting Hands**:
   - **2-player game**: First player draws 3 cards, second draws 5.
   - **3+ players (Free-for-All)**: First player draws 3, second draws 4, all others draw 5.
   - **Solo variants may differ** (e.g., always 5-card starting hand).

4. **Health**: All players start at 50 Health (tracked with paper or included cards).

### 4. Gameplay Flow

Each turn has three phases:

#### 1. Main Phase
At any time during this phase, you may:
- Play cards from your hand.
- Use **Expend**, **Ally**, or **Sacrifice** abilities.
- Spend Gold to **Acquire** cards from the Market.
- Use Combat to **Attack** opponents or their Champions.

#### 2. Discard Phase
- Lose all unspent Gold and Combat.
- Discard all in-play **Actions** and **Items**.
- Discard all cards remaining in hand.
- **Prepare** all your Champions (return them to vertical orientation).

#### 3. Draw Phase
- Draw 5 cards from your personal deck.
- If your deck is empty, shuffle your discard pile to form a new deck and continue drawing.

> If your deck is exhausted mid-turn, reshuffle your discard pile immediately to continue.

### 5. Core Mechanics

#### Card Types
- **Actions & Items**:
  - Played for immediate effect (primary ability).
  - Remain in play until Discard Phase.
  - May have **Expend**, **Ally**, or **Sacrifice** abilities.

- **Champions**:
  - Remain in play until stunned or sacrificed.
  - Start in **prepared** (vertical) state.
  - Can use **Expend** ability by turning sideways.
  - Have **Defense** value: damage equal to or exceeding this in one turn stuns them.
  - **Damage does not carry over** between turns.

#### Expend Ability (‚óè)
- Turn card sideways to use.
- Can be used any time during your Main Phase, including the turn played.
- Cards with "prepare" effects can allow reuse in same turn.

#### Ally Ability (faction icon)
- Trigger when you have **at least one other card of the same faction** in play.
- Can be used **once per turn**, at any time during your Main Phase.
- Order of play does not matter.

#### Sacrifice Ability (üî•)
- Use by placing card in the **Sacrifice Pile**.
- Only triggers if you activate the ability ‚Äî not if removed another way.
- **Fire Gem exception**: If a Fire Gem would go to Sacrifice Pile, return it to its stack instead.

#### Gold & Combat Pools
- **Gold Pool**: Accumulates from card effects. Used to buy cards from Market.
- **Combat Pool**: Accumulates from card effects. Used to attack.

> Unused Gold and Combat are lost at the end of your turn.

#### Acquiring Cards
- Pay Gold equal to card‚Äôs cost (top-right corner).
- Place acquired card into your **discard pile**.
- Replace Market slot with top card of Market Deck.
- Acquiring is **not playing** ‚Äî abilities do not trigger.

#### Attacking
- **Combat Pool** is used to attack.
- **Attack a Champion**: Assign any amount of Combat. If total damage ‚â• Defense, **stun** it (send to owner‚Äôs discard pile).
- **Attack a Player**: Reduce their Health by Combat spent.
- **Guards (black shield)**:
  - While a player has a prepared Guard, they and their non-Guard Champions **cannot be targeted**.
  - Must stun all Guards before attacking player or non-Guard Champions.

> Champions with Expend abilities that grant Combat do **not attack directly** ‚Äî Combat goes to your pool.

#### Stun
- A Champion is stunned when:
  - Damage taken in one turn ‚â• its Defense.
  - An effect explicitly says ‚ÄúStun target champion.‚Äù
- Stunned Champion goes to owner‚Äôs discard pile.
- Champions **prepare at end of their owner‚Äôs turn**.

### 6. Scoring & End Game

- **Health** is the score. Starts at 50.
- A player is **eliminated** when Health ‚â§ 0.
- **Victory**:
  - **Free-for-All**: Last player standing wins.
  - **Hunter - First Blood**: First player eliminated ‚Üí player to their right wins.
  - **Hunter - Last One Standing**: Continue after eliminations; teammate to eliminated gains 10 Health and draws 1 card. Last player standing wins.
  - **Hydra (2v2)**: Teams share Health (start at 75). Last team with surviving player(s) wins.
  - **Emperor (3v3)**: Emperors have 60 Health, Commanders 50. Defeating Emperor ends game.
  - **Solo Modes**: Defeat the Boss/Demon. Lose if your Health ‚â§ 0 or Boss reaches 100 Health.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases

- **Can I use an Ally ability before playing the second card of that faction?**  
  Yes. As soon as two cards of the same faction are in play, Ally abilities can be used.

- **Can I attack multiple times in one turn?**  
  Yes. You may make any number of attacks using your Combat Pool.

- **Can I attack a non-Guard Champion if opponent has a Guard?**  
  No. Guards protect all non-Guard Champions and the player until stunned.

- **What happens to unused Gold or Combat?**  
  Lost at the end of your turn (Discard Phase).

- **Can I use a Sacrifice ability and then play the card?**  
  No. Sacrificing sends the card to the Sacrifice Pile immediately.

- **Do Champions stay in play between turns?**  
  Yes, unless stunned or sacrificed.

- **Can I use a Champion‚Äôs Expend ability the turn it enters play?**  
  Yes.

- **Can I acquire a card and use its ability the same turn?**  
  No. Acquired cards go to discard pile; they are not played.

- **In team games, do Ally abilities work across teammates?**  
  No. Only cards in your own play area count.

- **What if the Market Deck runs out?**  
  Shuffle the discard pile of the Market Deck to form a new deck.
