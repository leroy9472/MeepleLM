# Earth: Comprehensive Reference Manual

## 1. Lore & Objective
Players build self-sustaining island ecosystems by planting Flora and Terrain cards in a 4x4 grid. The goal is to score the most Victory Points (VP) by completing synergistic combinations of cards, fulfilling objectives, and efficiently managing resources. The game ends when a player completes their 16-card tableau, triggering final scoring.

## 2. Components
- Fauna Board (2 sides: Beginner and Standard/Advanced)
- Player Boards (front for multiplayer, back for solo)
- 283 Earth Cards: Flora (179), Terrain (66), Event (38)
- 10 Island Cards (double-sided)
- 10 Climate Cards (double-sided)
- 32 Ecosystem Cards (double-sided)
- 23 Fauna Cards (double-sided)
- 150 Sprouts (Green Cubes)
- 105 Soil Tokens (1, 5, 10 denominations)
- 166 Growth Tokens: 90 Trunks (wooden cylinders), 76 Canopies (cones, multi-colored)
- 12 Leaf Tokens (5 colors: Red, Yellow, Green, Blue, Purple)
- First Player Token (Polar Bear)
- Active Player Token (Fox)
- Score Pad

## 3. Setup
### Shared Setup
1. Place Fauna Board in center. Use Beginner side (no Ecosystem slots, flat 10 VP per Fauna objective) or Standard/Advanced side (Ecosystem slots, tiered VP).
2. Give each player a Player Board (front side).
3. Create central supply:
   - 150 Sprouts
   - 105 Soil Tokens
   - 166 Growth Tokens (90 Trunks, 76 Canopies)
4. Shuffle 283 Earth Cards (remove 6 Solo Mode cards) into face-down draw pile with discard space.
5. Shuffle 23 Fauna Cards, place one on each of 4 Fauna Board spaces (random face up). Return remainder to box.
6. For Ecosystem Cards:
   - Beginner: Return to box.
   - Standard/Advanced: Shuffle 32 Ecosystem Cards, place one on each of 2 Ecosystem spaces on Fauna Board (random face up).
     - Standard: Deal 1 Ecosystem Card to each player.
     - Advanced: Deal 2 Ecosystem Cards to each player.
     - Return remainder to box.

### Individual Setup
7. Shuffle 10 Island Cards:
   - Beginner/Standard: Deal 1 to each player.
   - Advanced: Deal 2 to each player.
8. Shuffle 10 Climate Cards:
   - Beginner/Standard: Deal 1 to each player.
   - Advanced: Deal 2 to each player.
9. Randomly choose Starting Player. Give them First Player Token (Polar Bear) and Active Player Token (Fox).
10. All players simultaneously:
    - Choose face of Island, Climate, and Ecosystem Cards (if applicable).
    - Place chosen cards on Player Board.
    - Beginner: Choose Island and Climate only.
    - Standard: Choose Island, Climate, and 1 Ecosystem.
    - Advanced: Choose 1 of 2 for each type (discard other).
11. Each player takes 5 Leaf Tokens in chosen color:
    - 4 placed on Player Board (for claiming Fauna objectives).
    - 1 kept aside (to mark chosen action).
12. Each player gains starting resources per chosen Island Card:
    - Draws [X] Earth Cards to hand.
    - Composts [Y] cards from hand to personal discard pile.
    - Takes [Z] Soil, places next to board.

## 4. Gameplay Flow
1. Starting with First Player and proceeding clockwise, players take turns.
2. On a turn, the Active Player:
   - Chooses 1 of 4 Actions (Plant, Compost, Water, Grow).
   - Resolves the "top" effect of that action.
   - All other players resolve the "bottom" effect.
   - All players simultaneously activate abilities matching the chosen action's color.
3. After turn, pass Active Player Token clockwise.
4. Game ends at the end of the round when any player plants their 16th card (completing 4x4 tableau). That player claims 7 VP bonus.
5. All players complete the round (equal turns).
6. Proceed to Final Scoring.

## 5. Core Mechanics
### Actions
- **Plant (Green)**: 
  - Active: Plant up to 2 cards, then draw 4, keep 1, discard 3.
  - Others: Plant 1 card and draw 1.
- **Compost (Red)**:
  - Active: Gain 5 Soil, Compost 2 cards from draw pile.
  - Others: Gain 2 Soil or Compost 2 cards from draw pile.
- **Water (Blue)**:
  - Active: Place 6 Sprouts, gain 2 Soil.
  - Others: Place 2 Sprouts or gain 2 Soil.
- **Grow (Yellow)**:
  - Active: Draw 4 cards, place 2 Growth.
  - Others: Draw 2 cards or place 2 Growth.

### Card Types
- **Flora**: Plantable. Types: Tree, Herb, Mushroom, Bush. Have Soil cost, VP, Habitat, Growth/Canopy, Sprout spaces, abilities.
- **Terrain**: Plantable. Provide passive effects or end-game scoring.
- **Event**: Play anytime (even on others' turns). Place on Player Board. One-time effect. Not part of 16-card limit.
- **Island/Climate**: On Player Board. Provide starting resources/abilities. Count toward objectives.
- **Ecosystem**: On Player Board or Fauna Board. Provide end-game scoring objectives. Not activated.
- **Fauna**: On Fauna Board. Public objectives with diminishing VP (15/11/8/6/5) or flat 10 VP (Beginner).

### Activating Abilities
- After action resolution, all players activate abilities matching the chosen action's color.
- Resolve in order: left to right, top to bottom in tableau.
- Player Board abilities (Island, Climate) may be activated before or after tableau, but not interrupted.
- Costs must be paid fully; benefits may be taken partially.
- Green abilities activate same turn planted.
- Multicolored cards activate on any matching color phase and count as each color for objectives.
- Black abilities: Trigger once when card is played.
- Brown abilities: Ongoing or end-game scoring; not activated by actions.

### Planting Rules
- Only Flora and Terrain can be planted.
- Pay Soil from personal supply to central supply.
- First card: Anywhere in 4x4 grid.
- Subsequent cards: Orthogonally or diagonally adjacent to existing card.
- Tableau must fit in 4x4 grid.
- If draw pile empty, shuffle discard pile (not compost).

### Composting Rules
- No limit to Soil or compost pile size.
- May compost from discard pile by agreement.
- Cards composted from hand at setup count toward 1 VP each.

### Watering Rules
- Sprouts placed immediately on Sprout spaces only.
- Excess sprouts (no space) return to supply.
- Sprouts worth 1 VP each at end game.
- Convert 3 Sprouts â†’ 2 Soil at any time except during Watering action or ability resolution.

### Growing Rules
- Growth (Trunks/Canopies) placed immediately on Growth spaces up to maximum.
- Final Growth piece is a Canopy (color for flavor only).
- Excess Growth returns to supply.
- Trunks without Canopy: 1 VP each. With Canopy: score Canopy Completion VP instead.

### Fauna Cards
- Public objectives on Fauna Board.
- When objective met, place Leaf Token on highest available VP tier.
- Multiple players on same turn: tokens placed clockwise starting with Active Player.
- Once claimed, objective cannot be lost.
- VP awarded at end game.
- First player to complete tableau: 7 VP bonus.

### Ecosystem Cards
- Public (on Fauna Board) or Private (on Player Board).
- Score VP at end game for each time requirements are met.
- Cards can be used for multiple Ecosystem objectives, but not the same one twice.

## 6. Scoring & End Game
Game ends when any player completes 4x4 tableau. That player claims 7 VP bonus. All players complete the round.

**Final Scoring:**
1. Base VP: Island, Climate, and all Tableau cards.
2. Base VP: Event cards on Player Board (may be negative).
3. 1 VP per card in Compost pile.
4. 1 VP per Sprout in Tableau.
5. 1 VP per Trunk in Tableau (not under Canopy), OR Canopy Completion VP if Canopy present.
6. End-game scoring from Terrain cards with brown bar abilities.
7. VP from Ecosystem Objectives (personal and public).
8. VP from Fauna Objectives (Leaf Tokens on Fauna Board).
9. 7 VP bonus for first player to complete tableau.

**Tiebreakers (in order):**
1. Most Soil
2. Most cards in hand
3. Most Growth on Tableau
4. Most Sprouts
5. Most cards in Compost

## 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Can I choose the same action twice in a row?** Yes.
- **Can I plant diagonally?** Yes, cards can be placed orthogonally or diagonally adjacent.
- **Do Island/Climate cards count for objectives?** Yes, for Fauna and Ecosystem objectives.
- **Can multicolored cards count as multiple colors?** Yes, for objectives and scoring.
- **When is a Fauna objective claimed?** At end of turn after action and ability resolution. Active player gets priority if tied.
- **Can I convert Sprouts during an ability?** No, only between abilities or actions.
- **Can I play an Event card during an ability?** Yes, anytime, even between ability resolutions.
- **Can I use a card for multiple Ecosystems?** Yes, but not the same Ecosystem twice.
- **Do Terrain cards count as Flora?** No, unless specified.
- **Can I compost from discard pile?** Yes, if players agree.
