# Distilled - Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective
You have inherited an abandoned distillery and must restore it to prominence over seven rounds. By acquiring ingredients, recipes, and upgrades, you distill and sell spirits to earn Spirit Points (SP). The player with the most SP at the end of the game becomes Master Distiller.

### 2. Components
- 5 Distillery Boards  
- 18 Distiller Identity Cards  
- 18 Signature Recipe Label Tokens  
- 18 Signature Ingredient Cards  
- 20 Distillery Goal Cards  
- 88 Basic Market Ingredient/Item Cards  
- 36 Premium Market Ingredient Cards  
- 33 Premium Market Item Cards  
- 30 Distillery Upgrade Cards  
- 10 Starting Item Cards (Metal Barrel, Glass Bottle)  
- 60 Alcohol Cards  
- 40 Flavor Cards  
- 23 Spirit Award Tokens  
- 5 Player Reference Cards  
- 5 Dual-Layered Recipe Clipboards & 20 Double-Sided Recipe Tasting Flights  
- 59 Money Tokens (1, 5, 10 value)  
- 1 Truck Board & 1 Basic Market Board  
- 1 First Player Token & 1 Round Marker Barrel  
- 1 Shelf Board  
- 5 Double-Sided Score Modifier Tokens  
- 85 Spirit Label Tokens  
- 37 Wooden Bronze, Silver, and Gold Cubes  
- 5 Player Scoring Tokens  

### 3. Setup
1. **Shelf Board**: Place on table. Set round marker to '1' on Round Track. Each player places scoring token beside it.
2. **Basic Market**: Place Basic Market Board. Lay out 7 faceup piles of Basic Ingredient and Item Cards.
3. **Premium Market**: Shuffle decks of Distillery Upgrade, Premium Ingredient, and Premium Item Cards separately. Place in column (Upgrade on top). Reveal top 4 cards per deck in rows beside.
4. **Truck Board**: Place to right of Premium Market.
5. **Flavor & Alcohol Decks**: Shuffle Flavor Cards face down. Place Alcohol Cards face up nearby.
6. **Recipe Cubes**: Place bronze, silver, gold cubes within reach.
7. **Tasting Flight**: Choose one. Give matching Tasting Flight card to each player.
8. **Spirit Labels**: Place Moonshine (2 per player), Vodka (2 per player), and one of each spirit on Tasting Flight in faceup stacks on Shelf Board.
9. **Spirit Awards**: Randomly reveal (number of players + 1). Discard and redraw if tied to unrepresented region.
10. **Player Setup**:
    - Each player takes: 1 Distillery Board, 1 Scoring Token, 1 Score Modifier Token, 1 Recipe Clipboard, 1 Tasting Flight card, 1 Metal Barrel, 1 Glass Bottle.
    - Draw 3 Distillery Goals; discard and redraw any involving unrepresented region.
    - Randomly deal 2 Distiller Identities (from those paired with Tasting Flight). Choose one, discard other.
    - Take starting resources (money, ingredients, Signature Recipe Label, Signature Ingredient) as specified on chosen identity.
    - Place Signature Recipe Label on clipboard (recipe-side up), Signature Ingredient beside board.
    - Place Distiller Identity in Office space.
11. **First Player**: Randomly choose; give First Player Token.

### 4. Gameplay Flow
The game lasts 7 rounds. Each round has 4 phases:

#### Start of Round
- Resolve any "Start of Round" effects on Distiller Identity or Distillery Upgrade Cards. If multiple, resolve in turn order.

#### 1. Market Phase
- Clockwise from First Player, each player may:
  - **PURCHASE 1 CARD**: Buy any 1 card from Basic or Premium Market. Pay cost (apply discounts).
    - Premium Market: After purchase, shift cards right; draw to refill leftmost space.
    - Basic Market: Max 2 purchases per player per round. Keep purchased cards faceup beside board.
    - Ingredient Cards → Pantry.
    - Item Cards → Storeroom.
    - Distillery Upgrade Cards → Upgrade space (discard existing to make room).
  - **PURCHASE 1 RECIPE**:
    - Check tier (bronze, silver, gold) on Tasting Flight.
    - Pay cost, take matching cube, place beside recipe on clipboard.
    - Now know how to distill that spirit.
  - **PASS**: Once passed, no further actions this phase.
- Continue until all pass.
- **Market Cleanup**:
  - Discard rightmost faceup card from each Premium Market row (2 rightmost in 2-player).
  - Shift cards right; refill with top card from deck.
  - Move all purchased Basic Cards to Pantry.

#### 2. Distill Phase
- **Trade Step**: Each player may discard 1 Ingredient or Item Card to gain 1 Basic Ingredient of equal or lesser market value from Basic Market. Cannot trade Alcohol, Yeast, or Starting Items.
- **Fill Washback**:
  - Move ingredients from Pantry to Washback slots (Water, Sugar, Yeast).
  - Alcohol Cards may go in Water or Yeast slot.
  - Must have at least 1 card in each of Water, Sugar, Yeast slots.
- **Add Alcohol**: Add 1 Alcohol Card per Sugar card in central slot.
- **Shuffle**: Shuffle all Washback cards into facedown Spirit Stack.
- **Remove Heads & Tails**: Reveal and return top and bottom cards to Pantry.
- **Reveal Stack**: Remaining cards = Spirit Stack.
- **Determine Spirit**:
  - Stack must match at least one known recipe (including Vodka or Moonshine).
  - If multiple matches, choose one.
  - Requirements:
    - Correct Sugar types/quantities.
    - No disallowed Sugar types (marked with ⊗).
    - Correct Barrel type (Metal, Wood, Clay).
    - Signature Recipes: Require Signature Ingredient or matching Premium Ingredient + other requirements.
    - Signature Recipes can only be made once.
- **Claim Label**:
  - In turn order, claim matching Spirit Label (from Shelf Board or clipboard).
  - Place on Spirit Stack.
  - If no labels left, still distill spirit but no label bonus later.

#### 3. Sell Phase
- **Selling Rules**:
  - Non-aged spirits (●): Must sell this round.
  - Aged spirits (●): Must age at least one round before selling.
- **Selling Steps** (one spirit per turn, clockwise):
  1. Lay out entire Spirit Stack (include Barrel, Flavor Cards).
  2. Add 1 Bottle from Storeroom.
  3. **Gain Money**: Total sell value (cards + recipe for Moonshine/Vodka/signatures).
  4. **Score SP**: Total SP on cards + recipe + Aged Spirit Flavor Bonus (if aged spirit).
  5. **Place Label**: Choose empty Spirit Label Bonus space; place label, gain bonus.
  6. **Return Items**:
     - Starting Metal Barrel/Glass Bottle → Storeroom.
     - Premium Bottles → Display beside board (Bottle Collection).
  7. **Discard/Return**:
     - Basic Market Cards → Basic Market.
     - Alcohol Cards → Alcohol Deck.
     - Premium Market Ingredients/Barrels → Truck discard piles.
     - Flavor Cards → Flavor discard pile.
     - Signature Ingredient → Box (not reclaimable).
- **Selling Aged Spirits**: Follow same steps. Can sell multiple spirits if warehouse has space.
- **Warehouse Full**: If must age new spirit and warehouse full, sell one aged spirit first.

#### 4. Age Phase
- **Aging New Spirit**:
  1. Place Spirit Stack (no Barrel) facedown in Warehouse.
  2. Place Barrel faceup on stack.
  3. Place Spirit Label on Barrel.
  4. Draw top Flavor Card, add facedown to bottom of stack.
- **Aging Existing Spirits**: For each aged spirit in warehouse, draw top Flavor Card, add facedown to bottom of stack.
- **Visibility**: Players may look at Spirit Stack contents (except facedown Flavor Cards).
- **Final Round**: If distill aged spirit in Round 7, must age it. Cannot sell, but scores SP at game end.

#### End of Round
- **Spirit Awards**: If player meets goal, score SP shown. If multiple, divide SP evenly (round up). Flip award facedown (cannot score again).
- **Tastings**: If player did not sell a spirit, may spend up to 4 SP to gain equal money.
- **Third Round**: After Round 3, each player discards 1 Distillery Goal.
- **Advance**: Move round marker forward; pass First Player Token clockwise.
- **Game End**: After Round 7, proceed to End Game Scoring.

### 5. Core Mechanics
- **Spirit Stack**: Created during Distill Phase. Determines spirit type after removing top/bottom cards.
- **Trading**: One-time trade at start of Distill Phase for Basic Ingredient.
- **Label Bonuses**: 7 unique one-time bonuses. Cannot reuse space.
- **Aged Spirit Flavor Bonus**: Based on number of Flavor Cards when sold:
  - 1 → 1 SP
  - 2 → 3 SP
  - 3 → 6 SP
  - 4 → 10 SP
  - 5 → 15 SP
  - 6+ → 15 SP (max)
- **Distillery Upgrades**: Equipment or Specialists. Provide ongoing abilities. Max 3; discard to truck to replace.
- **Signature Recipes**: Prestigious, one-time use. Require specific ingredient (Signature or Premium).

### 6. Scoring & End Game
After Round 7:
- **Warehouse Spirits**: For each, score SP on all cards (ingredients, barrel, recipe) + 1 SP per Flavor Card. Do not get money, bottle, label bonus, or Aged Spirit Flavor Bonus.
- **Bottle Collection**: Score for displayed Premium Bottles:
  - Bottles without Region → do not count.
  - At least 1 bottle from 3 different Regions → 5 SP.
  - At least 1 bottle from 4 different Regions → 10 SP.
- **Distillery Upgrades**: Score SP listed on cards.
- **Distillery Goals**: Score SP for achieved goals.
- **Money**: 1 SP per 5 Money (round down).
- **Total SP**: Sum all sources. Highest wins.
- **Tiebreaker**: Most remaining Money. If still tied, share victory.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **No Labels Available**: Can still distill spirit, but cannot claim label bonus.
- **Starting Items**: Can be reused every time they are used in Sell Phase.
- **Water/Yeast Bonuses**: Only trigger when purchased from Basic Market, not via abilities or trade.
- **Flavor Cards**: Added face down. Only revealed when spirit is sold.
- **Deck Runs Out**: Reshuffle respective discard pile to form new deck.
- **Multiple “Once per Phase” Abilities**: Can use in any order during phase.
- **Signature Ingredient Lost**: If not used in distillation (e.g., removed as head/tail), it is lost and cannot be reclaimed.
