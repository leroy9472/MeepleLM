## Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective  
Crystal Palace is a competitive strategy board game set in Victorian-era London, where players represent different nations striving for scientific and industrial dominance. The game spans five rounds (representing three years, with the first two years consisting of two rounds each). Players compete to earn the most Victory Points (VP) through patents, prototypes, character recruitment, income, buzz, research, and national objectives.

**Objective**: Score the most Victory Points by the end of Round 5, Phase 6. Points are earned both immediately and at game end.

---

### 2. Components  
- **Game Board**: Features 8 locations (Patent Office, British Museum, Bank of England, Westminster, Reform Club, London Times, Port of London, Waterloo Station), Black Market track, Income track, Buzz track.
- **Player Mats**: One per player, with a national objective track and spaces for research tiles, loans, and character/prototype cards.
- **Dice**: 4 per player (colored), plus 1 D10 for solo mode.
- **Tokens**:  
  - Money (Pounds £)  
  - Gears  
  - Energy  
  - Newspapers  
  - Buzz and Income trackers  
  - Assistant tokens (5 per player)  
  - Loan tiles (£10)  
  - Victory Point tokens  
  - Buzz markers (for posters)  
  - Round marker  
- **Cards**:  
  - Patent Cards  
  - Character Cards  
- **Tiles**:  
  - Research Tiles (green and violet backings)  
  - Pocket Watch (double-sided token)  
- **Other**:  
  - Treasure chests (for hiding dice)  
  - Tickets (to mark spent dice at Port of London)  

---

### 3. Setup  
**For 1–4 Players**:  
- Set up the central board based on player count (some locations may have fewer spaces).
- Each player receives:  
  - 40£, 1 Gear, 1 Energy token  
  - 4 Dice, 5 Assistant tokens (in player color)  
  - 1 Player Mat (random side)  
  - Trackers: Buzz, Income, Westminster, and Objective Track set to starting positions  
- Place:  
  - 3 Patent Cards face-up at Patent Office (Location 1)  
  - 3 Research Tiles at British Museum (Location 2)  
  - 3 Shares at Bank of England (Location 3)  
  - 3 Character Cards at Reform Club (Location 5)  
  - Refill Black Market spots with Gears  
- Place Round Marker at Round 1.  
- **Solo Mode (Optional)**: Use 2-player setup; Pesky Villain (PV) uses 5 dice (3 fixed: 5/3/1, 2 rolled).

---

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
The game proceeds in **7 Phases** per round for 5 rounds. Game ends in **Phase 6 of Round 5**.

#### Phase 1: Decide on Dice Value and Pay for It (Simultaneous)  
- Players secretly assign values to all their dice using treasure chests.  
- Reveal simultaneously.  
- Pay £1 per pip shown.  
- **Lowest total pip value**: Gain 1 Newspaper (tie: all tied players gain 1).  
- **Start Player**: Highest total pip value. Tiebreak: compare highest single die, then 2nd highest, etc.  
- **Solo Mode**: PV uses 5 dice (3 fixed: 5/3/1; 2 rolled). If ≥3 dice ≥4, lowest of those is placed last.

#### Phase 2: Place Dice (Turn Order)  
- In turn order (start player first), each player places one die at a time on free dice spots.  
- Die value must meet or exceed the spot’s minimum value.  
- If spot has **Assistant Icon**:  
  - Take 1 Assistant Action:  
    - Place new assistant in lowest free Black Market space and pay cost, **or**  
    - Move assistant up one space in Black Market and pay cost.  
  - Optionally, if you meet requirements, advance 1 level on your **Objective Track**.  
- After placing a die:  
  - You may move **one** of your assistants **down** one space in the Black Market to take **1 Gear** (only if assistant is on Tier 3 or higher).  
- Once a player passes, they cannot place more dice this round.  

#### Phase 3: Take Actions (Location Order)  
- Resolve locations 1 to 8 in order.  
- At each location:  
  - Dice are resolved **highest value first**.  
  - Ties: leftmost die (on board) goes first.  
  - If no space for a die, owner gains £1 per leftover die.  

**Location Actions**:  
1. **Patent Office**: Take any face-up Patent Card. Place blue-side up beside your mat.  
2. **British Museum**: Take any Research Tile or draw randomly. Place on your mat (free space or displace another).  
3. **Bank of England**: Take a Share. Gain VP and/or Income as shown.  
4. **Westminster**: Move your token 1 space forward. Gain immediate bonus.  
   - If on final space: gain 1 Energy or 2 Buzz instead.  
   - Bonus at space III: gain bonus again.  
5. **Reform Club**: Recruit a Character. Pay cost (top left).  
   - Gain instant bonus and VP (top right, based on year).  
   - Gain +4 VP per matching built Prototype.  
   - Flip card face down; place on mat.  
6. **London Times**: Advance on Buzz Track per round’s condition (e.g., “Advance 2 if you have 2+ Research Tiles”).  
7. **Port of London**: Choose one action (each only once per round):  
   - 2 Assistant Actions (first is free if used in Black Market)  
   - 2 Gears  
   - 1 new Die (place immediately)  
   - 1 new Die for £2 (available only in rounds 4–5)  
   - Gain VP = Year + Pips of die you **waive** (place ticket on die; die is lost)  
8. **Waterloo Station**: Choose one:  
   - 2 Energy  
   - 1 Energy + use Phase 6 bonus  
   - 1 Energy + 2 Buzz  
   - Use Phase 6 bonus **or** 2 Buzz (rounds 4–5 only)  

#### Phase 4: Pay Salary & Use Character Abilities (Simultaneous)  
- Pay salary based on **Westminster position** (cost shown on track).  
- Use **Phase 4 (Orange Banner)** abilities from Character cards.  
- Abilities activate **after** salary payment.  

#### Phase 5: Convert Patents to Prototypes (Turn Order)  
- In turn order, each player may **build up to 2 Prototypes**.  
- To build:  
  - Pay cost (top left of Patent Card).  
  - Gain VP (top right + 4 per matching Character card).  
  - Activate bottom ability (choose any player; they gain all effects).  
  - Flip card face down, place on mat.  
- Use **Phase 5 (Blue Banner)** abilities.  

#### Phase 6: Income, Permanent Effects, Buzz (Simultaneous)  
- **Income**: Gain/lose money and VP based on Income Track position.  
- **Move down 3 spaces** on Income Track.  
  - Pay £1 per space you cannot move down.  
- **Green Banner Effects**: Activate  
  - Research Tiles (green/violet)  
  - Black Market bonuses (per assistant)  
  - Pocket Watch (if owned)  
- **Buzz Track Bonuses**:  
  - Land on/past **Flyer**: gain immediate bonus.  
  - Land on/past **Poster**: may place 1 token (max 2 in game, not on same poster). Gain bonus in Phase 6.  
  - Final positions (1st/2nd/3rd) scored at game end.  

#### Phase 7: Preparation for Next Round (Simultaneous)  
- **Black Market**:  
  - Move each assistant **1 space down**.  
  - Refill gears on empty spaces.  
  - If full, **Black Market bursts**: remove all assistants except the one placed last.  
- Retrieve all dice (except those with tickets).  
- Remove and **refill**: Patent Cards, Research Tiles, Shares, Character Cards.  
- Advance Round Marker.  
- **Skip Phase 7 in Round 5**.

### 5. Core Mechanics  

#### Research Tiles  
- **Green Back (British Museum)**: Permanent effects in Phase 6.  
  - Example: “Each round in Phase 6.3: receive 1£ per Character card.”  
- **Violet Back**: One-time or recurring effects.  
  - Example: “Each turn in Phase 6.3: get 1 green Research Tile.”  

#### Character Cards  
- **Recruitment (Phase 3)**: Pay cost, gain instant bonus and VP.  
- **Abilities**:  
  - Phase 4 (Orange): Use after salary  
  - Phase 5 (Blue): Use during Prototype building  
  - Phase 6 (Green): Use in Phase 6  

#### Patent Cards  
- Held face-up; built into Prototypes in Phase 5.  
- Bottom ability affects **any player** (must take all effects).  

#### Black Market  
- Assistants occupy spaces (1–5 tiers).  
- Gains:  
  - **Phase 2**: Place/move assistant and pay cost.  
  - **Phase 6**: Gain bonus per assistant (shown on right).  
- **Gears**: Only gained when **player moves assistant down** (not via card effects).  
- **Market Bursts** when full: all assistants removed except last placed.  
- **End Game Scoring**: Top 3 positions score 3/2/1 VP.  

#### Pocket Watch  
- Obtained via card effect.  
- Choose one side:  
  - **2 VP immediately** (can be re-earned if retaken)  
  - **3£ in Phase 6.3** (as long as you hold it)  
- If another player takes it, you lose it immediately.  

#### Loans  
- **Take**: Anytime you can’t pay, draw random Loan tile, place on free research space (or beside board), gain £10.  
- **Pay Back**: Pay £10 anytime (during turn or game end), flip tile.  
- **Game End Scoring**:  
  - Unpaid loan: -8 to -10 VP  
  - Paid loan: -5 VP  

#### Newspapers (Anytime Use)  
- 4: Gain 1 die (use now if in Phase 1, else next round)  
- 3: +1 Income  
- 2: +1 Gear  
- 1: +1£  

#### Publisher Strips (on Player Mat)  
- Based on Income Track.  
- Reward: Buzz (steps on Buzz Track).  
- If multiple levels met, take **highest** reward.  
- Example: Income 7 → 3 Buzz; Income 10 → 4 Buzz.  

#### Nation Objectives (Player Mat)  
- Each nation has 3 levels (e.g., Spain: 1/2/3 Research tiles with immediate effect).  
- Score VP for **highest level achieved** at game end.  

#### Icon Reference  
- **Immediate Bonus**: Receive once, immediately  
- **Phase 4/5/6.3**: Effect used in that phase  
- **VP Immediate / End Game**: When scored  
- **Assistant Action**: Take action in Black Market  
- **Free Assistant Action**: In Black Market, no cost  
- **Move Assistant Down**: In Black Market, take gear if Tier 3+  
- **Number of Assistants in Black Market**: For scoring  
- **Link (Prototype + Character)**: +4 VP when building or recruiting  
- **Exchange Icons**:  
  - Arrow: discard left to gain right (unlimited)  
  - Double Arrow: one-time exchange  

---

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
Game ends in **Phase 6 of Round 5**. Final scoring:

1. **Instant VP**: Already scored during game (Characters, Patents, Buzz posters, etc.)  
2. **End Game VP**:  
   - **Nation Objective**: Highest level reached  
   - **Loans**: -5 (paid), -8 to -10 (unpaid)  
   - **Empty Research Spaces**: -1 VP per empty space  
   - **Buzz Track**:  
     - Farthest: 6 VP, 2nd: 4 VP, 3rd: 2 VP  
     - Poster bonuses already scored in Phase 6  
   - **Black Market**: Top 3: 3/2/1 VP  
   - **Resources**:  
     - Convert Energy, Gears, Newspapers → £1 each  
     - Convert every £10 → cover one free research space (prevents -1 VP)  

**No tiebreaker**.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Can I use a newspaper to gain a die in Phase 1 after revealing?**  
  No. New die can only be used if owned **before** Phase 1 begins.  
- **Can I place a buzz token on the same poster twice?**  
  No. Max 2 tokens total, never on same poster.  
- **Can I use a Patent card’s ability on myself?**  
  Yes. You choose any player, including yourself.  
- **If I retake the Pocket Watch, do I get 2 VP again?**  
  Yes, if you choose the VP side again.  
- **Can I build a Prototype without the matching Character?**  
  Yes. The link only gives +4 VP.  
- **Do I lose VP for unpaid loans even if I have money?**  
  Yes. Must **actively pay** £10 to flip.  
- **Can I use Phase 6 abilities before income?**  
  Yes. Order: Income → Move down 3 → Green effects → Buzz bonuses.  
- **Can I use multiple newspaper effects at once?**  
  Yes, anytime during your turn.
