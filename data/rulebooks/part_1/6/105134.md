## Comprehensive Reference Manual  

### 1. Lore & Objective  
**Faction: The Mortem Drudge**  
The Mortem Drudge is a unique faction in the game with no traditional capital. They begin play on the Large Factory and grow in strength through control of industrial infrastructure. They thrive on destruction and domination, gaining stars when eliminating other factions and receiving troop bonuses from factories they control. Unlike other factions, they receive no benefits from cities or HQs and are immune to certain penalties.

**Primary Objective**  
- Gain stars by eliminating other factions (1 star per elimination).  
- Complete the **Mission**: *Control the Large Factory and all Small Factories*.  
- Sabotage enemy industrial efforts while securing your own.  

### 2. Components  
- **Large Factory**: Starting territory for The Mortem Drudge.  
- **Small Factory scars (x3)**: Can only be placed one per continent.  
- **Destroyed Factory scar**: Used to sabotage enemy Small Factories.  
- **Event Cards**:  
  - Sabotage  
  - Reinforcement  
  - Mission  
- **Stickers**:  
  - +1 troop for controlling Synthetic Creator  
  - +2 troops on Large Factory  
  - Scar stickers for sabotage and reinforcement  
- **Faction Card for The Mortem Drudge**  

### 3. Setup  
- The Mortem Drudge **starts on the Large Factory**.  
- Only The Mortem Drudge may begin the game with **Small Factory scars**.  
- Place **one Small Factory scar per continent** (max one per continent).  
- The Mortem Drudge receives **2 troops at the end of each turn** while controlling the Large Factory.  
- If The Mortem Drudge controls the **Synthetic Creator** territory, place the corresponding sticker and gain 1 troop at the end of each turn.  

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
1. **Start Turn**:  
   - Recruit troops based on controlled territories, continents, cards, etc.  
   - The Mortem Drudge gains **2 troops** if controlling the Large Factory.  
   - Gain **+1 troop** if controlling Synthetic Creator.  
   - Gain **+1 troop** at end of turn for each Small Factory owned.  

2. **Combat & Movement**:  
   - Attack and conquer territories.  
   - The Mortem Drudge receives no city bonuses and **loses troops when entering cities** for the first time.  
   - When taking over a territory with **6+ troops**, roll a die and **add that number to your own troops** in that territory.  

3. **End of Turn**:  
   - Gain bonus troops from factories and scars.  
   - Draw resource card if you conquered a territory.  
   - Apply sabotage and reinforcement effects if triggered.  

### 5. Core Mechanics  
- **No Capital**: The Mortem Drudge does **not start with a capital**.  
- **No City Bonuses**:  
  - No bonus for city population.  
  - Must **sacrifice troops** when moving into a city for the first time.  
- **No HQ Bonuses**:  
  - No stars or bonuses for controlling HQs.  
  - May **spend 3 missiles to destroy an HQ** they have taken over this turn.  
- **Factory Bonuses**:  
  - **Small Factory**: +1 troop at end of turn (only for The Mortem Drudge).  
  - **Large Factory**: +2 troops per turn (base), upgraded to **+3** if Reinforcement card is active.  
- **Elimination Star**: Gain **1 star** whenever another faction is eliminated.  
- **Mission – Control Factories**:  
  - Must control **Large Factory + all Small Factories**.  
  - If another faction completes this while The Mortem Drudge is in play, they receive **2 stars** instead.  

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
- **Stars are earned by**:  
  - Eliminating other factions (**1 star per elimination**).  
  - Completing the **Mission** (if applicable).  
  - Other standard game objectives.  
- The Mortem Drudge **does not benefit** from:  
  - City population bonuses  
  - HQ control  
  - Mercenary scars  
- **Sabotage Mechanic**:  
  - If a **non-Mortem faction** controls a Small Factory, place the **Destroyed Factory scar** over it.  
  - If **The Mortem Drudge controls all factories**, **discard** the Sabotage card instead.  
- **Reinforcement Card**:  
  - If The Mortem Drudge controls the Large Factory, place sticker and receive **3 troops per turn** (up from 2).  
  - If they lose control, **discard** the card.  

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Q: Can The Mortem Drudge start on a city?**  
  A: No, they start on the Large Factory, which is not a city.  

- **Q: Do they get the +2 troops on Large Factory even if they didn’t start there?**  
  A: Yes, as long as they control it at the end of their turn.  

- **Q: What happens if a Small Factory is destroyed by sabotage?**  
  A: The scar is covered with the **Destroyed Factory** sticker and no longer provides benefits.  

- **Q: Can another faction use a Small Factory?**  
  A: Yes, but if they do, the **Sabotage card triggers**, and the factory is destroyed unless The Mortem Drudge controls all factories.  

- **Q: What happens to the Reinforcement card if The Mortem Drudge loses the Large Factory?**  
  A: The card is **discarded** and the bonus is lost.  

- **Q: Can The Mortem Drudge gain stars from Private Missions or other non-elimination sources?**  
  A: Only if specified by other game effects. Their faction ability only grants stars for **eliminations**.
