### 3. Setup
1. **Player Decks**: Each player takes one 20-card deck, shuffles it, and places it face down.
2. **Commons**:
   - **Advancements**: Separate into Level 1, 2, and 3 decks.
     - Level 1: Count out 12 (2p), 15 (3p), 18 (4p), 24 (5p), or 30 (6p) cards; shuffle and place the rest back. Reveal 3 from each level.
     - Level 2 & 3: Use all cards; reveal 3 from each.
   - **Fertile Soil**: Keep as a separate stack, always available for purchase.
3. **Vale Cards**: Shuffle Level 1 and Level 2 decks separately; reveal 4 of each face up.
4. **VP Tokens**: Create a pool of 23 (2p), 28 (3p), 33 (4p), 40 (5p), or 47 (6p) tokens.
5. **Player Fields**:
   - Shuffle deck, reveal top card as on-deck.
   - Place on-deck card into field, reveal new on-deck.
   - Repeat until field has 2 Cursed Lands and on-deck has a third (providing 3 total decay).
6. **Starting Player**: Randomly distribute 4 Mana Tokens (including one with a star). The player with the star begins.

### 4. Gameplay Flow
Each turn consists of 4 phases, taken in order:
1. **Planting Phase**
2. **Harvest Phase**
3. **Discard Phase**
4. **Prep Phase**

Play proceeds clockwise. The game ends after the round in which the VP token pool is exhausted.

### 5. Core Mechanics
- **Card Locations**:
  - **Field**: Cards in play, used for abilities and symbols.
  - **On-Deck**: Top card of deck, face-up. Counts for spoil check; ongoing abilities active.

### 6. Scoring & End Game
- **Trigger**: When VP token pool is exhausted, finish the current round.
- **Final Scoring**:
  - VP tokens collected during game.
  - Grey VP symbols on advancements (right side).
  - Grey VP symbols on vale cards.
- **Winning**: Most VP wins.
- **Tiebreaker**:
  1. Most combined Level 3 advancements and Level 2 vale cards.
  2. If still tied, players share the win.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Spoiling**: Can occur in Planting or Prep Phase. Skips Harvest, but Discard and Prep proceed. Mana token flipped if inactive.
- **On-Deck Card**: Not in deck or field. Only mana and decay/growth count. Ongoing abilities active.
- **Sleeving**: Cannot cover advancements unless long-wise. Fertile Soil and Eclipse advancements may have exceptions.
- **Deck Exhaustion**: Shuffle discard pile to form new deck when drawing.
- **Mana Token**: Can remain active across turns. Only spent during Harvest Phase.
- **Buying Fertile Soil**: Choose any from stack; counts toward 2-advancement limit.
- **Wild Spirit**: Can substitute for any spirit symbol.
- **Guardian Symbol**: Only used when referenced by abilities.
- **No Legal Sleeve Spot**: Cannot buy advancement.
- **Harvest Abilities**: Resolved only from cards in field, not on-deck.
