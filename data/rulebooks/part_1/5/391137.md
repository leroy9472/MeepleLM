### 1. Lore & Objective
Players are supervisors at Galactic Cruise, a company offering luxury space vacations. The goal is to earn the most Victory Points (VP) by constructing ships, satisfying guests, and impressing the Board of Directors to become the new CEO.

### 2. Components
- Main Board
- Player Boards
- Workers and Expert Workers
- Cruise Consultants
- Development tokens (gears)
- Progress Cubes
- Reputation Trackers
- Resource Trackers (Food, Oxygen, Fuel)
- Money and Ad tokens
- Agenda Cards
- Blueprint tiles
- Cockpit and Engine tiles
- Cruise tiles
- Guest meeples
- Upgrade Tokens
- Technology tiles
- Various reference materials

### 3. Setup
1. Place Main Board in center with Marketing Board to the right.
2. Shuffle Agenda Cards and reveal 4 faceup.
3. Place Progress Track tiles according to player count.
4. Place neutral cubes in Progress Track.
5. Place Company Goal tracker in each goal space.
6. Randomly place 4 Technology tiles faceup.
7. Place neutral Development in areas shown on facedown Technology tile.
8. Fill Blueprint display with 5 tiles.
9. Place Storage Silo Resource trackers at "2".
10. Fill Cockpit/Engine display with 4 pairs.
11. Shuffle Guest Bonus tokens and place one on each Marketing Board space.
12. Use Marketing Board overlays based on player count.
13. Fill Marketing Board with Cruise tiles.
14. Set aside Guests based on Destinations, add more if needed, then randomly place in Queue.
15. Each player takes Player Board, Employee Handbook, and components in their color:
   - Place Starting Cockpit and Engine to left of board
   - Place 2 Expert Workers on board
   - Place 2 Workers in Break Room
   - Place 9 Developments on board
   - Place 3 Large Resource trackers at "1"
   - Place 4 brown Upgrade Tokens on top of Launch Tower, 4 gray on bottom
   - Take 10 Money and 2 Ads
   - Place 3 Progress Cubes above Company Goal tile
   - Place VP Tracker at "5", Reputation Tracker at "0"
   - Place 100/200 VP token beside board
   - Place Cruise Consultant on Marketing Board
16. First player determined by most recent vacation.
17. In reverse order, draw one Agenda Card and set starting Reputation (0,1,2,3 respectively).
18. Place one Development from left column into Network (covering Reputation icon).
19. Take one Blueprint from display.
20. Slide remaining Blueprints down and refill display.

### 4. Gameplay Flow
On your turn:
1. **Advance Ships**: Move pilots on cruises to next stop, resolving effects.
2. **Choose one action**:
   - Assign a Worker: Place worker, take up to 2 actions
   - Launch a Ship: Initiate launch sequence
   - Call a Meeting: Recall workers, take one action
3. **Accomplish Company Goal**: Check if you've met any goal requirements.

### 5. Core Mechanics
**Worker Placement**
- Place worker on empty location or bump opponent's worker
- Bumped worker returns to owner's Break Room, triggering Funding Bonus
- Worker has access to actions in placed location and adjacent locations connected by developments

**Action Selection**
After placing worker, take up to 2 actions from accessible locations:
- **Acquire Blueprints**: Take up to 2 blueprints from display
- **Purchase Supplies**: Pay $1 for 2 resources and/or $1 for 2 Ads
- **Build Development**: Pay cost to place development in Network or under Technology
- **Hire Expert Worker**: Pay cost to add Expert to Break Room
- **Draw Agenda Cards**: Take 2 cards from display or pay 1 Reputation to draw from discard
- **Refill Agenda Cards**: Gain visible bonuses, discard remaining cards, refill display
- **Gain Resources**: Take up to 3 resources from Storage Silo
- **Refill Storage Silo**: Discard 1 Agenda Card to refill one resource to maximum
- **Schedule a Cruise**: Place Cruise Consultant, flip Upgrade Token for bonus
- **Advertise for a Cruise**: Pay Ads to place guests on cruise, gain Money and Reputation

**Resource Management**
- Resources (Food, Oxygen, Fuel) tracked on player board
- Reputation used to gain bonuses or pay for development access
- Money and Ads used for various actions and purchases

**Ship Construction**
- Acquire Blueprints to build ship segments
- Build up to 2 segments per action
- 2 segments = 1 cabin (1 guest), 3 segments = 2 cabins (2 guests)
- Acquire new ships (up to 3 total)

**Cruise Operations**
- Schedule cruises using Cruise Consultant
- Advertise to sell tickets to guests
- Launch ships when ready
- Advance ships through itinerary
- Score points at destinations

**Launch Sequence**
1. Assign and Board: Choose ship, place cruise, board guests
2. Place Progress Cube: Add cube to Progress Track
3. Pay Resources: Spend Food, Oxygen, Fuel
4. Score Cockpit: Score VP based on cockpit criteria
5. Load Up: Move flipped Upgrade Token to engine
6. Liftoff: Place worker in cockpit, add new cruise

### 6. Scoring & End Game
**Progress Track**
- Filled when launching ships or accomplishing company goals
- Triggers Annual General Meetings (AGMs) when sections fill
- Game ends when Section 3 is filled

**AGM Scoring (A & B)**
- Score VP = (Progress Cubes in section) × (Revealed Wings)
- Player with most Reputation gets +1 cube for scoring
- Ties: Most cubes wins, then most Reputation

**Final Scoring (AGM C)**
1. **Supplies**: 1 VP per 3 leftover supplies (resources, money, ads, agenda cards, blueprints)
2. **Progress Scoring**: Cubes in Section 3 × Revealed Wings (+1 cube for most Reputation)
3. **Reputation**: Score based on threshold (0-6: 0 VP, 7-10: 3 VP, 11-14: 8 VP, 15+: 15 VP)
4. **Ship Segments**: VP on each built segment
5. **Developments**: VP of rightmost empty column on player board
6. **Cockpits**: Score each cockpit criteria (-5 VP for unlaunched ships)

**Winning**
- Player with most VP wins
- Tiebreakers: Most Progress Cubes, then most Reputation, then most Cockpit VP, then later turn order

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
**Calling a Meeting with Workers in Space**
- Recall workers from Network only
- Gain Funding Bonus for each recalled worker
- Still get to take one action connected to your developments

**Reputation at Extremes**
- Gain 1 VP for each Reputation gained at 18
- Lose 1 VP for each Reputation lost at 0

**Spending Reputation**
- Can be done at any time during turn (once per turn)
- Can be done mid-action

**Using Other Players' Developments**
- Pay at start of turn based on current Reputation
- Access lasts entire turn
- Cost changes if Reputation changes during turn

**Last-Minute Sales**
- Can choose to take guests from Queue instead of pre-sold guests
- Must follow "Not Enough Room" rules for pre-sold guests

**Company Goals at End**
- Can accomplish goals during final advance
- Accomplish during final turn

**Running Out of Components**
- Cockpits/Engines: Display may have empty spaces
- Blueprints: Display may have empty spaces
- Upgrade Tokens: Can schedule/launch without flipping
- Money/Ads: Use substitutes
