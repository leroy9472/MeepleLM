## Comprehensive Reference Manual

1. Lore & Objective  
Players represent powerful Chinese families vying for influence by bribing officials through gift exchanges over 4 days. The goal is to earn the most Victory Points (VP) while securing an audience with the Emperor by reaching the top of the Palace of Heavenly Purity. Failure to reach the Palace results in disqualification regardless of VP.

2. Components  
- Game Board (2-sided for player count)  
- Summer Palace Board and Peasant Revolt Board (expansion)  
- Game Overview Strips  
- Day Tracker  
- Palace Tracks (left and right variants)  
- 3 Destiny Dice  
- Start Player and Next Start Player Tokens  
- Player Boards, Servant Cubes (12 per player), Black Neutral Cubes (12 per player)  
- Court Lady Meeples  
- VP Disks  
- Intrigue Markers  
- Envoy Meeples  
- Travellers  
- Canal Ships (3 per player)  
- Travel Tokens (including brown and bonus types)  
- Decree Tiles (Levels 1–4)  
- Peasant Revolt Tokens  
- Jade Pieces (unlimited supply)  
- Gift Cards (fan and yin-yang types)  
- Double Servant Meeples  
- Favourite Gift Marker  

3. Setup  
1. Place Game, Summer Palace, and Peasant Boards per player count.  
2. Attach Game Overview Strips to the Game Board.  
3. Place Day Tracker on Day 1.  
4. Randomly place one of each Palace Track beneath the Palace.  
5. Remove base game Bonus Travel Tokens.  
6. Add 6 new Panjūn Gift Cards and remove specified base cards.  
7. Combine 3 new and 5 brown Travel Tokens with base tokens; stack face-down, then place randomly face-up on cities.  
8. Roll 3 Destiny Dice and place in ascending order.  
9. Randomly determine Start Player; give Start Player Token.  
10. Deal new Gift Cards:  
   - Randomly place 7 face-up on Game Board.  
   - Form face-down Draw Deck.  
   - Deal 4 starting cards per player based on turn order (1 dark segment = 1st player, etc.).  
11. Place 1 Jade on each Jade Zone spot; 10 Jade on Summer Palace; rest in supply.  
12. Sort Decree Tiles; place 2 of each level (1–3); add Level 4 Decree on Summer Palace.  
13. Stack Peasant Revolt Tokens face-down on Peasant Board.  
14. Place Next Start Player Token in Intrigue Zone.  
15. Place 1 random Favourite Gift on Summer Palace.  
16. Each player chooses color and takes:  
   - 1 Player Board  
   - 12 Servant Cubes (6 in Pool, 6 in General Supply)  
   - 12 Black Neutral Cubes (on Summer Palace Board)  
   - 1 Court Lady (on Summer Palace Board)  
   - 1 VP Disk (on VP Track start)  
   - 1 Intrigue Marker (stacked on Intrigue Track, 1st player on bottom)  
   - 1 Envoy (on Palace Track start)  
   - 1 Traveller (near Travel Zone)  
   - 3 Canal Ships  

4. Gameplay Flow  
Gugong is played over 4 Rounds, each consisting of 3 Phases:  
- Phase 1: Morning Phase (skip in Round 1)  
- Phase 2: Day Phase  
- Phase 3: Night Phase  

5. Core Mechanics  
- Card Exchange: On your turn, exchange 1 hand card with a board card.  
  - Higher value: Free action.  
  - Equal/lower: Pay 2 Servants OR discard another card OR take no action.  
  - Exception: Value 1 card may freely exchange with value 9.  
- Actions: After exchange, perform:  
  - Card’s action (if any)  
  - Location’s action  
  - Both (card action first)  
- Travel: Move Traveller 1 space (collect token) or pay 2 Servants to move twice. First move: place on any city.  
- Great Wall: Place 1 Servant or pay 1 to place 2. Wall completes at 4/5/6/7 Servants (2–5 players).  
  - Scoring: Most Servants → 3 VP + 1 Palace step. Return all their Servants.  
  - Intrigue Benefits: Present players may lower Intrigue Marker for rewards (1 step = 1 Servant; 3 = 2 Servants; 5 = control 1 die; 7 = 1 Jade).  
- Jade: Pay Servants to gain Jade (houses or 5 Servants for 1 Jade).  
- Intrigue: Advance 1 step (take Next Start Player Token) or pay 1 Servant to advance 3.  
- Palace: Advance 1 step or pay 2 Servants to advance 2 + 1 Intrigue step.  
  - Choose Track (left/right) on first move; cannot switch.  
  - Top of track: extra steps = 1 VP each.  
- Decrees: Pay base cost +1 per opponent’s Servant present. Place 1 Servant. Score VP immediately (Levels 1–2) or at game end (Levels 3–4).  
- Grand Canal: Place 1 Servant on Ship OR pay 1 to place 2. Optionally move 1 Ship.  
  - Claim Harbor Reward at 3 Servants:  
    - H2: 4 VP  
    - H3: Draw 1 card  
    - H4: Unlock Double Servant  
    - H5: Choose any previous reward  
- Summer Palace (Expansion):  
  - Place 1 Servant from General Supply on Pier OR move back 1 Palace step to place up to 2.  
  - Ponds Scoring: Triggered at Night. Most Servants on Pier → gain benefit (2 Neutral Servants, 1 Jade, or Court Lady).  
- Peasant Revolt (Expansion):  
  - Revolt triggers when track fills (player-count based).  
  - Players bid 1 discard + optional Bid Token.  
  - If total ≥ Revolt value: lowest bidder retrieves card.  
  - If total < Revolt value: highest bidder retrieves card; others lose 1 Palace step.  
- Travel Tokens:  
  - Discard 2 → 1 Servant  
  - Discard 4 → 2 VP  
  - Discard 6 → 1 Jade  
  - Brown Token: Add Peasant Revolt Token when collected.  

6. Scoring & End Game  
Final Scoring occurs after Round 4 Night Phase:  
1. Score uncompleted Great Wall: Most Servants → 3 VP + 1 Palace step (no Intrigue Benefits).  
2. Score Level 3 and 4 Decrees for VP.  
3. Score 3 VP if still own Court Lady.  
4. Score VP by order of Palace arrival (highest available VP spots).  
5. Score Jade: 1/3/6/10/15 VP for 1–5 Jade; +2 VP per additional Jade.  
6. Level 4 Decree Bonuses:  
   - 2 VP per Neutral Servant (max 12)  
   - 2 VP per Travel Token (max 10)  
   - 2 VP per Intrigue Benefit taken  
   - Sum of card values ÷ 3  
   - Move Envoy to Palace (tie by Intrigue)  
7. Only players with Envoy at Palace top are eligible to win.  
8. Highest VP wins. Tie: Intrigue Track order.

7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- Tie-breaking: Always resolved by Intrigue Track (highest marker wins).  
- Servant Placement: Great Wall and Ships fill leftmost spaces.  
- Double Servant: Counts as 2 when placed/spent; 1 when gained. Cannot mark Decrees or Harbor rewards.  
- Court Lady: Return to use action twice (card or location).  
- Favourite Gift: Placing matching card allows scoring any Pond during turn.  
- Overflow Peasant Tokens: Set aside during full track; add after Revolt resolves.  
- Palace Track Movement: Moving back from Palace opens spot; others may advance.  
- Morning Phase Requirements: Must fulfill step conditions when moving onto/over; penalties apply if unable.
