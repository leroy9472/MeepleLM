# Firefly: The Game — Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective
In the aftermath of a civil war, independent ships drift on the fringes of a colonized solar system, surviving through trade, smuggling, and daring misdeeds. Players take on the role of a ship captain, hiring a crew, taking jobs, and navigating political and criminal dangers. The goal is to fulfill the conditions on a Story Card to win the game.

### 2. Components
- 5 Supply Decks (Crew, Gear, Ship Upgrades)
- 5 Contact Decks (Jobs)
- 2 Nav Decks (Alliance, Border Space)
- 1 Misbehave Deck
- 11 Set Up Cards (Leaders, Ships, Drive Cores)
- 4 Ship Cards
- Currency (150 bills)
- 6 Story Cards
- 2 Dice
- 167 Tokens (Fuel, Parts, Disgruntled, Warrant, Goal, etc.)
- 4 Firefly ship models
- 1 Alliance Cruiser model
- 1 Reaver Cutter model
- Game Board (with expansions: Blue Sun, Kalidasa)

### 3. Setup
1. Place the game board. Add Rim Space and expansion sectors if used.
2. Place the Alliance Cruiser in Londinium and the Reaver Cutter in the Firefly-logo sector.
3. Place "RESHUFFLE DECK" cards from Nav Decks into their discard piles (3+ players).
4. Reveal top 3 cards of each Supply Deck to discard piles.
5. Shuffle Contact, Supply, Nav, and Misbehave decks.
6. Place Bounty Deck and reveal top 3 Bounty Cards.
7. Players roll dice; highest roller chooses Leader, Ship, and Drive Core. Selection proceeds clockwise.
8. Last to choose places ship first; others place in reverse order.
9. Each player receives $3,000, 6 Fuel, 2 Parts.
10. Each player draws one Job card from each Contact Deck; discard down to 3.
11. Choose a Story Card; follow any special setup rules.

### 4. Gameplay Flow
On your turn:
1. Resolve any start-of-turn events (e.g., Reaver Contact if in same sector as Reaver Cutter).
2. Take two different actions in any order: Fly, Buy, Deal, or Work.
3. Interact freely with players in the same sector (trade, hire disgruntled crew).
4. Pass the Dinosaur Marker to the next player.

### 5. Core Mechanics
#### Actions (Choose 2, No Repeats)
- **Fly**
  - **Full Burn**: Spend 1 Fuel. Move up to your Drive Core’s range. After entering each sector, draw and resolve a Nav Card (Alliance, Border, or Rim deck based on sector). Options:
    - *Keep Flying*: Continue movement.
    - *Full Stop*: End movement.
    - *Evade*: Move to adjacent sector, end movement, no further Nav draw.
  - **Mosey**: Move 1 sector. No Fuel cost. No Nav Card drawn.
- **Buy** (at Supply Planet)
  - **Purchase Supplies**: Look through discard pile; choose up to 3 cards to consider. Draw from deck to make 3. Buy up to 2. Unpurchased cards go to discard.
  - **Buy Fuel/Parts**: $100 per Fuel, $300 per Part.
  - **Shore Leave**: Pay $100 per crew to remove all Disgruntled tokens.
- **Deal** (at Contact’s Location)
  - **Take Jobs**: Look through discard pile; choose up to 3 to consider. Draw from deck to make 3. Accept up to 2. Discard unaccepted. Must discard down to 3 inactive Jobs.
  - **Sell Goods**: If Solid with Contact, sell Cargo/Contraband at listed prices.
- **Work**
  - **Start/Complete a Job**:
    1. *Equip Crew*: Assign 1 Gear per Crew. Gear cannot change hands during job.
    2. *Confirm Needs*: Meet Skill/Keyword requirements on Job card.
    3. *Do the Job*: Follow instructions (e.g., pick up/deliver goods, Misbehave).
    4. *Outcome*: Get paid, pay crew, become Solid with Contact.
  - **Make-Work**: Earn $200 if on a planet sector.
  - **Advance a Goal**: Use Work Action to attempt Story Card Goal (may require Skill Test or Misbehave).

#### Skill Tests
- Roll 1d6, add relevant Skill points (Fight, Tech, Negotiate) from Crew and Gear.
- **Thrillin’ Heroics**: Roll a Firefly (counts as 6), roll again and add result.
- **Kosherized Fight Test**: Cannot use Fight Skill from Gear.
- **Bribes (Negotiate Test)**: Pay $100 per +1 to total before rolling.

#### Misbehaving
- Required for Illegal Jobs. Draw number of Misbehave Cards shown.
- Each card has two options. Choose one:
  - *Proceed*: Continue.
  - *Attempt Botched*: Fail, job remains active.
  - *Warrant Issued*: Fail, discard job, gain Warrant.
- "Ace in the Hole": Automatically pass if you have named Crew, Profession, or Gear.

#### Showdowns & Boarding
- **Boarding Test** (required to attack rival ship):
  - Roll Tech or Negotiate Skill. Success on 6+ (Thrillin’ Heroics applies).
- **Showdown** (PvP conflict):
  - Attacker and Defender each choose Fight or Tech.
  - Roll, add Skill. Highest wins; Defender wins ties.
  - Gear may be allocated before roll.

#### Reputations (Solid)
- Become Solid with a Contact by completing their Job.
- Flip Job card, place under Ship Card.
- Gain perks: sell goods, special abilities.
- Lose Solid if Warrant issued while working their job (except Harken: lose Solid with Harken for any Warrant).

#### Disgruntled Crew
- Crew become Disgruntled if:
  - Not paid after Job.
  - Assigned to Immoral Job (if Moral).
- Disgruntled Crew can be hired by others in same sector (pay hiring cost).
- Second Disgruntled token: discard Crew.
- Leader with second token: discard entire Crew, remove tokens.

#### Death & Medic
- When a Crew is killed, choose which to remove.
- **Medic Check**: Roll d6. 5–6: Crew returns to ship. Only one check per death.
- **Leader Killed**: Return with Disgruntled token.

### 6. Scoring & End Game
- Win by fulfilling the Story Card’s victory condition (e.g., complete all Goals).
- Story Cards may require:
  - Becoming Solid with certain Contacts.
  - Completing specific Jobs or Goals.
  - Earning credits or bounties.
- First player to meet all requirements wins.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Can you trade while not on your turn?** Yes, with players in same sector.
- **Can you dump cargo mid-action?** Yes, at any time on your turn.
- **Do you pay crew after Make-Work?** No.
- **Can you work multiple jobs in one action?** No.
- **Do you reshuffle Nav deck immediately if Reshuffle card drawn?** Yes.
- **Can you use Gear not carried?** No.
- **Can you change Gear between actions?** Yes, except during Work Action.
- **Can you hire a disgruntled Leader?** No.
- **Can you discard Active Jobs?** Only via Warrant or completion.
- **Do bonus opportunities require a Work Action?** Yes, during job advancement.
