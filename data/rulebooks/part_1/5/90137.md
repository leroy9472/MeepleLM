# Comprehensive Reference Manual

## 1. Lore & Objective
Blood Bowl: Team Manager is a card game where players take on the role of team managers in a brutal fantasy football league. The objective is to lead a team through a season of games, improving players and team value, competing in tournaments, and ultimately winning the Blood Bowl Tournament Games. Success is measured by accumulating the most fans by the end of the season.

## 2. Components
- Team Player Cards (12 per team)
- Team Upgrade Cards (5 per team, 6 with expansions)
- Staff Upgrade Cards
- Star Player Cards (divided by unions: OWA, CWC, DSS, PPG)
- Highlight Cards
- Tournament Cards (including Chaos Cup, Dungeonbowl, etc.)
- Headline Cards
- Cheating Tokens
- Ball Tokens
- Tackle Dice (2)
- Scoreboards
- Team Tokens
- Golden Coin Marker
- Contract Tokens (Sudden Death expansion)
- Penalty Cards (Foul Play expansion)
- Enchanted Ball Tokens (optional)
- Disease Tokens (Foul Play expansion)
- Blood Tokens (Sudden Death expansion)
- Stadium Cards (optional)
- Corrupt Ref token (optional)

## 3. Setup
1. Shuffle the Highlight deck and place it at one end of the play area.
2. Each manager chooses a team and takes:
   - 12 Starting Player Cards
   - 5 (or 6 with expansions) Team Upgrade Cards
   - 3 Team Tokens
   - 1 Scoreboard (set to "00")
3. Shuffle each manager's Player and Team Upgrade decks separately.
4. Prepare Star Player decks by union (OWA, CWC, DSS, PPG) and place near the Highlight deck.
5. Shuffle Staff Upgrade deck (excluding specific cards if not using "No Salary Cap" variant).
6. Prepare Spike! Magazine deck:
   - Set aside "The Blood Bowl" card
   - Randomly select 2 Tournament cards and 2 Headline cards
   - Shuffle them together and place "The Blood Bowl" card on the bottom
7. Mix Cheating Tokens facedown to form a pool.
8. Place Ball Tokens and Tackle Dice in reach.
9. Youngest manager takes the Golden Coin and becomes First Manager.

## 4. Gameplay Flow
Each game consists of 5 Rounds (weeks), each with 3 phases:

### Maintenance Phase
1. Refresh exhausted cards
2. Replenish hand to 6 cards
3. Restock Cheating Token Pool
4. Reveal top Spike! Magazine card
5. "Roll the Highlights" - draw and place Highlight cards
6. Prepare for Kickoff - place Ball Tokens

### Matchup Phase
Beginning with First Manager and proceeding clockwise:
1. Commit 1 Player to a Matchup
   - Resolve "When Played" abilities
   - Use Skill Icons (Cheating, Passing, Sprinting, Tackling) from left to right
2. Resolve 1 Matchup Action (optional) - exhaust Staff/Team Upgrade card
3. Pass when unable or unwilling to play

### Scoreboard Phase
1. Resolve Matchups in order from Highlight deck:
   a. Reveal Cheating Tokens
   b. Resolve Scoreboard Phase abilities
   c. Determine Winner
   d. Collect Payouts
   e. Clear the Pitch
2. Reveal Improvement Pile
3. Pass Golden Coin to left
4. Check if "The Blood Bowl" tournament was resolved (game end)

## 5. Core Mechanics
### Matchups
- **Highlights**: Two-team matchups with team zone payouts and central payout
- **Tournaments**: Multi-team matchups with Winner, Runner-up, and LOSE! payouts
- Only two managers can compete at a Highlight
- Any number can compete at a Tournament

### Player States
- **Standing**: Card perpendicular to Highlight, uses Standing Star Power
- **Downed**: Card rotated 90Â°, uses Downed Star Power, drops ball if carrier
- **Injured**: Removed to discard pile, discards cheating tokens, drops ball

### Skills
- **Cheating** (mandatory): Assign random facedown cheating token
- **Passing** (optional): Move ball to/from midfield or between teammates
- **Sprinting** (optional): Draw card, then discard one from hand
- **Tackling** (optional): Compare Star Power, roll dice accordingly
- **Fouling** (Foul Play): Steal and potentially discard opponent's card
- **Regeneration** (Sudden Death/Foul Play): Downed skill to potentially stand

### Tackle Resolution
- Attacker SP > Defender SP: Roll 2 dice, attacker chooses result
- Attacker SP = Defender SP: Roll 1 die, apply result
- Attacker SP < Defender SP: Roll 2 dice, defender chooses result

### Tackle Results
- **Target Down**: Target becomes Downed (Standing) or Injured (Downed)
- **Target Missed**: No effect
- **Tackler Down**: Tackler becomes Downed (Standing) or Injured (Downed)

### Payout Collection
- **Fans**: Gain fans immediately
- **Star Players**: Draw cards, draft one, return others to bottom
- **Team/Staff Upgrades**: Draw cards, draft one, return others to bottom
- **Contracts**: Draw facedown tokens, reveal at game end
- **Either/Or**: Choose one of the separated rewards

## 6. Scoring & End Game
The game ends after resolving "The Blood Bowl" tournament in Round 5.

### Final Scoring
1. Gain fans from "End of Game" abilities on Team/Staff Upgrades
2. Reveal and score Contract Tokens
3. Total fans determine winner

### Winning
- Manager with most fans wins
- Tiebreaker 1: Most improvements (Star Players + Team Upgrades + Staff Upgrades)
- Tiebreaker 2: Tied managers lose all fans, winner is manager with most fans among non-tied
- If all still tied: Nobody wins

## 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Morg 'N Thorg**: Cannot have multiple copies in same matchup (OWA and CWC versions are considered the same)
- **Freebooter Star Players**: Must return one player to box, then shuffle Freebooter into deck
- **Non-Freebooter Star Players**: Place on top of deck in any order
- **Cheating Tokens**: Set aside when discarded, return to pool in next Maintenance Phase
- **Downed Skills**: Resolve immediately when player is downed, interrupting current turn
- **Regeneration**: Must be resolved in sequence with other downed skills; if successful, subsequent downed skills are ignored
- **Banned Skills** (Stadiums): Using banned skill results in penalty
- **Player Limit** (Stadiums): Exceeding limit results in penalty
- **Star Power Requirement** (Stadiums): Using player outside SP range results in penalty
- **Corrupt Ref**: Assigns faceup cheating token, moves based on committed player's SP
- **Enchanted Balls**: Provide Star Power, Fans, or Skills when ball carrier changes
- **Blood Tokens** (Black Fangs): Increase player's SP by 1 per token, removed at end of round
- **Disease Tokens** (Nurgle): Decrease player's SP by 1 per token, removed at end of round
- **Penalties**: Draw Penalty card when received, resolve at end of Scoreboard Phase
