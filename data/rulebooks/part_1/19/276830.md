## Comprehensive Reference Manual

1. **Lore & Objective**  
   - The Demon Lord Malghazar has been released from the Jade Sarcophagus, and his minions ravage the land.  
   - Players take on the roles of heroes journeying through Sanctum to defeat the Demon Lord.  
   - The objective is to survive the Final Battle in the best health. Among those who defeat the Demon Lord, the winner is the one with the highest remaining Hit Points. Ties are broken in favor of the player with more Achievements.

2. **Components**  
   - 3 double-sided act boards  
   - 3 divine intervention tiles  
   - 1 achievement board with 12 achievement tiles  
   - 1 hordes board  
   - 84 demon cards (3 decks by level)  
   - 33 Demon Lord cards  
   - 18 fury cards  
   - 6 starting bonus cards  
   - 18 double-sided potion tiles  
   - Stamina, focus, and hit markers (28+ each)  
   - 84 gems in four colors  
   - 24 dice  
   - 4 player boards  
   - 4 quick reference sheets  
   - 4 player figures  
   - 5 rage tiles  
   - 4 health counters  
   - 12 skill tiles and 24 skill cards  

3. **Setup**  
   - **Act Boards**:  
     - 4-player: Use all 6 acts in order.  
     - 3-player: Skip Act III.  
     - 2-player: Skip Act II and Act IV.  
     - Only two boards are used at a time.  
   - **Demon Decks**:  
     - Sort demon cards by icon (level I, II, III), shuffle each deck, and place on hordes board.  
   - **Achievement Board**:  
     - Shuffle 11 achievement tiles and place randomly (12th returned to box).  
     - 2-player: Randomly block one space per level; place 8 tiles on remaining spaces.  
   - **Divine Intervention Tiles**:  
     - Act V tile always used; place on achievement board with one die per player.  
     - Act II (3-player) or Act III (2- or 4-player) tile placed on corresponding board with one die per player.  
   - **Player Setup**:  
     - Each player takes matching figure, places rage tile (active side up), starts with 2 dice.  
     - Place 1 white gem and 1 die (from angel icon) in gem pool.  
     - Set up skill table with initial skills.  
     - Stamina and focus pools start at values shown on player board.  

4. **Gameplay Flow**  
   - Players take turns clockwise, choosing one of three actions: Move, Fight, or Rest.  
   - **First Action**: Must be Move.  
   - **Turn Order**:  
     1. Move  
     2. Fight  
     3. Rest  
   - Game progresses through acts. Act V begins when a player reaches the Walls of Sanctum.  
   - Act VI is the Final Battle against the Demon Lord.  

5. **Core Mechanics**  
   - **Move**:  
     - Advance to front of line on current act board.  
     - First player on board: deals 5 sets of demons; all others: add 1 set.  
     - Take 1 set of demons and place on battle board.  
     - At treasure chest (end of board): flip remaining demon cards to item side; players choose one item each in marching order.  
     - **Divine Intervention**: When a player lands on the space, all players gain 1 extra die permanently.  
   - **Fight**:  
     - **Before Roll**: Discard potions to regenerate stamina/focus.  
     - **Roll**: Roll all dice.  
     - **Attack**: Assign dice to hit demon locations. Use abilities or rage to modify dice.  
     - **Block**: Use shield abilities to block damage from undefeated demons.  
     - **Gain Levels & Items**: For each defeated demon, move gems to gain levels. Flip card to claim item (stored in item sack).  
     - **Check Achievements**: Trigger if conditions are met.  
     - **Rage**: If unassigned dice remain and undefeated demons exist, flip rage tile to active.  
   - **Rest**:  
     - Restore all spent stamina and focus.  
     - Reorganize and equip items (spend matching gems).  
     - Buy potions: discard 1 item = gain 1 potion (red or blue).  
     - Check Achievements.  
     - **First Item Equipped**: Gain the third die from gem pool.  

6. **Scoring & End Game**  
   - **Act V – The Walls of Sanctum**:  
     - First player to reach Act V triggers:  
       - All players gain 1 die.  
       - Player may **Breaching the Walls** if no demons remain: move to cathedral, deal 2 face-down Demon Lord cards.  
     - **Call to Arms**: Other players may discard demons and join by moving to latest Demon Lord card. Mandatory after 4th card.  
     - **A Brief Respite**: After all join, all take final Rest.  
     - **The Demon Lord's Answer**: Reveal all Demon Lord cards; each player suffers effects based on position.  
   - **Act VI – The Final Battle**:  
     - **Setup**: Each player deals 5 Demon Lord and 4 Fury cards alternately on their board (left to right).  
     - **Battle**:  
       - Fight cards in order.  
       - Move to next card after defeating current.  
       - Flip Fury cards when landed on; resolve effect immediately (only to that player).  
       - Block damage from current card and all undefeated ahead.  
       - Use potions at start; use blessings (flip after use).  
     - **The Demon Lord Roars**: After each round, flip and resolve Demon Lord cards on Act VI board:  
       - Normal: 2-card row → 1-card row → no more.  
       - Harder modes: start with 3–5 cards, decreasing each time.  
     - **Victory**:  
       - If all players die: winner is the one who defeated the most cards (tiebreak: hits on current card).  
       - If any survive: among victors, highest HP wins (tiebreak: most Achievements).  

7. **FAQ or Edge Cases**  
   - **Rage Activation**: Only if unassigned dice remain AND undefeated demons exist.  
   - **Item Flipping**: Flip demon card only after gaining levels.  
   - **Gem Movement**: Move one gem at a time; white gems match any color.  
   - **Achievement Gains**: Can gain multiple at once; resolve immediately.  
   - **Potion Use**: Can use at start of any Fight; limited by potion slots (usually 4).  
   - **Simultaneous Play**: Fights and Demon Lord effects can be resolved simultaneously.  
   - **Hidden Info**: Do not look at back of demon cards.  
   - **Difficulty**: Set before game; affects number of cards in Demon Lord Roars.
