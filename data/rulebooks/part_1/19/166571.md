## Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective  
Tramways is a solo or competitive city-building strategy game where players develop a network of tram lines and buildings to transport passengers, earn money, and gain Happiness Points (HP). The objective is to maximize your final HP through efficient rail construction, building development, and passenger movement, while managing stress and resources.

The player with the most HP at the end of six rounds wins. Ties are broken by cash on hand.

---

### 2. Components  
- Player boards with stress and HP tracks  
- Map boards (arranged 3×1 in solo)  
- Parcel cards and markers  
- Building tiles (Residence, Commerce, Industry, Leisure) and matching building cards  
- Rail tiles (standard and upgraded)  
- Passenger meeples  
- Rail Workers (grey) and Super Rail Workers  
- Generic cards (Build 2 Rail + Get Worker, Get $1 + Construct Building)  
- Auction deck (includes Parcel, Action, and Void cards)  
- Development (D) cards  
- Ticket books (with magnetic strip cards)  
- Round marker and Ticket Inspector (solo)  
- Money (coins in $1, $5, $20)  

---

### 3. Setup  
**Solo Setup:**  
1. Choose 3 map boards (at least 4 Parcel spaces; max one "J" board). Arrange 3×1.  
2. Find matching Parcel cards. Shuffle all Parcel cards, draw 4, and mark those parcels with your markers.  
3. Add remaining Parcel cards to all Auction cards. Shuffle to form Auction deck.  
4. Place black round marker on Round I of solo board.  
5. Draw 5 random small building tiles and place one next to each Round I–V space.  
6. Take player board, $3, 2 Rail Workers (3 in expanded rules), 1 Super Rail Worker.  
7. Take 2 Generic cards:  
   - Build 2 Rail + Get Rail Worker  
   - Get $1 + Construct Building  
8. Shuffle 6 Development cards into 2 piles of 3. Reveal top card of each. Choose one card from one pile; return pile with top card face-up.  
9. Place one set of Building tiles and cards near the board (2 sets for Industry).  
10. Place 1 Passenger on each building space (including Station). Randomly replace one with the Mayor.  
11. Place Station, Stock Exchange, Park, Funfair, and Airports on valid terrain (e.g., Park adjacent to plains).

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
Each of the 6 rounds follows this sequence:  

#### C. Auction Phase  
**Rounds I–V:**  
1. Pay $1 for each card currently in the auction row (free on Round I).  
2. Reveal a new card from the Auction deck.  
3. Choose:  
   a. Take the newest card, increase Stress +1  
   b. Take any older revealed card for free  
   c. Pay another $1 and repeat steps 1–3  
4. Discard all auction row cards after selection. Reshuffle discards if deck runs out.  

**Round VI:**  
- Increase Stress +2. No card selection.  

#### D. Action Phase (Two Action Rounds)  
**Action Round 1:**  
- Perform 1 action **or** take $2  

**Action Round 2:**  
- Perform 2 actions  
- Or 1 action + $2  
- Or take $2 only  

**Action Rules:**  
- Use action icons from cards in hand.  
- First icon per card: no penalty. Each additional icon from same card: +1 Stress.  
- Pay any card consequence (cost) **before** using the card.  
- Must fully complete an action or not attempt it.  

**Available Actions:**  
- **Build New Rail**  
  - Play ≥1 Build Rail icon(s)  
  - Discard 1 Rail Worker  
  - Cost per tile:  
    - Plains: 1 Rail icon  
    - Mountains: 2 Rail icons  
    - Rivers: 3 Rail icons (+2 HP when completed)  
    - Forests: -1 HP per Forest tile built  
  - To complete a link: play a destination icon (Building type or Parcel) matching one endpoint  
  - Incomplete links not extended in a round are removed at end of round  

- **Upgrade Existing Link**  
  - Play 1 Upgrade Link icon  
  - Play 1 destination icon (matching either end)  
  - Flip all rail tiles in link to upgraded side  
  - Gain +3 HP  
  - Upgraded links earn +50% money from passenger movement  

- **Construct New Building**  
  - Play 1 Construct Building icon  
  - Play 1 Parcel icon (matching unoccupied Parcel you own)  
  - Place Building tile (of any available type) on Parcel  
  - Take matching Building card into hand  
  - Add 1 Passenger to building  
  - Gain +1 HP  
  - If Industrial building: hand limit increases by 1 (max 9)  

- **Upgrade Existing Building**  
  - Play 1 Upgrade Building icon  
  - Play 1 Building Type icon matching the building  
  - Flip building tile to upgraded side  
  - If no Passenger present, add 1  
  - Gain +3 HP  

- **Move a Passenger**  
  - Play 1 Ticket card (magnetic strip)  
  - Play 1 destination icon (Building type or Parcel)  
  - Move Passenger along connected links  
  - Must stop at **first** matching destination  
  - Cannot visit same Building or Parcel twice  
  - **In order:**  
    1. Each link traversed: owner gains +1 HP  
    2. Delivering player gains destination bonus  
    3. Each rail tile traversed: owner earns $1 (upgraded: +50%, rounded up)  
       - Your links: Bank pays you  
       - Others’ links: You pay owner. If insufficient funds, pay what you can, then no further payments and gain +1 Stress  
  - Passenger is returned to pool (unless Mayor, who stays)  
  - Moving Mayor: gain +1 HP  

- **Convert to Residence / Commerce / Industry**  
  - Requires:  
    - 2 matching destination icons (e.g., R R)  
    - 1 preprinted, non-developed, non-matching building  
  - Place token (Residence, Commerce, Industry) on building  
  - Gain:  
    - +3 HP  
    - +1 Development card  
  - (Note: Convert to Leisure not detailed in core rules)  

- **Build a Tower** (Expansion)  
  - Requires:  
    - Multiple Parcel Number icons (e.g., A1 A1 A1)  
    - 1 empty space (not water, lake, rail, or tower)  
  - Pay -1 HP  
  - Place Tower  

#### E. Administration Phase  
Simultaneously, players may use any of the following from remaining cards:  
- **Get Money**: Bank pays amount shown  
- **Get Rail Worker**: Gain 1 if fewer than 2 (or 3 with expansion)  
- **Decrease Stress**: Reduce Stress -1  
- **Add Passenger**: Add 1 Passenger to any building square without one  
- **Discard Cards**:  
  - 1st card: free  
  - Each additional: pay $1  
  - Consequences (costs) must be paid when discarding  

#### F. End of Round (Skip in Round VI)  
1. **Refill Hand**:  
   - Rounds I–IV: Draw up to hand limit (7, 8, or 9)  
   - Round V: Lay out entire deck and discard pile; choose cards to fill hand  
2. **Stress Check**: If Stress at 21, lose -1 HP  
3. **Add Passengers**: Add 1 Passenger to each empty building space matching the building type tile next to current round marker  
4. **Remove Incomplete Links**: Any link not extended this round is removed  
5. **Advance Round Marker**  
6. **Ticket Inspector**: Place on any of your links (Heart of Commerce expansion)  
7. **Fill Station**: Add Passengers up to 2 maximum

### 5. Core Mechanics  
- **Action Icons**: Must use at least one per card. Extra icons cost +1 Stress each.  
- **Card Consequences**: Must pay cost (money or stress) when playing or discarding a card.  
- **Link Completion**: A complete link connects two buildings or a building and a parcel.  
- **Passenger Movement**:  
  - Must stop at first matching destination  
  - Payments made in order of travel  
  - Inability to pay halts further payments but trip continues  
- **Stress**:  
  - Reaching 21 or beyond: lose -1 HP  
  - Can be reduced via Administration or Residential deliveries  
- **Hand Limit**: Starts at 7–9; can increase via Industrial buildings  

---

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
**Final Scoring (after Round VI):**  
- HP on scoring track  
- +3 HP for each **completed link**  
- +1 HP for every **$10** in cash (round down)  
- **–X HP** where X = final Stress level (1 to 21)  

**Final Score =**  
HP + (3 × Completed Links) + (Cash ÷ 10) – Stress  

**Winning**: Highest final score wins. Tiebreaker: most cash.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Can I build on my parcel without completing a link?**  
  Yes, but it doesn’t count as a stop unless connected to a building.

- **What if I can’t pay for passenger movement?**  
  Pay what you can. No further payments occur, gain +1 Stress. Trip still completes.

- **Can I use money icons during Auction?**  
  Yes. You may discard cards with money icons to pay bids. Bank gives change.

- **Do upgraded links give more HP?**  
  No. Only +50% money from passenger movement. HP per link is always +1.

- **Can I move a passenger through my own links only?**  
  Yes. You pay yourself via the Bank.

- **What happens to Development cards?**  
  Kept in hand. Can be used for bonuses (e.g., when delivering to Commerce).

- **Can I discard cards during Action Phase?**  
  No. Only during Administration.

- **What if I run out of Building tiles?**  
  Cannot build more of that type.

- **Can I redirect an incomplete link?**  
  Yes, for cost of 1 Rail icon at start of build action.
