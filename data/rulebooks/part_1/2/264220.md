# Comprehensive Reference Manual

## 1. Lore & Objective
Tainted Grail: The Fall of Avalon is a cooperative narrative-driven adventure game set in a dark, mythic version of Arthurian legend. Players take on the roles of characters exploring the island of Avalon, uncovering secrets, completing quests, and surviving dangerous encounters. The primary objective is to progress through the campaign by completing chapter goals, advancing characters, and surviving the evolving world. Victory is achieved by completing the final chapter objectives.

## 2. Components
- Character tiles and trays
- Combat and diplomacy cards (basic and advanced)
- Encounter cards (green, gray, purple, blue decks)
- Location cards
- Menhir models and dials
- Guardian models and die
- Item and secret cards
- Resource cubes (Food, Wealth, Magic, Reputation, Experience)
- Health and terror markers
- Time tokens and dials
- Event cards
- Exploration Journal
- Save sheets
- Direction keys and adventure maps
- Icon guide and turn order cards

## 3. Setup
1. Choose game mode (Story, Standard, Challenge, or Free Play).
2. Each player selects a character, takes matching character tray and tile, and marks starting energy, terror, and health.
3. Record starting attributes and resources from character tile.
4. Create starter decks: Select 15 basic combat and 15 basic diplomacy cards matching character tray color.
5. Form advancement pools: Combine 15 advanced cards with 10 remaining basic cards of matching color for both combat and diplomacy.
6. Place character on starting location card (#101 for Fall of Avalon).
7. Place menhir on starting location with dial set to 8 (1 player), 7 (2 players), 6 (3 players), or 5 (4 players).
8. Place adjacent location cards matching direction keys on starting card.
9. Sort encounter cards by color into four decks.
10. Shuffle item cards and sort secret cards by number.
11. Prepare save sheet and give each player icon guide and turn order card.
12. Designate a chronicler (recommended for 2-4 players).

## 4. Gameplay Flow
### The Order of the Day
**1. Start of the Day (skip on first day)**
- Resolve start-of-day effects
- Remove expired menhirs (no dial)
- Remove locations out of menhir range
- Reduce menhir dials by 1
- Reduce time dials and remove 1 time token from location cards
- Move guardians
- Reveal and read new event card
- Discuss plans (co-op)
- Equip journey (limit 1 item per keyword faceup)

**2. During the Day**
- Players take turns performing one action each in agreed order (lowest character number first if no agreement)
- Actions continue in rounds until all characters pass or run out of energy
- Group of 2+ players in same location may take party actions together

**3. End of the Day**
- Resolve end-of-day effects
- Rest: Pay 1 Food to gain 1 Energy and lose 1 Terror
- Restore energy to starting slot (only 4 points if exhausted)
- Advance character (spend EXP)
- Build character decks (customize combat/diplomacy decks of 15 cards)
- Experience dreams/nightmares if in location with dream icon

## 5. Core Mechanics
### Actions
- **Explore (cost 1 Energy)**: Flip location card and follow text/explore journal. In party: costs 1 per member, effects apply to all, rewards shared.
- **Travel (cost 1 Energy)**: Move to connected location (matching direction keys). Move in straight lines only.
- **Location Action (cost varies)**: Activate action on location card. May be "once per day" (marked with time token). In party: pay cost once, receive output once.
- **Character Action (cost varies)**: Use personal action on character tile. Only character can use it, but party may help pay cost (performing character must pay at least 1).
- **Inspect a Menhir (cost 0)**: Free action in locations with menhir seal. Flip card to read menhir section. Activate if requirements met. Costs scale with number of players.
- **Pass (cost 0)**: End your turn. Cannot take more actions until next day.

### Party Rules
- Players in same location may form party to take actions together
- Party members can trade Food, Wealth, Items, Secrets at any time when not in encounter
- Party members can pay costs for each other (including Energy, Magic)
- When performing character action, active character must pay at least 1 resource
- Characters may leave party before action begins
- If party member cannot/will not pay cost, they drop out and others continue
- Cannot leave party during action unless escaping encounter

### Exploration Rules
- Requirement: One party member meets attribute/item requirement
- Rolls: Use highest party member attribute value (do not add together)
- Gain: Rewards shared unless specified "each player gains"
- Pay: Cannot choose option if insufficient resources; those who can't/won't pay drop out
- Lose: Can lose resources even if have fewer than specified (lose as many as possible)
- Trading: Free exchange of Food, Wealth, Items, Secrets among party members
- Sharing costs: Party members can pay Energy or Magic for each other

### Combat & Diplomacy
**Initiation**
- Combat: Draw from green, gray, or purple encounter decks
- Diplomacy: Draw from blue encounter deck
- Check encounter traits
- All party members set aside unused items (max 1 per keyword)

**Card Drawing**
- 1-3 characters: Draw 3 cards each
- 4 characters: Draw 2 cards each
- May discard and redraw with 1 less card, repeating until 1 card

**Turn Structure**
1. Pick Active Character (lowest number if no agreement)
2. Character Activation
   - Resolve delayed abilities (remove 1 time token, trigger if last)
   - Play cards
     - Play 1st card (no connection requirements)
     - Play additional cards (must connect by key)
     - In Combat: Check for opportunity attack if no cards played
     - Perform victory/affinity check
   - Enemy attack (Combat) or Opponent's response (Diplomacy)
3. Check Readiness (all activated? If not, return to step 1)
4. End Turn
   - Discard down to 3 cards
   - Draw 1 card (unless panicking)
   - Remove time tokens from character trays
   - Return to step 1

- Victory Check: Combat pool markers â‰¥ target value = win
- Opportunity Attack: Triggered when no cards played or escaping
- Negative Combat Pool: Discard 1 card per excess point needed
- No Cards in Deck: Forced to escape

**Diplomacy Specifics**
- Affinity Check: Marker on highest slot = win stage
- Multiple stages may exist
- No Cards in Deck: Lose encounter

**Panic**
- Occurs when Health > Energy (marker above T-limiter wings)
- Play first card from top of deck instead of hand
- Do not draw at end of turn
- Bonus cards from abilities still drawn

**Escaping**
- Combat: Lose 1 Energy, receive opportunity attack, place encounter on bottom of deck
- Diplomacy: Apply failure rules
- In party: Escaping character leaves encounter; others continue

### Menhirs
- Located in areas with menhir seal
- Inspect for free by flipping location card
- Activation costs scale with number of players
- Extend duration by reactivating (previous power lost)
- If last menhir expires: characters lose 2 Energy and gain 2 Terror at each Start of Day

### Guardians
- If failed encounter, place card on location
- Start of each day: roll guardian die
  - Cardinal direction: move guardian
  - Nothing: no move
  - Discard: remove guardian
- If moves to invalid location: move to highest-numbered connected location
- Immediate encounter when guardian and character occupy same location
- Trigger guardian before instant actions on location

### Character Advancement
**Attribute Advancement**
- Cost based on total in opposing pair:
  - 1st point: 2 EXP
  - 2nd point: 4 EXP
  - 3rd point: 6 EXP
  - 4th point: 8 EXP
  - 5th+: 10 EXP
- Raise attribute to 2+ to gain skill card (choose one from pair)

**Deck Advancement**
- Pay 2 EXP: Draw 3 from advancement pool, pick 1, return 2
- Decks must contain exactly 15 cards
- Swap cards in/out at end of day

### Items & Secrets
- Keywords: WEAPON, ARMOR, SHIELD, COMPANION, RELIC
- Max 1 faceup item per keyword
- Flip used items facedown during combat/diplomacy; flip back after
- Craftable items marked with "C"
- Draw craftable item: keep drawing until first "C" item, take it, shuffle rest
- Secrets: story-related, numbered, never lost unless specified
- Trade items/secrets when in same location and not in action
- PERSONAL secrets cannot be transferred

## 6. Scoring & End Game
### Victory
- Complete chapter objectives as directed by Exploration Journal
- Progress through campaign to complete final chapter

### Defeat
- Single player: character dies (Health reaches 0)
- Cooperative: all characters dead and no replacements available
- Challenge Mode: any death results in campaign failure
- All characters die and choose not to use Allmother's Mercy

### Death & Insanity
**Death**
- Attach "You Are Dying" card, escape encounter
- Single player: game ends
- Cooperative:
  - Take unclaimed character if available (place in lowest-numbered location)
  - Items left on death location (others can pick up)
  - Distribute secrets (owner's choice); PERSONAL secrets disappear
  - Game ends if no characters available

**Insanity**
- Terror reaches top two red slots: attach "You Are Going Insane" card
- Experience nightmares instead of dreams

**Allmother's Mercy**
- Survive death by marking "Saved by the goddess" on save sheet
- Restart at chapter beginning with predefined resources
- Not available in Challenge Mode

## 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Multiple Guardians**: When entering location, choose encounter order
- **Discard Empty Hand**: Discard from top of deck
- **Simultaneous Effects**: Resolve in player-chosen order
- **Tie-breaking**: Lowest character number decides
- **Resource Limits**: Max 20 of each resource (except EXP)
- **Item Limits**: No limit on ownership, but max 1 faceup per keyword
- **Menhir Range**: Locations must be adjacent (including diagonally) to active menhir
- **Saving Game**: After chapter completion or emergency; record character and world state
- **Adding/Removing Players**: Distribute removed character's secrets; new characters start with veteran
- **Story Mode**: Setup as if one fewer player; remove Health limiter in solo; reduce menhir costs
- **Challenge Mode**: Increased EXP costs; flip Health limiter; take Secret 42; no Allmother's Mercy
