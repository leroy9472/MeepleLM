# Comprehensive Reference Manual

1. Lore & Objective  
Dwellings of Eldervale is a turn-based strategy game where players control factions returning to the ruined kingdom of Eldervale to harness its elemental powers, build Dwellings, and claim victory. The goal is to score the most Victory Points (VP) through constructing Dwellings, gaining Elemental Power, activating Tableau cards, and fulfilling quests and prophecies.

3. Setup  
- Place the Score Board and Resource Trays.  
- Players select a faction and corresponding Player Tray and Starter Card.  
- Determine starting player by dice roll (highest die wins).  
- Place scoring markers on VP Track according to turn order.  
- Select Elements in play: all chosen faction Elements plus 2 additional.  
- For each Element in play: 3 Elemental Realms, 1 Monster, 1 Orb.  
- Build Eldervale:  
  - Shuffle Elemental Realms and draw initial tiles (5 for 2p, 7 for 3p, 8 for 4/5p). Limit to 1 Lair tile.  
  - Add Ruins (Mage Tower, Fortress, Mill, Portal, Dungeon; second Dungeon in 4/5p).  
  - Randomly arrange tiles into map.  
  - Reshuffle remaining tiles into Realm Stack (in Discard Tray).  
- Prepare Realms:  
  - Place 3 Treasure Tokens per stack on Elemental Realms.  
  - Place Monster in Lair.  
- Shuffle Magic Deck; each player draws 5 cards.  
- Place 1 Orb at top of each Elemental Power Track and 2 on Glory Track.  
- Player Setup:  
  - Place 3 Workers in Ready Area.  
  - Keep Dragon, Wizard, Warrior, and 3 Workers in Tray.  
  - Keep 6 Rooftops in Tray.  
  - Place 1 Gem, 1 Potion, 1 Scroll, 1 Tool, 1 Sword in Tray.  
  - Place 1 scoring marker on matching Element‚Äôs Power Track.  
  - Keep 1 marker near Glory Track (to be placed after first Regroup).  
  - Optional: give each player a Tactics Token (Tactics side up).

4. Gameplay Flow  
On your turn, choose one:  
A. Place a Unit  
B. Regroup  
You may perform any number of Free Actions during your turn.

A. Place a Unit  
1. Select a Unit from Ready Area and place it in a Realm.  
2. Immediately perform the Realm‚Äôs action.  
3. After action, Monster Rush may occur (if adjacent Monster).  
4. Battle is triggered if:  
   - Placing in a Realm with opponent‚Äôs Unit or Monster.  
   - Monster Rushes into a Realm with your Unit.  
   - Opponent places in your Realm.

B. Regroup  
1. Retrieve Units from Realms:  
   - Move one Unit at a time from Realms to:  
     a. Perform a Tableau card action, or  
     b. Return to Ready Area.  
2. Return Units from Underworld to Ready Area (no card actions).  
3. Return Units from Tableau cards to Ready Area.  
4. Return Mercenaries hired with Gold to supply.

5. Core Mechanics  
- **Unit Placement**:  
  - First Placement: in any unoccupied Realm (no Units, no Monsters).  
    - Warrior may place in occupied Realm (Aggression).  
  - Subsequent Placements: adjacent to your Unit.  
    - Dragon: up to 2 Realms away (Flight).  
    - Wizard: any unoccupied Realm (Teleport).  
- **Realm Actions**:  
  - Elemental Realm: take top Treasure Token (or resource if stack empty).  
  - Mage Tower: spend 2 resources to draw 3 Magic Cards, discard 1.  
  - Fortress: sell 2 resources for 2 Gold.  
  - Mill: pay cost to convert Worker in Elemental Realm to Dwelling.  
  - Dungeon:  
    1. Place new Realm tile (adjacent to 2+ tiles).  
    2. Buy 1 Adventure Card (pay cost).  
    3. Gain 1 Elemental Power in card‚Äôs Element.  
    4. Buy 2nd card or burn 1 face-up card.  
    5. Reveal new cards.  
  - Summoning Portal: pay cost on Starter Card to summon Unit to Ready Area.  
  - Oracle: gain 1 Magic or Adventure Card (pay cost).  
  - Bifrost Bridge: gain 1 Magic Card.  
  - Dragon‚Äôs Den: gain 1 Gold; defeat Mother of Dragons to gain Trove Token.  
- **Battle**:  
  - Triggered after placement or Monster Rush.  
  - Steps:  
    1. Join: players move Units from adjacent Realms into battle (starting left of active player).  
    2. Battle Dice:  
       - Base dice from Units.  
       - +1 die for Dwelling in battle Realm.  
       - +1 die per adjacent Dwelling (Castles grant +2).  
    3. Spend Swords: each Sword spent = +1 die (max 6 dice per player).  
    4. Roll: all participants roll simultaneously.  
    5. Winner: highest single die wins; ties broken by next highest die. True tie = all lose.  
    6. Winner gains:  
       - Advance on Glory Track (gain reward), OR  
       - If Monster defeated, gain 1 Elemental Power in Monster‚Äôs Element.  
    7. Losers: move all Units to Underworld (gain 1 Sword each).  
  - Peaceful Beginnings: no battles until all players have Regrouped once.  
- **Dwellings**:  
  - Convert Worker in Elemental Realm to Dwelling via Mill or Starter Card.  
  - Pay cost shown on Realm.  
  - Score VP: 2 per adjacent Ruin, 2 per adjacent Dwelling (any player).  
  - Gain Elemental Power: 1 or 2 spaces on Realm‚Äôs Element track.  
  - Dwellings provide +1 battle die (+2 if Castle).  
- **Free Actions (unlimited on your turn)**:  
  - Discard Treasure Token: gain shown resources (including Magic Card if ü§è).  
  - Slot Treasure Token: place on Tableau card Slot (replace existing token).  
  - Play Magic Card: as specified on card (some on other players‚Äô turns).  
  - Perform Orb Action:  
    - Place on Orb Reward Track (gain benefit, 1 VP at game end).  
    - Slot on Tableau input (counts as 0 cost).  
    - Empower Tableau card (if Orb icon).  
    - Hire Mercenary permanently (if Mercenary has Orb cost).  
  - Hire Mercenary: 1 Gold (temporary) or 1 Orb (permanent).  
  - Restore exhausted Tactics Token.  
  - Pay 3 Swords to flip exhausted Tactics Token.

6. Scoring & End Game  
- **Endgame Triggered When**:  
  - Any player builds 6th Dwelling, OR  
  - Last Realm tile is drawn.  
- All players take 1 more turn (including triggerer).  

**Final Scoring**:  
1. **Orbs**: 1 VP per unspent Orb.  
2. **Elemental Power Track**: VP equal to value beside each marker on track.  
3. **Dwellings**:  
   - Each Dwelling scores VP equal to value on its Realm‚Äôs Elemental Power Track.  
4. **Tableau Cards**:  
   - Each Adventure Card scores VP equal to value on its Element‚Äôs Power Track.  
   - Maximum cards scored = 3 √ó number of Dwellings built.  
   - Starter Card always scores.  
5. **Vault Cards**: Score VP as specified on card (e.g., based on slotted tokens or resources).  
6. **Prophecy Cards**: Score VP as specified on card (revealed in score order).  

Tiebreaker: highest number of resources (including Magic Cards). Still tied? Share victory.

7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- Q: Can I use Dungeon if no tiles left?  
  A: Yes, skip Step 1 (placing new tile).  
- Q: Is Starter Card an Adventure Card?  
  A: No. It scores separately; Adventure card limit based on Dwellings excludes Starter.  
- Q: Can Units coexist without battle?  
  A: Yes, during Peaceful Beginnings, or via spells (Faerie Charm), abilities, or Tactics Token.  
- Q: Can Dominated Monster and Monster occupy same Realm?  
  A: Yes. Dominated Monsters are Units.  
- Q: Can Mercenary or Dominated Monster use card actions?  
  A: Yes, if action allows Worker or matching Unit type.  
- Q: Can I move a slotted Aura?  
  A: Yes, by paying its cost again.  
- Q: Can I spend Magic Cards as any resource?  
  A: Only when cost shows Treasure Chest (any resource); not as wild otherwise.  
- Q: Can Gold be used for Treasure Chest?  
  A: Yes. Gold is wild for any resource cost.
