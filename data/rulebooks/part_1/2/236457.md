### 1. Lore & Objective
In *Architects of the West Kingdom*, players are royal architects in the Carolingian Empire competing to earn the king’s favor by constructing buildings and advancing work on the Cathedral. The goal is to accumulate the most Victory Points (VP) by the end of the game through construction, influence, and moral standing.

### 2. Components
- Main Board
- 100 Workers (20 per player in 5 colors)
- 10 Player Boards (5 double-sided)
- 10 Player Markers
- 40 Building Cards
- 40 Apprentice Cards
- 10 Black Market Cards (double-sided)
- 11 Reward Cards
- 22 Debt Cards
- 35 Clay, 40 Wood, 40 Stone, 50 Silver, 25 Gold, 30 Marble
- 6 Multiplier Cards
- (Expansions add Craft Cards, Influence Board, Wonder Cards, Contribution/Consequence Deck, Artisan Meeples)

### 3. Setup
1. Place the Main Board; shuffle and place decks:
   - Black Market Cards: "Small Market" side up on left space.
   - Reward Cards: face-down pile to the right of the Cathedral (2 per player + 1).
   - Debt Cards: "unpaid" side up near Guardhouse.
   - Apprentice Cards: 8 face-up to the right of the Workshop.
   - Building Cards: face-down pile to the left of the Workshop.
2. Place 4 Silver on the Tax Stand.
3. Each player:
   - Takes a Player Board (standard or variable side), 20 Workers, and 2 Markers.
   - Places one Marker under the Cathedral and one on Virtue 7.
   - (Variable Setup): Begin with resources, Virtue, and Workers in Prison as specified by chosen board.
4. Building Draft:
   - Deal 4 Building Cards to each player.
   - Draft: Keep 1, pass 3 left; repeat until each has 3 cards.
5. Starting Player:
   - Standard: Randomly chosen; receives 3 Silver (next players receive +1 each).
   - Variable: Player with highest Virtue (tie: highest Influence) starts; no turn-order bonus.

### 4. Gameplay Flow
- Players take turns clockwise, placing one Worker per turn.
- On a turn, place a Worker on a location and resolve its action.
- Worker types:
  - Large circles: Unlimited Workers.
  - Small circles: 1 Worker only (Black Market).
  - Worker outlines (Guildhall): Permanent placement, left to right, top to bottom.
- Endgame trigger: Final Worker placed in the Guildhall.
- After trigger, all players (including triggerer) get one final turn.

### 5. Core Mechanics
#### Location Actions
- **Quarry/Forest**: Gain 1 resource per Worker at location (including current).
- **Mines**: Choose:
  - Clay: 1 + 1 per Worker.
  - Gold: 1 per 2 Workers.
- **Silversmith**: Gain 1 + 1 per Worker in Silver.
- **King’s Storehouse**: 1 action per Worker:
  - Trade 2 resources (Clay/Wood/Stone) for 1 Virtue.
  - Trade 3 resources (Wood/Stone) for 1 Marble.
  - (With Apprentices): Additional trades or effects.
- **Tax Stand**: Take all Silver; lose 2 Virtue. Cannot use if no Silver.
- **Black Market** (1 Worker per space):
  - Left: Pay 1 Silver, lose 1 Virtue → gain 2 resources (Small Market).
  - Middle: Pay 2 Silver, lose 1 Virtue → hire any face-up Apprentice OR draw 5 Building Cards, keep 1.
  - Right: Pay 3 Silver, lose 1 Virtue → gain 4 resources (Large Market).
- **Workshop**:
  - Hire Apprentice: Pay 4 Silver (2 to Tax, 2 to Supply). Hire from column ≤ # Workers at Workshop. Skip cards by placing 1 Silver per skipped card. Max 5 Apprentices.
  - Gain Building Plans: Draw 1 +1 per 2 Workers at Workshop. Max 6 Building Cards in hand.
- **Town Centre**: 1 capture per Worker:
  - Pay 1 Silver (first taxed) to capture 1 group (same color, same location).
  - 2–3 players: Up to 2 locations.
  - 4–5 players: 1 location only.
  - Captured Workers: Opponent’s go to your board; your own return to supply.
- **Guardhouse**: 1 action per Worker:
  - Send captured Workers to Prison: Gain 1 Silver per Worker.
  - Release own Workers from Prison.
  - Release own Workers from other boards: Pay 5 Silver (2 Tax) OR take 1 Debt & lose 1 Virtue.
  - Pay off 1 Debt: Pay 6 Silver (3 Tax); flip card to gain 1 Virtue.
- **Guildhall**: Place Worker permanently; choose:
  - Construct Building: Pay cost, meet Apprentice skill requirements, resolve immediate effect.
  - Advance Cathedral: Pay resource cost (1 level up), discard 1 Building Card, move marker, gain Reward Card (or 1 Virtue if none left).
  - Construct Wonder (Works of Wonder): Pay cost (reduced by resources on Wonder), gain 2 Virtue, place Wonder token on chosen location.

#### Variable Player Powers (Examples)
- **Ada**: Starts with 11 Virtue, 1 Gold, 1 extra Building Card; ignores 1 Tax on all actions.
- **Arnulf**: Starts with 3 Workers in Prison, 4 Silver, 6 Virtue, 3 Clay; counts Princess as 1 Worker for placement.
- **Caroline**: Starts with 8 Workers in Prison, 10 Silver, 3 Virtue, 1 Debt, 1 Marble; gains 1 Building Card per Black Market action.
- **Clovis**: Starts with 10 Virtue, 1 Silver, 1 Marble, 1 Clay; gains 1 Building Card when constructing any Building.
- **Emma**: Starts with 14 Workers in Prison, 11 Silver, 0 Virtue, 1 Gold, 1 Marble; gains 1 Silver and 1 Clay per Debt gained.
- **Frederick**: Starts with 7 Virtue, 4 Silver, 1 Stone; does not discard Building Card when advancing Cathedral.
- **Hugo**: Starts with 10 Workers in Prison, 12 Silver, 2 Virtue, 2 extra Building Cards; may spend 2 Silver to avoid gaining a Debt.
- **Judith**: Starts with 10 Workers in Prison, 8 Silver, 1 Virtue, 3 extra Building Cards; may discard Building Cards instead of resources when required.
- **Louis**: Starts with 13 Workers in Prison, 11 Silver, 2 Virtue, 2 Influence, 1 Gold; may spend 2 Silver to avoid losing Influence; may work on Cathedral regardless of Virtue.
- **Merovech**: Starts with 12 Workers in Prison, 0 Silver, 1 Virtue, 1 Stone, 1 Wood, 1 Clay; may discard Building Cards instead of losing Virtue; hand limit 8.
- **Rudolf**: Starts with 9 Virtue, 3 Silver, 1 Wood; pays 1 less Silver (non-Tax) when hiring Apprentices; may hire up to 6 Apprentices.
- **Therese**: Starts with 4 Workers in Prison, 5 Silver, 5 Virtue, 1 extra Building Card; treats Marble and Gold as identical.
- **Zesiro**: Starts with 10 Virtue, 2 Silver, 1 Influence, 2 Stone; gains 1 Influence when constructing Building or advancing Cathedral; may always access Black Market regardless of Virtue.

#### Black Market Reset
Triggered when:
1. All 3 Black Market spaces occupied.
2. Worker placed on left-most space of bottom two Guildhall rows.
3. 5 Resources on Contribution Card (Works of Wonder).

Steps:
1. Move all Black Market Workers to Prison.
2. Flip top Small Market Card to Large Market.
3. Resolve Street Urchin and Aristocrat effects.
4. Players with Workers at Profiteer resolve Consequence Card.
5. Player(s) with most Workers at Princess gain Consequence reward.
6. Move Contribution Card resources to matching Wonder Cards.
7. Flip top Contribution Card to Consequence side.
8. Move Profiteer and Princess to new locations.
9. Players resolve Black Market Reset-based Apprentice effects.
10. Players with 3+ Workers in Prison lose 1 Virtue.
11. Player(s) with most Workers in Prison gain 1 Debt.

### 6. Scoring & End Game
Game ends after final turn post-Guildhall trigger. Score:
1. Constructed Buildings (including end-game effects).
2. Cathedral Level.
3. Virtue Track position (VP as marked).
4. -2 VP per unpaid Debt.
5. +1 VP per Gold or Marble.
6. +1 VP per 10 Silver.
7. -1 VP per 2 Workers in Prison.
8. (Works of Wonder) VP from Wonders.
9. (Age of Artisans) VP from Adornments and Tools (1/2/4/6/9 VP for 1–5+ Tools).
Tiebreaker: Highest Virtue → most Silver → shared victory.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **No Workers at Start of Turn**: Spend turn retrieving 1 Worker (not from Guildhall, Black Market, Prison, or opponent's board).
- **Virtue Limits**:
  - ≥10 Virtue: Cannot use Black Market.
  - ≤4 Virtue: Cannot advance Cathedral.
  - <3 Virtue: Ignore 1–2 Tax per action.
  - >14 Virtue: Destroy 1 Debt per Virtue above 14.
  - <0 Virtue: Gain 1 Debt per Virtue below 0.
- **Building Requirements**: Apprentice skills must be met; Apprentices not discarded.
- **Hand Limits**: 6 Building Cards; discard excess to bottom of deck.
- **Apprentice Limits**: Max 5; may discard freely.
- **Guildhall Overflow**: Final round Workers placed beside track.
