### 1. Lore & Objective
You are a monarch seeking to expand your kingdom by acquiring unclaimed land. You compete against other monarchs to build the most prosperous Dominion by hiring minions, constructing buildings, and accumulating wealth. The goal is to amass the most victory points through cards in your deck by game end.

### 2. Components
- **500 Cards Total**:
  - **Treasure Cards**: 130 total (60 Copper, 40 Silver, 30 Gold)
  - **Victory Cards**: 48 total (24 Estates, 12 Duchies, 12 Provinces)
  - **Curse Cards**: 30
  - **Blank Cards**: 7
  - **Randomizer Cards**: 32
  - **Trash Card**: 1
  - **Kingdom Cards**: 252 (24 Action cards of 10 types, including Action-Attack, Action-Reaction, and 1 Victory card type with 12 copies)

### 3. Setup
- **Player Decks**: Each player starts with 10 cards: 7 Coppers and 3 Estates. Shuffle and place face-down as a deck.
- **Draw Initial Hand**: Each player draws 5 cards.
- **Supply Piles**:
  - Place all Treasure, Victory, Curse, and Trash cards in face-up piles.
  - For 2 players: 8 Estates, 8 Duchies, 8 Provinces, 10 Curse cards.
  - For 3–4 players: 12 of each Victory card, 20–30 Curse cards.
  - Include 10 randomly or intentionally chosen Kingdom card piles with 10 cards each (8 each for Kingdom Victory cards in 2-player games).
- **Starting Player**: Chosen randomly. Play proceeds clockwise.

### 4. Gameplay Flow
Each turn has three phases in order:
1. **Action Phase**:
   - Play up to 1 Action card (unless modified by effects).
   - Resolve all effects top to bottom.
   - May play additional Actions if granted by cards.
   - Play ends when no more Actions can or are chosen to be played.
2. **Buy Phase**:
   - Spend coins from played Treasures and Action effects.
   - Buy one card (more if +Buy is available) from the Supply.
   - Pay exact cost; place purchased card in discard pile.
3. **Clean-up Phase**:
   - Discard all cards in hand and in play to discard pile (face-up).
   - Draw 5 new cards from deck.
   - If deck is empty, shuffle discard pile to form a new deck.

### 5. Core Mechanics
- **+X Cards**: Draw X cards immediately. Shuffle discard if deck is empty.
- **+X Actions**: May play X additional Action cards this turn.
- **+X Coins**: Gain X spendable coins during Buy phase.
- **+1 Buy**: Allows purchasing one additional card.
- **Discard**: Place card(s) from hand face-up on discard pile.
- **Trash**: Remove card from game by placing in Trash pile.
- **Gain**: Take a card from Supply and place in discard pile.
- **Reveal**: Show card to all players, then return it.
- **Set Aside**: Temporarily place card face-up; follow later instructions.

### 6. Scoring & End Game
- **Game End**: Ends at the end of any player’s turn when:
  - Province pile is empty, OR
  - Any 3 Supply piles are empty (4 in 5–6 player games).
- **Scoring**:
  - Count all victory points from cards in hand, deck, discard, and play.
  - Most victory points wins.
  - **Tiebreaker**: Fewest turns wins. If still tied, shared victory.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Shuffling**: Only shuffle discard pile when drawing/revealing from an empty deck.
- **Card Visibility**: Opponents can see top card of discard pile. Players may count cards in deck and Supply piles. Cannot look through discard pile.
- **Resolving Multiplayer Effects**: Resolve in turn order, starting with current player.
- **Inability to Perform All Actions**: Do as much as possible; skip unresolvable steps.
- **Duration Cards (Seaside)**: Effects last into next turn. Keep in play above main play area until resolved on next turn.
