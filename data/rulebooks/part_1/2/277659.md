## Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective  
Final Girl is a solo survival horror game where you play as the Final Girl, the last survivor facing off against a deadly Killer in a terrifying location. Your goal is to survive long enough to weaken and ultimately defeat the Killer before you‚Äîor all remaining victims‚Äîare eliminated. The game tracks escalating tension via the Horror and Bloodlust meters, and victory comes only through strategic action, resource management, and timing.

### 2. Components  
- Final Girl character board  
- Killer board (with Bloodlust/Health track, Dark Power, Finale)  
- Location board  
- Action cards (including Zero-Cost starting cards)  
- Terror deck (mixture of Location and Killer-specific cards)  
- Event cards  
- Item cards (organized in stacks)  
- Health tokens (white and black Final Health tokens)  
- Time, Horror, and Bloodlust markers  
- Dice (standard six-sided)  
- Victims (meeples or tokens)  
- Relics (in specific variants)  
- Minor and Major Curses, Papyrus cards (in specific variants)  
- Minion tokens (if applicable)  
- Mist/Wolf tokens (Dracula variant)  

### 3. Setup  
1. **Choose Killer, Location, and Final Girl.**  
2. **Place Player Boards:** Position Final Girl and Killer boards appropriately.  
3. **Starting Hand:** Take all Zero-Cost Action cards as your starting hand.  
4. **Action Cards:** Organize remaining Action cards into a shop tableau.  
5. **Finale & Dark Power:** Shuffle and randomly select one Finale and one Dark Power card; place them on the Killer board.  
6. **Terror Deck:** Shuffle Killer and Location Terror cards together; draw 10 to form the Terror deck.  
7. **Item Stacks:** Shuffle Item cards and create three face-up stacks of four cards each. Flip the top card of each pile.  
8. **Setup Card:** Shuffle and resolve one Location Setup card.  
9. **Final Health Tokens:** Randomly assign one black Final Health token to both the Killer and Final Girl; place face down.  
10. **Health Markers:** Add starting health markers to both Final Girl and Killer.  
11. **Bloodlust Marker:** Place at the bottom of the Bloodlust track.  
12. **Time Marker:** Set to "6" on the Time track.  
13. **Horror Track:** Set to starting value (varies by Killer; e.g., Dracula starts at 4).  
14. **Killer Meeple:** Place second Killer meeple on the Horror track as indicated on the Killer board.  
15. **Event Card:** Shuffle and draw one Event card; resolve its effect.  
16. **Special Rules (if applicable):**  
   - *The Mummy:* Shuffle Minor Curses; place two facedown on slots. Place remaining Minor Curses facedown on board. Link one Papyrus card to each Minor Curse slot. Place Major Curse on Finale slot. Place Relics above Item piles.  
   - *Dracula:* Shuffle 5 Wolf and 5 Mist cards separately. Set aside 3 Mist tokens. Add ‚ÄúExplore‚Äù action cards to tableau.  

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
Each round proceeds through five phases:  
1. **Action Phase** ‚Äì Player uses Action cards to move, search, attack, heal, etc.  
2. **Planning Phase** ‚Äì Spend leftover Time to buy new Action cards.  
3. **Killer Phase** ‚Äì Resolve Terror card or Finale; Killer moves and attacks.  
4. **Panic Phase** ‚Äì Victims flee if any were killed this turn.  
5. **Upkeep Phase** ‚Äì Check for Finale, perform upkeep, rearrange inventory.  

### 5. Core Mechanics  

#### Action Phase  
- Play Action cards one at a time.  
- Roll Horror dice: number = Horror track level, minimum of 1 die.  
- Success: Roll ‚â•3 or discard 2 cards to convert failure to success.  
- Actions may modify Time; if Time drops below 0, Action Phase ends immediately.  
- You may discard any number of Action cards **before** the phase ends to gain +1 Time per card.  
- Movement: You may take up to 2 Victims with you when leaving a space; Victims never follow into Killer‚Äôs space.  
- Saving Victims: In any space with an Exit symbol and Victims, you may save them (even mid-movement). Place saved Victims on your character card.  
- Searching: Gain top Item from a stack. Items have hand requirements (0, 1, or 2 hands). Track limited-use items with tokens.  
- Healing: Cannot exceed starting health.  
- Attacking: Add attack value from Action card + weapon damage (if held in hand). Range restrictions apply. Dual-wielding: choose one weapon.  
- Reaction Cards: May be played **in reaction** to an attack. Each Reaction card defends against one attack. Cannot protect Victims or defend against ‚Äúloss of health‚Äù icons (e.g., from ùüò).  

#### Planning Phase  
- Spend unspent Time to purchase Action cards (cost shown on card).  
- 0-cost cards are free.  
- Cannot purchase cards if Time would drop below 0.  
- Cards played or discarded this turn are **not available** until next Planning Phase.  
- Hand limit: 10 cards. Cannot voluntarily discard outside Action Phase.  
- After purchasing: Reset Time to 6. Return played/discarded cards to shop.  

#### Killer Phase  
- **Finale Check:** If no Terror cards remain, reveal Finale card.  
- **Draw Terror Card** (unless Finale is active).  
- **Killer Movement & Attack:**  
  - Targets Victims over Final Girl if both present.  
  - Ties broken by most Victims in space.  
  - Moves shortest path; attacks number of times = knife icons at current Bloodlust level.  
  - Each attack deals damage = value in knife column.  
  - Victims have 1 health; excess damage lost.  
  - Each Victim killed increases Bloodlust by 1.  
  - If Bloodlust reaches Dark Power row, reveal Dark Power.  
  - If Bloodlust maxes, trigger top effect each additional increase.  
- **Minions (if applicable):**  
  - Act before Killer.  
  - ‚ÄúEnemies‚Äù rules apply to both Killer and Minions.  
  - Victims may follow you into Minion spaces (unless Killer also present).  
  - When attacking, hit all Minions in space; divide damage. Cannot split between Minions and Killer.  

#### Panic Phase  
Triggered if:  
- At least one Victim was killed this turn, OR  
- A card forces panic.  
- Roll a die for each Victim in Killer‚Äôs (or Minion‚Äôs) space. Move to adjacent space based on roll (per board directions). If no valid direction, remain in place.  

#### Upkeep Phase  
1. **Finale Check:** If Terror deck is empty, reveal Finale card. No further Terror draws.  
2. **Upkeep:** Resolve ongoing effects (Items, Events, etc.).  
3. **Inventory Rearrangement:** May freely move items between hands and backpack. Only allowed during this phase or when gaining a new Item.  

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
The game ends immediately when:  
- **Win:** You kill the Killer.  
- **Loss:** You die (Final Girl loses last health).  
- **Win (Special):** Killer and Final Girl die simultaneously.  

#### Final Health Tokens  
- Damage removes normal health first.  
- When last health token is hit:  
  - Flip black Final Health token:  
    - Blank = Dead.  
    - Numbered = Revive with that HP. Replace with white token and add health.  
- After revival: End current phase, continue next phase.  
- Second death (white token): Truly dead. No revival.  
- No excess damage carries over after revival.  

#### Adrenaline Rush  
- If Final Girl **or** Killer is on final health (black or white): +1 die to all Horror rolls.  
- If **both** are on final health: +2 dice.  
- Bonus lost individually when character regains normal health.  

#### Dark Powers  
- Minor Dark Powers provide temporary health to Killer.  
- Damage must remove Minor Dark Power health first.  
- Once depleted, discard the card.  

#### Events  
- Ongoing: Effects persist even after drawing new Event cards.  
- One-time effects: Discard after resolution.  
- Unresolvable effects: Ignore; do not draw replacement.  

#### Special Variants  
- **The Mummy:**  
  - Has Minor and Major Curses instead of Dark Powers/Finale.  
  - Minor Curses activate via Bloodlust or Terror; reveal effect.  
  - Use Translate action to draw linked Papyrus and learn how to break curse.  
  - Breaking a curse removes both Curse and Papyrus.  
  - Major Curse revealed when Finale would be. All active Minor Curses removed; take 2 damage per curse (no defense).  
  - Must break Major Curse before Mummy can lose final health.  
  - Relics: Found during search on relic space; needed to break Major Curse. Only carry one.  

- **Dracula:**  
  - **Mist Form:** Dracula removed from board; place mist tokens. Only draw Mist cards. Cannot take damage.  
    - ‚ÄúExplore‚Äù Action cards let you remove mist tokens.  
    - If last token removed or 2 successes on single roll: place Dracula on that space.  
    - If all 5 Mist cards resolved: place Dracula on closest mist token; remove all tokens, take 1 damage per token.  
  - **Wolf Form:** All attacks deal +1 damage. Only draw Wolf cards.  
    - When damaged, Wolf takes +1 damage. If on same space as Final Girl, panics away, then turns into Dracula.  
    - If all 5 Wolf cards resolved: deal 1 damage to all on Wolf‚Äôs space and adjacent spaces; then turn into Dracula.  
  - Shuffle used Mist/Wolf cards to form new deck when form changes.  

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Ties in targeting?** Killer targets space with most Victims.  
- **Multiple Events?** Yes, multiple ongoing Events can be active.  
- **Hand limit exceeded?** Only during Planning Phase; cannot discard voluntarily unless in Action Phase.  
- **Can Reaction cards protect Victims?** No.  
- **Can you save Victims mid-movement?** Yes.  
- **Can you use weapons from backpack?** No; must be held in hand.  
- **Game ambiguity?** Apply:  
  1. **Rule of Infinite Evil:** Worst outcome for player.  
  2. **Rule of Object Realism:** Most thematic outcome.  
  3. **Rule of Never-ending Hilarity:** Funniest outcome.
