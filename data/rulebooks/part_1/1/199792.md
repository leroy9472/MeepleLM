## Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective
Players take on the role of forest critter leaders in the valley of Everdell, competing to build the most prosperous city over the course of a year (from late Winter through Autumn). The goal is to score the most victory points (VP) by constructing buildings, meeting inhabitants, hosting events, and completing seasonal objectives before Winter arrives.

### 2. Components
- Game Board (with Ever Tree stump)
- 24 Workers (6 per player in 4 colors: hedgehogs, mice, squirrels, turtles)
- Resources: 30 twigs, 25 resin, 20 pebbles, 30 berries (unlimited; use substitutes if needed)
- Point Tokens (10x 3-point, 20x 1-point)
- 20 Occupied Tokens (closed door)
- 128 Critter & Construction Cards (base deck)
- 11 Forest Cards
- 16 Special Event Cards
- 4 Basic Event Tiles
- 1 Victory Card
- 1 8-sided die (for solo play)
- Expansion Components (if used):
  - Bellfaire: Fair Board, Flower Festival Tile, Market Tokens, Garland Award Tile
  - Pearlbrook: Lake Board, Pearls, Wonders, Adornment Cards, River Cards
  - Spirecrest: Adventure Board, Weather Cards, Discovery Decks, Map Tiles
  - Newleaf: Train Board, Train Car Tiles, Visitor Cards, Tickets, Reservation Tokens, Golden Occupy Tokens

### 3. Setup
1. Place main board with Ever Tree on stump.
2. Place resources (twigs, resin, pebbles, berries) along riverbank.
3. Place point tokens and occupied tokens beside board.
4. Shuffle Forest Cards and place:
   - 3 face-up for 2 players
   - 4 face-up for 3–4 players
5. Place 4 Basic Event Tiles along river.
6. Shuffle Special Event Cards and place 4 face-up on Ever Tree branches.
7. Shuffle main deck and place 8 cards face-up in the Meadow. Place deck inside Ever Tree.
8. Discard pile forms at bottom of board.
9. Each player:
   - Chooses a color and takes 6 workers.
   - Places 2 workers in supply.
   - Places 1 worker on Spring, 1 on Summer, 2 on Autumn of Ever Tree.
   - Draws starting hand:
     - 1st player: 5 cards
     - 2nd: 6 cards
     - 3rd: 7 cards
     - 4th: 8 cards
     - (5–6 players: 1st/2nd: 5, 3rd/4th: 6, 5th/6th: 7)
10. First player is the "most humble."

#### Expansion Setup
**Bellfaire:**
- Place Fair Board above main board.
- Place Flower Festival Tile on designated spot.
- Place 4 Market Tokens on "Gain" section.
- Place 1 random Garland Award Tile.

**Pearlbrook:**
- Place Lake Board left of main board.
- Replace 2 Wonders Covers near stump and place 4 Wonders.
- Place Pearls in Shoal.
- Place 4 River Cards (2 Citizens, 2 Locations) face-down in Lake, 1 Pearl each.
- Give each player 2 random facedown Adornment Cards.

**Spirecrest:**
- Place Adventure Board below main board.
- Place 4 Weather Cards facedown (1 per season).
- Place 3 Discovery Decks (Foothills, Peak, Ridge).
- Place (#players +1) facedown Map Tiles on each Path Section.
- Flip Winter Weather Card and Winter Map Tiles faceup.
- Place Rabbit traveler on trail start.

**Newleaf:**
- Place Train Board right of main board.
- Place 3 random cards from deck on Station.
- Place 1 random Train Car Tile on right of each Station Card.
- Place 2 random Visitor Cards faceup on platform.
- Give each player:
  - 3 Golden Occupy Tokens
  - 1 Ticket (Outbound side up)
  - 1 Reservation Token (Resource side up)

### 4. Gameplay Flow
1. Players take turns clockwise.
2. On a turn, perform exactly one action:
   a. Place 1 Worker
   b. Play 1 Card
   c. Prepare for the Next Season
3. After all players have passed in Autumn, score points.

### 5. Core Mechanics

#### Player Turn Actions
**1. Place 1 Worker**
- Deploy one worker to an available location.
- Immediately take the listed action or resources.
- Worker remains deployed until recalled via "Prepare for Season."
- Locations:
  - **Exclusive (closed circle)**: Max 1 worker total.
  - **Shared (open circle)**: Unlimited workers, including from same player.
- Types:
  - **Basic River Locations**: Gain resources/draw cards.
  - **Forest Locations**: More powerful; max 1 per player per card.
  - **Destination Cards**: In your city or opponent's city with "Open" symbol. Owner gains 1 VP if visited by opponent.
  - **Events**: Place worker to claim if requirements met and cost paid. Only one player per event.
  - **Haven**: Discard any number of cards; gain 1 resource per 2 discarded.
  - **Journey (Autumn only)**: Discard cards equal to point value; worker scores points at game end (5, 4, 3 exclusive; 2 shared).
  - **Expansion Locations**:
    - *Bellfaire Market*: Gain reward (move token down) or pay to trade (move up).
    - *Pearlbrook River Destinations*: Frog only; meet requirement, take Pearl, flip card, optionally pay to activate.
    - *Newleaf Train Station*: Discard faceup Visitor, reveal new, take one of three options.
    - *Newleaf Knoll*: Discard 3 Meadow/Station cards, replenish all, draw 3, gain Train Car rewards.

**2. Play 1 Card**
- Play one card from hand or Meadow.
- Pay cost in resources (twigs, resin, pebbles, berries).
- Place card in city tableau.
- Cards:
  - **Critters**: Cost berries or play free if matching Construction in city (place Occupied Token; one-time use).
  - **Constructions**: Cost twigs/resin/pebbles.
  - **Unique Cards**: Only one copy allowed per player.
  - **Common Cards**: Multiple copies allowed.
- Timing of Play:
  1. Use card-playing abilities (Dungeon, Judge, Innkeeper, etc.).
  2. Pay resources or use Occupied Token.
  3. Replenish Meadow (if card from Meadow).
  4. Place new card in city.
  5. Resolve new card’s effect.
  6. Resolve triggered effects (Courthouse, Shopkeeper, etc.).

**3. Prepare for the Next Season**
- May only be done if all workers are deployed.
- Recall all deployed workers.
- Gain season-specific rewards:
  - **Spring**: Gain 1 worker, activate all Green Production cards.
  - **Summer**: Gain 1 worker, draw up to 2 Meadow cards.
  - **Autumn**: Gain 2 workers, activate all Green Production cards.
- Players may be in different seasons.
- After Autumn, player may pass.

#### Card Types
- **Tan Traveler (Knapsack)**: Activates once immediately when played.
- **Green Production (Sprout)**: Activates immediately when played, and during Spring/Autumn Prepare for Season.
- **Red Destination (Paw)**: Activates when worker is placed. "Open" allows opponent use (owner gains 1 VP).
- **Blue Governance (Scroll)**: Grants bonuses after playing certain card types or discounts.
- **Purple Prosperity (Flower)**: Worth base VP + bonus VP at game end.

#### Occupied Tokens
- When a Critter is played for free via its matching Construction, place an Occupied Token on the Construction.
- Tokens are permanent; not removed if Critter is discarded.
- One free Critter per Construction.

#### Expansions
**Pearlbrook:**
- Frog ambassador visits only Frog-marked locations.
- Play Adornment Card: Pay 1 Pearl to flip face-up (immediate + end-game effect).
- Pearls = 2 VP at end.
- Wonders: Place worker, meet requirement, pay cost (no discounts).

**Spirecrest:**
- Rabbit traveler moves each Prepare for Season.
- Take 1 Chart Tile for Expedition.
- Reveal 3 Discovery Cards; purchase 1 (first free).
- Weather Cards affect only current season.
- Expedition: In Autumn, move Rabbit along path, pay costs for tiles traveled.

**Newleaf:**
- Play Station Card: Get adjacent Train Car reward.
- Reserve Card: Place Meadow/Station card under Reservation Token (once per season); later play with 1-resource discount.
- Use Ticket: Flip to move deployed worker to new location.
- Golden Occupy Tokens required for Newleaf Constructions hosting any critter.

### 6. Scoring & End Game
- Game ends when all players have passed after Autumn.
- Players may finish on different turns.
- Score:
  - **Base Game**:
    - Cards in city (base VP)
    - Purple Prosperity cards (bonus VP)
    - Point tokens (on cards/in supply)
    - Events (VP listed)
    - Journey points (from workers)
  - **Pearlbrook**: 1 Pearl = 2 VP
  - **Spirecrest**: Expedition Path (sum of paid tiles), Discovery Cards
  - **Bellfaire**: Garland Awards
  - **Newleaf**: Visitor cards
- Hand cards and unused resources worth 0 VP.
- **Tiebreaker**: Most Events > Most leftover resources.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Hand Limit**: Strictly 8. Cannot draw beyond 8. If giving cards and all opponents full, discard.
- **Card Playing**: Cannot combine card-playing abilities (e.g., Judge + Innkeeper).
- **City Limit**: 15 spaces. Cannot discard to make room; only via card effects (Ruins, University, etc.).
- **Events**: Once claimed, keep even if requirement cards are later removed.
- **Passed Players**: Workers remain on board. Opponents may use "Open" Destination cards.
- **Legendary Cards**: Do not count toward 15-card limit or hand limit. Replace basic version for free. Not considered Unique/Common for scoring.
- **Rugwort (Solo)**: Gains card after each player card play. May exceed 15 cards. Blocks Meadow cards he occupies.
