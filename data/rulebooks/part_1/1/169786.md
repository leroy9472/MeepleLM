## Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective  
Scythe is a 4X (eXplore, eXpand, eXploit, eXterminate) engine-building game set in an alternate-history 1920s Europe. Players control factions vying for dominance through economic strength, technological advancement, and military power. The goal is to amass the greatest fortune by the end of the game.

**Victory Condition**: The game ends immediately when any player places their 6th star. The winner is the player with the most coins. In case of a tie, tiebreakers are applied in order.

---

### 2. Components  
- Game board with territories, tunnels, rivers, lakes, and factory
- 11 Encounter tokens
- Encounter deck (purple cards)
- Combat deck (yellow cards)
- Factory cards (number = players + 1)
- Structure Bonus tile (randomly selected)
- Faction mats (6 standard + 2 expansion)
- Player mats (6 standard + 2 expansion)
- Plastic units: Characters, Mechs (4 per faction), Workers
- Resource tokens: Wood, Oil, Food, Metal
- Coins
- Power, Popularity, and Triumph tracks
- Action tokens
- Technology cubes
- Recruit tokens (cylinders)
- Star tokens
- Expansion modules:  
  - *Invaders from Afar*: Adds factions Albion and Togawa Shogunate  
  - *The Wind Gambit*: Adds Airships and Resolution tiles

---

### 3. Setup  
**Global Setup**:  
- Place board.  
- Place 1 Encounter token on each of the 11 encounter-marked hexes.  
- Shuffle and place Combat deck face-down.  
- Shuffle Factory cards; place (P+1) face-down (P = number of players).  
- Shuffle and place Encounter and Objective decks.  
- Randomly select 1 Structure Bonus tile; place face-up at bottom of Popularity track.  

**Player Setup**:  
- Each player randomly draws 1 Faction mat and 1 Player mat.  
- Sit by home base.  
- Place:  
  - Character on home base  
  - 1 Worker on each territory adjacent to home base  
  - Power token on Power track (per Faction mat)  
  - Popularity token on Popularity track (per Player mat)  
  - Starting coins (per Player mat)  
  - Draw starting Objective cards (per Player mat)  
- Place 6 technology cubes on green boxes (with black square).  
- Place 4 structure tokens and 4 recruit tokens on Player mat.  
- Place 6 stars and 4 mechs nearby.  
- First player: lowest number on Player mat. In case of expansion, order is 1, 2, 2a, 3, 3a, etc.

**Banned Combos**:  
- Rusviet / Industrial  
- Crimea / Patriotic  

**Expansion Setup**:  
- *Invaders from Afar*: Use updated abilities for Crimea (Wayfare → Move to any unoccupied farm) and Polania (Meander → pick 2 encounter options; gain $3 per controlled encounter hex at endgame) in 6+ player games.  
- *The Wind Gambit*:  
  - Shuffle and reveal 1 Aggressive and 1 Passive airship tile.  
  - Each player places Airship on home base.  
  - Draw 1 Resolution tile (if using module); read aloud and place near board.

---

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
1. **Turn Order**: Clockwise, starting with lowest-numbered Player mat.  
2. **Turn Structure**:  
   a. Place Action token on a **different** section of Player mat than last turn.  
   b. Take **top-row action** (optional).  
   c. Take **bottom-row action** (optional).  
   d. May complete **Objective card** before or after top/bottom actions.  
3. **Actions are optional**, but Action token must move.  
4. **Resources** used must come from hexes you control.  
5. **Combat** occurs after all movement, before bottom-row action.  
6. **Encounters** resolved after combat if character remains in hex.

---

### 5. Core Mechanics  

#### Top-Row Actions  
- **Move**:  
  - Move up to 2 units 1 territory each, **or** gain $1.  
  - Units may pick up/drop off resources during move.  
  - Mechs may carry any number of workers and resources.  
  - Movement ends when:  
    - Entering hex with enemy unit(s)  
    - Triggering an Encounter  
    - Gaining a Factory card  
  - Workers cannot move into hex with enemy unit(s).  
  - Rivers/lakes block movement unless ability allows.  
  - Tunnels are adjacent to each other.  

- **Bolster**: Pay $1 to gain:  
  - +1 Power per  
  - or draw 1 Combat card per  

- **Trade**: Pay $1 to gain:  
  - Any 2 resources (place on controlled hex with worker)  
  - or +1 Popularity  

- **Produce**: Pay cost (sum of exposed red boxes), choose up to 2 controlled hexes with workers. Each worker produces 1 resource (type by terrain). Mill counts as a worker and produces. Worker production places leftmost worker from Player mat onto village.

#### Bottom-Row Actions  
Must **announce** action for Enlist bonuses.  

- **Upgrade**: Pay cost, move tech cube from green box to red bracketed box. Gain $1.  
- **Deploy**: Pay cost, place mech on controlled hex with at least 1 worker. Gain $1. Unlocks mech ability for character and all mechs.  
- **Build**: Pay cost, place structure on controlled hex with at least 1 worker. Gain $1.  
  - Max 1 structure per hex.  
  - Structure abilities:  
    - Monument: +1 Popularity when Bolstering  
    - Mill: Produces as 1 worker during Produce  
    - Mine: Acts as tunnel (only for you)  
    - Armory: +1 Power when Trading  
- **Enlist**: Pay cost, place recruit token on Faction mat. Gain one-time bonus (2 Power, 2 Coins, 2 Popularity, or 2 Combat cards). Gain **ongoing bonus** when you or adjacent player takes same bottom-row action.

#### Factory  
- First time character ends move on Factory hex:  
  - Choose 1 Factory card from face-down pile.  
  - Place next to Player mat as 5th section.  
  - Bottom action: Move 1 unit up to 2 spaces.  
  - Can only have 1 Factory card.  
  - Rusviet’s Relentless does **not** allow repeating Factory card action.

#### Combat  
- Triggered when character/mech shares hex with opponent’s character/mech after movement.  
- Active player chooses order if multiple combats.  
- **Mech abilities** trigger: Attacker first, then defender.  
- **Combat Steps**:  
  1. **Secretly Set**:  
     - Set Power Dial (≤ Power track, max 7)  
     - Tuck up to 1 Combat card per combat unit (character/mech) behind dial  
  2. **Reveal**:  
     - Total Power = Dial + Combat card values  
     - Highest total wins; attacker wins ties  
  3. **Resolve**:  
     - Winner: Gains control, places 1 star (max 2 from combat), resolves Encounter if present  
     - Attacker loses 1 Popularity per retreating enemy worker  
     - Loser: Retreats all units to home base, leaves carried resources  
     - Loser gains 1 Combat card if ≥1 Power was revealed  

#### Encounters  
- Triggered when character ends movement in hex with Encounter token.  
- Resolve after all combats.  
- Choose 1 option, pay cost if required.  
- Benefits optional (may take partial).  
- Gained units/structures placed in same hex.  
- Polania (Meander): May pick up to 2 options; 1st benefit may pay cost of 2nd.

#### Movement Details  
- **Tunnels**: All tunnel hexes adjacent.  
- **Rivers/Lakes**: Normally block movement; abilities allow crossing.  
- **Riverwalk**: Allows crossing rivers into specific terrain types.  
- **Mine**: Acts as tunnel for owner.  
- **Control**: Hex controlled if:  
  - You have ≥1 unit (character, mech, worker)  
  - Or you have structure and no enemy units  
- **Max 1 structure per hex**.  
- **Resources** can be carried by any unit.  

#### Expansion Rules  

**Invaders from Afar**:  
- **Albion**:  
  - Exalt: After movement (post-combat/encounter), may place Flag token.  
  - Each Flag on non-home-adjacent controlled hex = +1 territory at scoring.  
- **Togawa**:  
  - Maifuku: After movement, may place armed Trap token.  
  - Armed Trap = +1 territory at scoring (grants control).  
  - Enemy entering hex with armed Trap:  
    - Movement ends  
    - Trigger penalty: Lose 2 Popularity, 3 Power, $4, or discard 2 random Combat cards  
    - Trap becomes disarmed  
  - Opponent may build on Trap; structure controls hex at endgame if only tokens present.  

**The Wind Gambit**:  
- **Airships**:  
  - Place on home base.  
  - Range = number on Passive tile (up to 3).  
  - Factory card move +1 range.  
  - Movement:  
    - Ignores rivers/lakes  
    - Does not end in enemy hex  
    - Cannot control hex or trigger combat (unless Blitzkrieg)  
    - Cannot enter home base or move through tunnels/mines  
  - **Transport**:  
    - Aggressive tile determines: carry 3 resources **or** 2 workers  
    - Resources on airship count for spending and scoring  
    - Workers on airship do not contribute to control or production  
    - Workers can only be dropped on unoccupied hexes  
  - **Combat**:  
    - Cannot initiate or be targeted unless Blitzkrieg  
    - Does not increase combat card limit  
- **Resolution Module**:  
  - Game ends per Resolution tile, not 6th star  
  - Multiple players may reach 6 stars

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
**End Game Trigger**:  
- Standard: Any player places 6th star.  
- Wind Gambit (Resolution): Per tile condition.  

**Scoring**:  
Total coins from:  
- Coins in hand  
- Stars placed (based on Popularity level)  
- Territories controlled (Factory = 3)  
- Every 2 resources controlled  
- Structure Bonus tile achievements  

**Tiebreakers** (in order):  
1. # Workers, Mechs, Structures  
2. Power  
3. Popularity  
4. # Resources controlled  
5. # Territories controlled  
6. # Stars placed  

**Expansion Scoring**:  
- **Albion**: Each Flag on non-home-adjacent controlled hex = +1 territory  
- **Togawa**: Each armed Trap = +1 territory

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Objective Completion**: May only complete 1 objective (except Saxon). Reveal during turn, before/after actions.  
- **Benefits Optional**: May decline full or partial benefits from actions or encounters.  
- **6th Star from Bottom-Row Action**: Gain benefit, coin, and Enlist bonus **before** placing star.  
- **6th Star Mid-Combat**: If star is placed but combat unresolved, units return to origin.  
- **Combat Card Count**: Public info. Number played = number of combat units (unless ability).  
- **Airships & Control**: Do not control territory unless Safe Haven (not in base rules).  
- **Flag/Trap Placement**: After character movement ends, post-combat/encounter. Max 1 per hex.  
- **Structure Bonus**: Count features (lakes, tunnels, encounters) only once per structure.  
- **Bribes/Alliances**: Informal agreements allowed; only coins may be exchanged. Not binding.
