### 1. Lore & Objective
Players lead expeditions to explore the uncharted island of Arnak, uncovering its secrets and researching the ruins of a vanished civilization. The goal is to score the most victory points (VP) over 5 rounds by discovering sites, overcoming guardians, conducting research, and collecting idols and artifacts.

### 2. Components
- Main board (Bird Temple / Snake Temple sides)
- Supply board
- 40 Item Cards, 35 Artifact Cards, 19 Fear Cards
- 4 Player Boards, 2 Archaeologist Figures per player
- 1 Notebook Token and 1 Magnifying Glass Token per player
- 15 Guardian Tiles, 16 Idol Tiles (hexagonal)
- 10 Level I Site Tiles, 6 Level II Site Tiles
- 12 Assistant Tiles
- Moon Staff, Starting Player Marker
- Resource Tokens: Coins, Compasses, Tablets, Arrowheads, Jewels
- 24 Temple Tiles (Bronze 2VP, Silver 6VP, Gold 11VP)
- 18 Research Bonus Tiles
- Blocking Tiles (5)
- Scoring Sheets

### 3. Setup
1. Place main board with Bird Temple side up (recommended for first game).
2. Set up card row:
   - Shuffle Artifact deck, place face down top-left.
   - Place Fear Cards face up top-middle.
   - Shuffle Item deck, place face down top-right.
   - Place Moon Staff between first Artifact and Item slots (Round I).
   - Deal 1 Artifact face up to the left of Moon Staff.
   - Deal 5 Items face up to the right of Moon Staff.
3. Place Idols:
   - Randomly place 1 faceup Idol on each Level I Site.
   - Randomly place 1 faceup and 1 facedown Idol on each Level II Site.
4. Blocking Tiles:
   - 4 players: Do not use.
   - 3 players: Randomly block 3 "double boots" spaces.
   - 2 players: Block all 5 "double boots" spaces.
5. Temple Tiles:
   - Create stacks: 1 Gold (11VP), 2 Silver (6VP), 3 Bronze (2VP).
   - Each stack has as many tiles as players.
6. Research Track:
   - Place face-down stack of Research Bonus Tiles (number = players) at Lost Temple.
   - Place 1 faceup Research Bonus Tile on each eligible space (check player count indicators).
7. Supply Board:
   - Place all Resources.
   - Shuffle Level I Site Tiles face down.
   - Shuffle Guardian Tiles face down.
   - Shuffle Level II Site Tiles face down.
   - Shuffle Assistant Tiles silver side up into 3 random stacks of 4.
8. Player Setup:
   - Each player chooses a color and takes:
     - Player Board
     - 2 Archaeologists (place on tent)
     - Notebook Token (place on bottom of Research Track)
     - Magnifying Glass Token (place on top of Notebook)
     - 4 Basic Cards + 2 Fear Cards → shuffle into 6-card deck
     - Starting Resources:
       - Player 1: 2 Coins
       - Player 2: 1 Coin, 1 Compass
       - Player 3: 2 Coins, 1 Compass
       - Player 4: 1 Coin, 2 Compasses
9. Randomly select starting player (e.g., most recent traveler). Give Starting Player Marker.

### 4. Gameplay Flow
The game lasts 5 rounds. Each round:
1. **Draw**: All players draw to 5 cards.
2. **Take Turns**: Starting with Starting Player, players take turns clockwise. On your turn:
   - Perform 1 Main Action.
   - Perform any number of Free Actions (before, during, or after main action).
3. **Pass**: You may Pass (counts as main action). Once all players pass, proceed to next step.
4. **Setup for Next Round**:
   - Return all Archaeologists to player boards.
   - Gain 1 Fear Card for each Archaeologist returning from a site with a Guardian.
   - Choose: Keep cards in hand or discard to play area.
   - Shuffle all cards in play area and place under deck.
   - Refresh all Assistants (turn upright).
   - Exile cards adjacent to Moon Staff:
     - Blue Moon Staff: 1 Artifact (left) and 1 Item (right).
     - Red Moon Staff: 2 Artifacts (left) and 2 Items (right).
   - Move Moon Staff one space right.
   - Slide cards toward Moon Staff and refill gaps.
   - Pass Starting Player Marker left.
5. Repeat until Round 5 ends.

### 5. Core Mechanics
#### Main Actions (Choose 1 per turn)
- **Dig at a Site**:
  1. Pay travel cost (discard card with matching icon(s) or 2 Coins = Plane).
  2. Move 1 Archaeologist from your board to an unoccupied space at a discovered site.
  3. Resolve site effect (gain resources, etc.).
- **Discover a New Site**:
  1. Pay discovery cost: 3 Compasses (Level I) or 6 Compasses (Level II).
  2. Pay travel cost to site.
  3. Move 1 Archaeologist to site.
  4. Take all Idols (faceup effect only; facedown kept).
  5. Place top Site Tile (matching level) on site, resolve effect.
  6. Place top Guardian Tile face up on site.
- **Overcome a Guardian**:
  1. Must have Archaeologist at site with Guardian.
  2. Pay cost on bottom of Guardian Tile.
  3. Take Guardian Tile to your player area (face up).
  4. May use Boon (top-right) once per game as Free Action (then flip facedown).
- **Buy a Card**:
  - **Artifact** (left of Moon Staff):
    1. Pay Compass cost.
    2. Move to play area.
    3. May resolve effect immediately, ignoring Tablet cost.
  - **Item** (right of Moon Staff):
    1. Pay Coin cost.
    2. Place facedown under your deck.
  - Refill card row after purchase: slide cards toward Moon Staff, draw new card.
- **Play a Card**:
  1. Move card from hand to play area.
  2. Resolve effect.
  - **Free Action Cards** (⚡): Do not count as main action.
  - **Main Action Cards**: Require main action.
  - **Artifacts**: Must pay Tablet cost (and any other costs) when played from hand.
- **Research**:
  1. Choose one of your tokens (Notebook or Magnifying Glass) to move.
  2. Move up one row to a connected space (Notebook cannot be above Magnifying Glass).
  3. Pay cost between spaces.
  4. Gain:
     - Research Bonus Tile effect (if present, first player only).
     - Row effect (based on which token moved).
  - **Lost Temple**: When Magnifying Glass reaches top row:
    - Place on highest available VP space.
    - Take 1 Bonus Tile from stack (look and choose).
    - Notebook cannot enter.
    - Subsequent Research actions: Pay costs to take 1 Temple Tile (Bronze, Silver, or Gold).
- **Pass**: End your participation for the round. You may still take Free Actions on this turn.

#### Free Actions (Unlimited per turn)
- Play a card with ⚡ icon.
- Move an Idol to an Idol Slot (choose 1 of 5 effects; slot cannot be reused).
- Use a Guardian's Boon (flip tile facedown after use).
- Use an Assistant (exhaust by turning sideways; refresh at round end).
- Exile a card from hand or play area.

#### Travel Costs & Hierarchy
- Icons: Boot, Car, Boat, Plane.
- Hierarchy:
  - Plane pays for any single icon.
  - Car or Boat pays for itself or Boot.
  - Boot pays for Boot.
  - 2 Coins = 1 Plane.
- Cards with two icons can pay two costs or one double cost.
- Excess icons do not carry over.

#### Card Management
- Cards used for effect go to play area.
- Cards used for travel value go to play area (ignore effect).
- At round end: Shuffle play area into bottom of deck.
- Cards in hand: May keep or discard to play area before shuffling.
- Fear Cards: Have travel value (Boot) but no effect.

### 6. Scoring & End Game
After Round 5:
- Return Archaeologists (gain Fear if from Guardian sites).
- Skip other cleanup steps.
- **Final Scoring**:
  - **Research Tokens**: Score VP based on row (Magnifying Glass position breaks ties).
  - **Temple Tiles**: Score VP shown.
  - **Idols**: 3 VP each (regardless of slot).
  - **Empty Idol Slots**: Score VP shown on slot.
  - **Guardians Overcome**: 5 VP each.
  - **Cards**: Score VP in bottom-right corner.
  - **Fear**: -1 VP per Fear Card, -2 VP per Fear Tile.
- **Winning**: Most VP wins. Tiebreakers:
  1. First to reach Lost Temple.
  2. Highest Research score.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Hand Size**: Can exceed 5; only draw to 5 at round start.
- **Card Limits**: No discard pile; play area shuffled under deck at round end.
- **Deck Empty**: Cannot draw; cards remain in play area until round end.
- **Exiling**: Cards sent to exile (special pile); Fear Cards return to deck.
- **Assistant Stacks**: Hidden; only top tile visible.
- **Upgrading Assistants**: Flip to gold side; refreshes even if used.
- **Idol Slots**: Effects are one-time; empty slots score VP.
- **Travel Costs**: Must be paid every time an archaeologist moves.
- **Overcoming Guardians**: Prevents Fear at round end.
- **Lost Temple Access**: Magnifying Glass must reach first; Notebook follows.
