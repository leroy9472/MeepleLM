### 1. Lore & Objective
In *Lisboa*, players take on the role of influential nobles rebuilding the city after the 1755 earthquake. The goal is to gain the most Wigs (victory points) by constructing stores, opening public buildings, influencing the court, and fulfilling decrees. Victory is determined by strategic use of resources, political maneuvering, and economic development.

### 2. Components

### 3. Setup
1. **Each player chooses a color and takes:**
   - 1 Player Board
   - 1 Player Aid Book
   - 5 Rubble Set Markers (place on board)
   - 8 Houses (place on board)
   - 8 Officials (place 7 on board, 1 in Blue Noble’s Office)
   - 1 Good of each type (Gold, Books, Cloth, Tools) → place in Warehouse
   - 10 Réis
   - 2 random Clergy Tiles → keep 1, return 1 to bag
   - 1 random Royal Favour Tile → place on board
   - 1 random dark blue-backed Starting Plan Tile → place on board
   - 1 Courtier Meeple → place on any Royal Court space
   - 1 Wigs Marker → place on space 5 of Wigs Track
   - 1 Influence Disk
   - 5 random Political Cards from Blue Deck

2. **Turn order:**
   - Starting Player receives Starting Player Tile.
   - Place Influence Discs: 1st player on 4, 2nd on 5, 3rd on 6, 4th on 7.

3. **Game board setup:**
   - Public Buildings: Shuffle 16 tiles (8 green-side up, 8 blue-side up). Place stacks beside respective architects. Place top tile from each stack on architect’s plan space.
   - Plans: Sort green and blue Plan stacks by number of Officials (fewest on top), place on board.
   - Market: Place 4 Price Markers on starting spaces.
   - City Display: Place 1 City Tile on each colored space (yellow, pink, brown, blue, small blue). Stack rest nearby.
   - Downtown: Place 1 Scoring Tile randomly at bottom of each column.
   - Church: Draw 6 Clergy Tiles, place face-up on dark spaces of Church Track. Place Cardinal Meeple on Influence Icon.
   - Nobles: Stack remaining Royal Favour Tiles below each Noble's portrait.
   - Treasury: Place Treasury Marker on space 3.
   - Political Cards: Distribute Red Political Cards face-down to 4 spaces. Flip top card of each deck face-up.
   - Ships: Stack Ship Cards (Blue on top of Red) based on player count:
     - 2 players: 1 of each
     - 3 players: 2 of each
     - 4 players: 3 of each
   - Decrees: Place Decree Deck face-down. Reveal 8 face-up.
   - Rubble: Place random Rubble Cubes:
     - One per column bottom and row end (skip row E in 2-player)
     - Two per Public Building Site (skip row E in 2-player)
     - 6 in Rubble Pile
   - 2-player only: Cover row E with overlay. Place neutral Official in each Noble’s Office.

### 4. Gameplay Flow
A game of *Lisboa* is played over **2 periods**, each ending under specific conditions.

**Player Turn Order:**
1. **Dock Ships:** Return goods from any "At Sea" ships (full cargo, face-down) to supply.
2. **Take Action or Get Gold:**
   - Discard 1 card → gain 1 Gold Good (alternative action)
   - OR Play 1 card from hand to perform 1 of 4 Main Actions.
3. **Take a Political Card:** Take 1 face-up card from display to refill hand to 5.
4. **Check Church Scoring:** If Cardinal passed Influence Icon, perform Church Scoring.
5. **Discard Goods:** Remove goods from State Action spaces.
6. **Refill:**
   - City Tile Display
   - Clergy Tiles (if any spaces empty)
   - Decree Display (to 8 cards)

**Periods:**
- **End of First Period:** Triggered when any player completes 2 Rubble Sets **or** 3 Political Card decks are exhausted. Ends after that player’s turn.
- **End of Game:** Triggered when any player completes 4 Rubble Sets **or** 3 Political Card decks are exhausted again. Ends after full round + 1 extra turn per player.

### 5. Core Mechanics
#### Portfolio & Warehouse Limits
- Portfolio holds 2 cards initially. +1 card per completed Rubble Set (max 5).
- Warehouse holds 2 of each Good initially. +1 per completed Rubble Set (max 5 per type).
- When completing a Rubble Set (1 Brown, 1 Red, 1 Blue), move one Rubble Set Marker to Marquis portrait.

#### Influence
- Gain Influence by:
  - Building Ships
  - Church Scoring
  - Card effects
- Spend Influence to:
  - Visit a Noble
  - Pay Réis (1 Influence = 1 Real, move marker left to next Real space)
- At 10 Influence: gain 1 Wig (even if already at 10). Reset to 10 if exceeded.

#### Main Actions
**1. Play a Noble Card into Portfolio**
- Tuck card into top slot.
- Resolve reward or penalty.
- Then: **Sell Goods** OR **Trade with Nobles**.

**2. Play a Treasury Card into Portfolio**
- Tuck into bottom slot.
- Gain Réis = current Treasury value.
- Move Treasury Marker down 1.
- Then: **Sell Goods** OR **Trade with Nobles**.

**3. Visit a Noble**
- Play Noble Card to Royal Court.
- Place Courtier on card.
- Spend Influence = Treasury Influence value + # of opponents’ Officials in that Noble’s Office.
- May take 1 State Action (no good cost).
- **Must** take Noble Action.
- Other players may **Follow a Visit** by:
  - Returning matching Royal Favour
  - Paying same Influence cost
  - Taking 1 of the 3 available actions (State or Noble)

**4. Sponsor an Event**
- Play Treasury Card to Royal Court.
- Pay Réis = current Treasury value.
- Take central action/reward on card.

#### State Actions (Trade with Nobles)
Pay 1 Good (or Gold) to activate:
- **Recruit State Officials:** Place up to 2 Officials in different Offices. If full, displace most dominant color(s).
- **Acquire a Plan:** Take top Plan from either architect.
- **Build a Ship:** Pay goods = hull size. Move Treasury Marker up. Gain Influence.
- **Produce Goods:** Gain 1 Good per Store (type = street). Each produced type drops 1 Real in price.
- **Meet the Cardinal:** Move Cardinal 1–2 spaces. Take adjacent Clergy Tile. May trigger Church Scoring or Treasury increase.
- **Get a Royal Favour:** Take 1 tile you don’t already have.

#### Noble Actions
- **Build a Store (Builder):**
  - Select City Tile from display.
  - Place on matching-color street.
  - Take reward from land.
  - Take 1 Rubble from row/column.
  - Pay land cost = Treasury value + total rubble cost in row/column.
  - Place House from player board.
  - Score Wigs = Scoring Tile value × number of matching-color Public Buildings in row/street.

- **Take a Decree (Minister):**
  - Take 1 Decree Card.
  - May take 2nd by discarding 1 Rubble Set Marker from Marquis.

- **Open a Public Building (King):**
  - Choose site (West, North, East).
  - Take both Rubble Cubes.
  - Select matching incomplete Plan.
  - Return # of Officials shown on Plan (from Offices/Plazas).
  - Hire missing Officials at Treasury cost each.
  - Flip Plan to Completed.
  - Score adjacent Stores: owners gain Wigs = Scoring Tile value.
  - Fill empty space with next Public Building.

#### Church Scoring
Triggered when Cardinal lands on or passes Influence Icon.
- Starting with player who moved Cardinal, each player may:
  - Discard 1+ Clergy Tiles
  - Gain Wigs (back of tiles)
  - Gain Influence = total from top-row cards
- Stand Cardinal up after.

### 6. Scoring & End Game
End Game triggered by 4th Rubble Set or 3rd card deck exhaustion. After final turn, score:

1. **Ships:** +Wigs = sum of hull sizes in Portfolio.
2. **Rubble Sets:** +3 Wigs per completed set.
3. **Influence:** Cash in for Réis (no marker move). +1 Wig per 5 Réis (rounded down).
4. **Decrees:** Fulfill conditions → gain Wigs as specified.
5. **Stores:** For each Good type, compare counts:
   - 1st: 9 Wigs
   - 2nd: 6 Wigs
   - 3rd: 3 Wigs
   - 4th: 0 Wigs
   - Ties: Add ranks, divide, round down. 2-player: only 1st and 3rd scored.
6. **Completed Plans:** Compare total Officials on completed Plans:
   - 1st: 15 Wigs
   - 2nd: 10 Wigs
   - 3rd: 5 Wigs
   - 4th: 0 Wigs
   - Ties: Add ranks, divide, round down.
7. **Royal Favors:** +2 Wigs each.

**Tiebreakers:**
1. Most Rubble Sets
2. Most Stores
3. Most Completed Plans
4. Most Réis

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Portfolio Full:** Must discard a card to play new one. If discarding a Ship, return its goods.
- **No Officials to Return:** Can hire missing ones during Open Public Building.
- **Church Scoring with No Clergy:** Cannot participate.
- **Follow a Visit:** Must have matching Royal Favour. Can take any of the 3 actions.
- **Selling Goods to Opponent Ships:** Allowed. Owner gains Wigs when full.
- **House Abilities:** Unlocked bottom-up in 3 columns:
  - Left: Spend Réis instead of Influence (1:1)
  - Middle: Discount Ship costs (1 good, then free)
  - Right: Extra Goods when producing (1 type, then all types)
