## 1. Lore & Objective
Players are industrialists in the early automotive era, competing to build the most profitable car manufacturing company. The goal is to maximize wealth by selling cars to buyers in a dynamic market shaped by evolving technology and consumer demand.

**Objective:** The player with the most Wealth at the end of the game wins. Wealth is earned by selling cars. Players must develop technology, build factories, and strategically sell cars before opponents.

## 3. Setup
1. **Board Setup:**
   - Place Market Board. Attach Spec Axes to read "0 1 2 3".
   - Randomly place two Research Tracks beside the board (X and Y axes). Point Obsolescence Marker at the X-axis track.
   - Place remaining 3 Research Tracks off-board in a column.
   - Place one brand piece per player on the first tech space of active tracks; on start space (left of first tech) for off-board tracks.
   - Place a Minimum Spec Indicator (wooden stick) between start and first tech on each track.

## 5. Core Mechanics

### Technology & Access
- **Technology Access:** To build a Technology Station, you must:
  - Have your marker on or past it on its Research Track, OR
  - Access via patent sharing: If you are in position Z on Engineering Focus, you can use tech researched by the Zth player on that track.
    - 1st: any tech by anyone
    - 2nd: tech by â‰¥2 players
    - 3rd: tech by â‰¥3 players, etc.
- **Spec Indicators:** Each Technology Station must have â‰¥1 adjacent Spec Indicator pointing to it (matching type). Can add more later if accessible.

### Factory Placement Rules
- **General:**
  - Tiles must be fully on black grid.
  - Must connect to Loading Bay or another tile (orthogonal adjacency).
  - Cannot block "Keep Clear" zones.
- **Specific:**
  - **Mainline & Dealership:** Must be adjacent (mutual).
  - **Marketing Department:** Must be adjacent to Dealership.
  - **Technology Station:**
    - Must connect to Mainline via matching manufacturing step (A/B/C/D).
    - Must be unique in its manufacturing zone (connected group of same-step tiles).
    - Must have â‰¥1 Spec Indicator.
- **Floor Expansion:** Add one per round (if available), adjacent to factory, not blocking Keep Clear.

### Selling
- **Eligibility:** Dealership must meet Minimum Specs on all 5 dimensions.
- **Market Window:** Size based on Marketing Departments. Placement must not cover niches exceeding specs.
- **Selling:** Must sell all possible cars in chosen niche. Cannot combine dealerships.
- **Scoring:** Price Marker value + bonus: Truck (+$1), Sports Car (+$2).

### Time & Game End
- **Punch Clock:** Advances when:
  - Any marker lands on a punch clock space.
  - A card with punch clock icons is played.
  - When it reaches 0, flip to "FINISH".
- **Game End:** Triggered at Phase 6 if:
  - Punch Clock is flipped, OR
  - No floor expansions placed (Year 7).
- Game continues until Phase 6 even if clock hits 0 earlier.

## 6. Scoring & End Game
- **Winner:** Player with most Wealth.
- **Tiebreaker:** Among tied players, highest Sales Focus wins.

## 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Can I build the same Technology Station multiple times?**  
  Yes, but not in the same manufacturing zone (same-step group connected to same Mainline).

- **Can I add a Spec Indicator to an existing station later?**  
  Yes, if you have access to the tech and space.

- **What if two players tie in Innovation?**  
  Use rightmost player marker, then topmost track.

- **Do Neutral Buyers respect Spark limits?**  
  No. They are placed regardless.

- **Can a Market Window be rotated?**  
  No. Must align with grid (cards show orientation).

- **What if a segment has no buyers during price setting?**  
  It is skipped. Lower segments may get higher prices.

- **Can I sell fewer cars than possible?**  
  No. Must sell all possible cars in the chosen niche.

- **When does the game end exactly?**  
  At the start of Phase 6, if either end condition is met.
