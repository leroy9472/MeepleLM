### 5. Core Mechanics

#### Moving Your Character
- Use a Power Tile to move up to the number of spaces shown (max 6).
- Diagonal spaces are not adjacent.
- Movement ends if you stop to take an action.
- Use another Power Tile to continue moving.

#### Escorting George and Lorraine
- While moving, you may escort **one** of George or Lorraine.
- You may pick them up or drop them off during movement.
- Cannot escort through or into Town Square.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases

**Q: Can I escort Biff?**  
A: No. Only George or Lorraine may be escorted.

**Q: Can I pause movement to use a Power Tile or Special Power?**  
A: No. Movement ends when you stop to take an action. Use another Power Tile to continue.

**Q: Can Einstein use "Bark! Bark!" on a knocked-down Biff?**  
A: Yes. Biff remains knocked down and keeps tokens.

**Q: If Biff is with both George and Lorraine, does each â­• lower Love Meter by 2?**  
A: No. Each â­• lowers it by 1, regardless of how many are with him.

**Q: Can I use my Special Character Power to assist another player?**  
A: No. Special powers may only be used during your own turn.

**Q: Can I use Item Cards to assist?**  
A: Only **Skateboard, Radiation Suit, Sci-Fi Magazine, Video Recorder** (and in expansion: **Biffâ€™s Matchbook, Grays Sports Almanac**) can be used while assisting.

**Q: What happens if Love Meter is at max and I succeed at Influence Love?**  
A: Nothing. It cannot go above the top Heart Zone space.

**Q: Can I reroll Biff dice more than once?**  
A: Only if using a "Reroll All" Power Tile. Normally, you may reroll unlocked dice once per roll cycle.

**Q: Can I attempt multiple challenges in one turn?**  
A: Yes, if you have enough Power Tiles and are at locations with available challenges.
