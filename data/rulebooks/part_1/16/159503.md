# The Captain is Dead ‚Äì Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective  
The Captain is Dead is a cooperative survival game set aboard a crippled starship under relentless attack. The crew must work together to repair the Jump Core and escape before the ship is destroyed. Players take on unique roles with special abilities, managing systems, threats, and limited resources under escalating pressure.

**Primary Objective:** Repair the Jump Core to "Engage!" status to win.  
**Team Loses If:**  
- Shields reach 0% and take another hit.  
- Cannot place a Hostile Alien due to lack of pawns.  
- Cannot draw a new Alert card when required.  
- Red Alerts eliminate the crew before victory.

### 3. Setup  
1. Place the board in the center.  
2. Place System cards on designated spots, **Online side up** (except Upgrade cards: **Inactive side up**, near Science Lab).  
3. Shuffle Alert decks separately. Stack: **Red (bottom), Orange (middle), Yellow (top)**.  
4. Draw **top 2 Alerts** and place face-up in the queue (visible via External Scanners).  
5. Shuffle Skill deck, place on Computers space. Draw **3 face-up Skills** next to Internal Sensors.  
6. Deal **5 Skill cards** to each player. Each chooses **1 Skill** to discard into the Cargo Pod.  
7. Place **Tools** in the Cargo Pod.  
8. Shuffle **Battle Plans**, place on Captain‚Äôs Journal space.  
9. Place **5 Torpedoes** on red icons in Armory; rest off-board.  
10. Place **yellow status bar** on Shields at **100%**.  
11. Set Jump Core difficulty by placing status bar at **Novice, Normal, or Veteran**.  
12. Place **orange blocker bar** near Engineering.  
13. Assign colored pawns to players (randomly or by role). Each chooses matching-color Role card.  
14. Place pawns in room of matching color.  
15. Determine turn order by **Rank (1 = first)**. Resolve ties by birthdate if needed (Any Role variant).  
16. Draw and resolve **5 initial Alerts** (one at a time), applying damage. Discard after.  
    - Cannot override these initial Alerts.  
    - If External Scanners go offline during setup, discard face-up Alerts.

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
1. **Active Player Phase:**  
   - Spend actions (based on Role).  
   - Perform actions (repair, move, use systems, transfer cards, etc.).  
   - May use Tools, Battle Plans, or Role abilities.  
2. **End of Turn:**  
   - Draw the next Alert (from face-up queue or deck).  
   - Resolve its effects immediately.  
   - Replenish face-up Alerts if needed.  
3. **Next Player:** Proceed in rank order.  
4. **Game Continues** until win or loss condition is met.

### 5. Core Mechanics  

#### Actions  
- Most Roles have **4 actions per turn** (Cyborg has 5, Ensign has 4).  
- Actions are spent to:  
  - Repair systems  
  - Use system abilities  
  - Move (via Teleporter or normal movement)  
  - Heal  
  - Manufacture or fire Torpedoes  
  - Draw Battle Plans  
  - Transfer Skills  
  - Kill Hostile Aliens  
- Actions do not carry over.

#### Skills & Discounts  
- Skill types: **Command, Engineering, Science, Tactical**.  
- Role cards list **Skill discounts** (e.g., 2 Tactical). These reduce cost of matching actions.  
- If discounts are insufficient, discard Skill cards to cover the difference.  
- **Discounts can be used repeatedly** and never deplete.  
- **Skill discounts apply when overriding Alerts**.  

#### Hand Size  
- Varies by Role (e.g., Counselor = 8, Ensign = 4).  
- Includes **Skill and Tool cards only**.  
- **Battle Plans do not count** toward hand size.  
- If over hand size, **discard immediately** down to limit.

#### Injuries  
- Triggered by Alerts or effects.  
- **Injured pawn is tipped sideways**.  
- **Limited actions available:**  
  - Move  
  - Kill Hostile Aliens  
  - Use Teleporter  
  - Use Trauma Station  
- **Activities marked with** üõë **symbol can be used while injured**.  
- **Role abilities remain usable** when injured.  
- Heal at Trauma Station (1 action).

#### Tools  
- Found in Cargo Pod.  
- Use **Side A**, then **Side B**. After Side B, remove from game.  
- **Cannot be transferred via Comm System**.  
- **Can be transferred via Teleporter** (if online).  
- **Hologram cannot use or carry Tools**.  
- Can use Tool abilities while injured **only if the action is allowed when injured** (e.g., fire rifle ‚úÖ, repair ‚ùå).

#### Systems  
- Must be **online** to use. Flip to **Offline** when damaged.  
- **Passive systems** (e.g., External Scanners) provide automatic benefits.  
- **Active systems** require actions and/or Skills.  
- Some systems can be used **remotely** (marked with icon).  
- **Role abilities tied to systems are disabled if system is offline** (e.g., Teleporter Chief).  

#### Repairing Systems  
- Spend actions and Skills listed on **Offline side** of card.  
- Flip back to **Online**.  
- **Shields & Jump Core:** Slide yellow bar up one level per repair.  
  - Shields: 2 actions, 2 Tactical ‚Üí +10%  
  - Jump Core: 2 actions, 5 Engineering ‚Üí +1 rank  

#### Destroyed Systems  
- Only if Alert explicitly says "destroyed".  
- **Offline + damaged ‚â† destroyed**.  
- Reinstall cost: **2 actions, 2 Tactical, 4 Science**.  
- Place System card back online.

#### Battle Plans  
- Draw from Captain‚Äôs Journal: **2 actions, 1 Command**.  
- **One-time use**. Discard after use.  
- **Do not count toward hand size**.  
- **Override conflicting rules**.  
- Once deck is empty, no more can be drawn.

#### Alerts  
- Draw **1 per turn** (end of each player‚Äôs turn).  
- Draw from **face-up queue first**, then replenish from deck.  
- **Overriding Alerts:**  
  - Spend **3 Command** (no action required).  
  - Only **active player** can override **their own turn‚Äôs Alert**.  
  - **Diplomat** can override **out of turn**, but pays **4 Command**.  
  - **Skill discounts apply**.  
- After Yellow and Orange decks are exhausted, draw **Red Alerts**.

#### Hostile Aliens  
- Placed by Alerts.  
- **Do not move or attack**.  
- **Block actions** in room: only allowed actions are:  
  - Kill (1 action, 1 Tactical)  
  - Use Teleporter  
- **Symbol on card = can be used in room with Hostile Alien**.  
- **Crewman dies** if injured **or** if **any player in same room is injured**.  
- **Soldier** can zap **all Hostile Aliens in a room** with Security Station.

#### Anomalies  
- Persistent negative effects from Alerts.  
- Removed by **Research Station**: 1 action, 4 Science.  
- **Irrational Fear/Anger**: Block all System use unless in Science Lab or playing as Cyborg.  
- **Strange Energies**: Uses orange blocker bar (see variant rules).  
- **Cyborg and players in Science Lab are immune**.

#### Alien Ships  
- Appear from Alerts. Attached to board.  
- **Fire Torpedo Tube** to:  
  - Destroy **1 ship**, **or**  
  - Skip Alert draw (use ability) ‚Äî **not both**.  
- **With External Scanners online**, may shoot ships **in the visible queue**:  
  - Shoot **first (next) ship**: **No Alert draw** at end of turn.  
  - Shoot **second ship**: Replace it when drawing Alert.  

#### Upgrades  
- Researched at Science Lab (2 actions, 4 Science).  
- Only **2 Upgrade slots** (except Auto Surgeon, which doesn‚Äôt use a slot).  
- Can replace existing Upgrades.

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
**Win Condition:**  
- Repair Jump Core to **"Engage!"** status.  
- **Team victory** ‚Äî all players win together.

**Loss Conditions:**  
- Shields reach **0%** and take another hit.  
- Cannot place a Hostile Alien (no pawns left).  
- Cannot draw an Alert card when required.  
- Eliminated by Red Alerts.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
**Q: Can I override the initial 5 setup Alerts?**  
A: No. Overrides only apply to Alerts drawn at end of turns.

**Q: Does Hologram get Battle Plans?**  
A: Yes. But cannot carry or use Tools.

**Q: Can I use Comm System to give a card while in a room with Hostile Aliens?**  
A: No. Only actions marked with the alien symbol are allowed.

**Q: Can I use a Battle Plan to repair a destroyed system?**  
A: Only if the Battle Plan specifically allows it. Otherwise, must reinstall first.

**Q: If External Scanners go offline, what happens to face-up Alerts?**  
A: Discard them immediately.

**Q: Can I shoot a ship in the queue if External Scanners are offline?**  
A: No. Must have External Scanners online.

**Q: Does the Ensign get affected by Increased Gravity?**  
A: No. Ensign is immune.

**Q: Can Teleporter Chief use ability out of turn?**  
A: No. Only during own turn.

**Q: Do Battle Plans count toward hand size?**  
A: No.

**Q: Can I repair a system while injured?**  
A: No ‚Äî unless the action has the üõë symbol.
