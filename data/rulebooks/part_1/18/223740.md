## Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective  
In *Bargain Quest*, players run item shops supplying heroes with gear to battle monsters threatening the town. The objective is to amass the most victory points by earning ★ (star) and ● (coin) tokens through successful hero sales and adventures. The game ends when the final monster is defeated or the hero deck runs out. The player with the highest score wins.

---

### 2. Components  
- 120 Item Cards  
- 16 Hero Cards (4 each of single-class: Rogue, Fighter, Cleric, Mage; 8 multi-class)  
- 20 Employee Cards  
- 12 Upgrade Cards (6 Display, 6 Storage)  
- 96 Coin Tokens (5, 10, 20 values)  
- 60 Star Tokens (1 and 3 values)  
- 10 Wound Tokens  
- 12 Monster Cards (4 each of Rank I, II, III)  
- 18 Adventure Cards  
- 6 Shop Boards  
- 1 Quest Token  
- Rulebook  

---

### 3. Setup  
**Hero Deck Construction**  
- Separate single-class heroes into 4 stacks (Rogue, Fighter, Cleric, Mage).  
- Shuffle multi-class heroes into one stack.  
- From each single-class stack, draw 2 random heroes.  
- From multi-class stack, draw 2 × number of players.  
- *R (Reliable Heroes)*: Remove all cards referencing adventure cards.  

**Table Setup**  
- Place supply piles: ★, ●, and wound tokens.  
- Place face-down decks: Item, Employee, Hero, Adventure (unless R variant).  
- Place face-up: Black Market Upgrade deck (if applicable).  
- Create monster deck: 1 random Rank I (top), II (middle), III (bottom), face-down.  
- Shuffle 6 event cards, place face-down (Retail Rumble variants).  
- Sort upgrade cards by type (Display/Storage), price-side up.  
- Shuffle black market item deck (if applicable).  
- Draw and place face-up: number-of-players (A2: 4) hero cards.  
- Place ● on each revealed hero equal to its purse value.  
- Shuffle ships and place face-down.  
- Place 1 item card (or black market card) face-down on each ship.  

**Player Setup**  
- Each player picks a shop board.  
- Each player takes 5 ●.  
- Determine first player: the one who most recently paid in cash claims the Quest Token.

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
Each round follows 6 steps:

#### Step 1: Supply  
- Flip top monster card face-up if face-down.  
- *RR (Retail Rumble)*: Reveal top event card if not already revealed.  
- Deal 4 item cards to each player (A2: 6).  
- Drafting:  
  - Each player selects 1 card from hand, places it face-down on shop.  
  - Pass remaining cards:  
    - Normal (1–4 players): Pass left.  
    - 5+ players:  
      - Rank I or III monster: Pass left.  
      - Rank II monster: Pass right.  
    - *RR*: Pass in direction shown on event card.  
  - Last card received is automatically drafted.  
  - If you haven’t taken a ship:  
    - Distribute drafted cards to ships (max 4 per ship), or  
    - Take a ship: collect all cards from it, place face-down in shop.  
- Each player takes all drafted and stored cards into hand.  

#### Step 2: Display  
- Choose 1 item from hand to place face-down in display area.  
- All players reveal display items simultaneously.  

#### Step 3: Shopping  
**Choosing Heroes**  
- Order: by total ♥ (Appeal) in display, descending.  
  - Tie: by total item value in display, descending.  
  - Still tied: clockwise from Quest Token holder.  
- Each player chooses 1 available hero matching **at least one class icon** in their display.  
- *A2*: After initial pick, each player picks a second hero (by total hero value).  
- Players unable to match class wait until others pick.  
- Remaining unoccupied shops pick in order of most ♥, then value, then clockwise from Quest Token.  
- When last hero is placed, that player claims the Quest Token.  

**Selling Items**  
- Sell any number of hand items to hero if item has **matching class icon**.  
- Display item cannot be sold.  
- Pay hero ● equal to item price; place item under hero to equip.  
- Hero (with items and leftover ●) moves in front of shop.  
- Return display item to hand.  

#### Step 4: Adventure  
- Deal 1 adventure card to each hero (unless R variant).  
- Resolve attacks clockwise from Quest Token holder:  
  - **Attack**: If hero’s total Attack ≥ monster’s Toughness:  
    - Place 1 wound token on monster.  
    - Gain 1 ★.  
  - **Defense**: If hero’s total Defense ≥ monster’s Strength:  
    - Hero survives; gain 1 ● (A2+RR: 2 ●).  
  - **Defeat**: If Defense < Strength:  
    - Discard hero, all equipped items, and remaining ● on hero.  
    - *A2+RR*: Place 1 wound token on 1 opposing hero; opponent gains 2 ★.  

**Resolution (non-RR)**  
- If **no hero wounded** monster:  
  - 1–4 players: Place 1 wound token.  
  - 5+ players: Place 2 wound tokens.  
- If **only 1 hero wounded** monster (5+ players): Add 1 extra wound.  
- Monster defeated if wound tokens ≥ number of players (A2: 4).  
  - Surviving heroes gain ● equal to treasure chest (bottom-right of monster).  
- If monster survives:  
  - Surviving heroes gain ● equal to coin value (bottom-left of monster).  
  - All gain ● equal to event card value (if applicable).  

**End of Adventure**  
- Shuffle adventure cards back into deck.  
- Discard all equipped items and adventure cards.  
- Return surviving heroes to center with accumulated ●.  
- Replace each defeated hero: draw new hero, place with purse ●.  

#### Step 5: Upgrade  
- Draw 2 employee cards face-up (A2: 4).  
  - If duplicate name already in market, discard and draw new.  
- Starting with Quest Token holder (clockwise), each may:  
  - Purchase 1 employee or upgrade card (A2: 2 actions).  
  - Upgrade: pay 10 ● to flip owned upgrade card to higher level.  
  - Skip.  
- If no upgrade cards available and 7+ players (typo? likely "if player has no upgrades"), draw 1 employee per missing upgrade (clarify with context).  
- Replace purchased employee immediately.  
- Shuffle leftover face-up employees back into deck.  

#### Step 6: Storage  
- Each player stores 1 item card from hand face-down on shop.  
- *Storage Upgrade*: allows storing additional cards.  
- Shuffle ships back into ship deck.  
- Place event card on bottom of deck (*RR*: discard current event card).  

---

### 5. Core Mechanics  
- **Drafting**: Card passing depends on player count and monster rank.  
- **Hero Matching**: Must match at least one class icon with display item.  
- **Quest Token**: Determines turn order in Adventure, Upgrade; awarded to last hero placer.  
- **Upgrades**:  
  - Display Upgrade: +1 or +2 display slots.  
  - Storage Upgrade: +1 or +2 storage capacity.  
  - Flip by paying 10 ●.  
- **Employees**: Unique abilities; used in specified step; no duplicates.  
- **Scoring Resources**:  
  - ★: 1 point each.  
  - ●: 1 point per 10 (rounded down).  

---

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
**End Game Triggers**  
- Core:  
  - Rank III monster defeated → end game.  
  - Hero deck empty when drawing new hero → end game (all lose).  
- *RR*: Last event card revealed → end game.  

**Scoring**  
- 1 point per ★.  
- 1 point per full 10 ● (e.g., 23 ● = 2 points).  
- **Tiebreakers**:  
  1. Most ● wins.  
  2. If still tied, share victory.  

---

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Can I sell to multiple heroes?** Yes, in A2 variant.  
- **What if I can’t match any hero?** Wait; pick after others if needed.  
- **Can I use stored items in display?** Yes, after returning from storage to hand.  
- **Do wound tokens persist on monsters?** Yes, until defeated or replaced.  
- **Can I flip an upgrade without purchasing?** Yes, by paying 10 ● during Upgrade Step.  
- **What if employee deck runs out?** No new employees added.
