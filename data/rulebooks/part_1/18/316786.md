# TABANNUSI: BUILDERS OF UR  
## Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective
In the ancient city of Ur, players take on the roles of master architects competing to leave the greatest legacy. By constructing buildings, cultivating gardens, claiming ships, and building on ziggurats, players gain prestige and victory points. The goal is to earn the most victory points by the end of the game through strategic use of resources, mastery tracks, and district scoring.

### 2. Components
- 1 double-sided main board (5 districts)
- 5 Barge tiles
- 5 Property Cost tiles
- 6 Action tiles
- 15 Ship tiles (5 each of white, yellow, brown)
- 9 Harbormaster tiles
- 60 Project tiles (20 each of white, yellow, brown)
- 16 Decree cards
- 32 Urban cards
- 10 Ziggurat tiles
- 20 Regular Water tiles
- 10 Special Water tiles
- 25 Garden tiles
- 6 Crate tiles
- 4 Player boards
- 15 Claim markers per player (4 colors)
- 3 Mastery markers per player (4 colors)
- Scoring markers
- Architects and Assistants (1 pair per player)
- 7 Resource dice per color (white, yellow, brown, blue, green)
- Starting Player token
- Unlimited Gold tokens (use proxies if needed)
- Building pieces (25 per color)
- Houses (14 per player)

### 3. Setup
1. Place main board in center, choose side based on player count.
2. Shuffle 5 Barge tiles and place one on each Barge space. Cover with matching Property Cost tile.
3. Roll 5/6/7 Resource dice per color for 2/3/4 players and place on matching colored Barges.
4. Place 5 Gold on Time Track.
5. Shuffle Ship tiles and place 7 randomly on Port spaces.
6. Place 6 random Harbormaster tiles in Port.
7. Place 6 random Crate tiles in Port crate spaces.
8. Place Water, ?-Water, and Garden stacks on board. Flip 3 ?-Water tiles face up.
9. Shuffle 6 Action tiles and place on dedicated spaces.
10. Shuffle Ziggurat tiles and place 1 face up on each Ziggurat space.
11. Place Decree cards (1 of each symbol type) on board.
12. Shuffle Urban cards and place near board.
13. Separate Project tiles by color into 3 stacks on board.
14. Place Building pieces next to board.
15. Each player:
   - Takes Architect, Assistant, 14 Houses, 25 Buildings in their color
   - Keeps 5 Claim markers (return rest to supply)
   - Receives 5 Gold, 2 Claim markers, 1 random Crate tile, 1 random Urban card
   - Places 3 Mastery markers on bottom spaces of each Mastery track
   - Places Scoring marker on 10 VP
16. In reverse order, each player places 1 Building and 1 House in a common District, gaining space bonus.
17. Place player's Architect and Assistant in chosen District.

### 4. Gameplay Flow
On your turn:
1. **Take Resource & Move Architect**: Take one die from your current District's Barge. Move Architect to District matching die value. May pay 1 Gold to ignore value and move anywhere. On die=6, move to any District not accessible by other dice.
2. **Perform Actions**: In Assistant's District, perform 0, 1, or 2 actions (same or different).
3. **Check for Scoring**: If any Barge is empty, score all empty Barges (active player chooses order).
4. **Move Assistant**: Move Assistant to Architect's District.
5. Pass turn clockwise.

### 5. Core Mechanics

#### Actions (available in respective Districts)
- **Place Project (Districts 1-3)**:
  1. Select color with <3 existing tiles of that color in District.
  2. Place adjacent to same-colored Projects if any exist.
  3. Cannot place adjacent to same-colored Building.
  4. Place Claim marker on tile.
  5. Gain bonus if covered.

- **Construct Building (Districts 1-3)**:
  1. Choose all Projects of one color in District.
  2. Pay 2 local Resources + 1 Resource per color for each of your Claim markers on chosen Projects.
  3. Replace Projects with Building of same shape/color.
  4. Place House from matching color row (or bottom row) on Building.
  5. Gain bonus from uncovered space on player board.
  6. Discard your Claim markers. Opponents with Claim markers on Projects return markers and advance 1 step on matching Mastery track. Players with adjacent Gardens advance 1 step on track of choice.
  7. (Optional) Spend 1 Gold to claim Ship matching Building size.

- **Gardening (any District)**:
  - Purchase Water tiles (1 blue Resource each) and/or Garden tiles (1 green Resource each).
  - Place Water tiles adjacent to Buildings, Water, or Garden tiles.
  - Place up to 3 Garden tiles on Water tiles. Gain bonus of covered Water tiles.
  - Must place Claim marker on each Garden tile.
  - Cannot perform if no Claim markers available.

- **Place House in Port**:
  1. Pay 2 blue Resources.
  2. Place House from bottom row on Port House space.
  3. Cannot have Houses in both spaces of same row/column.
  4. Gain Crate or bonus if applicable.

- **Claim Ship in Port**:
  1. Pay 2 Gold (1 required).
  2. Place Claim marker on any Ship without your marker.
  3. Gain Ship ability from next turn.
  4. If you complete row/column of 3 Ships, take corresponding Harbormaster tile(s).

- **Place House in Ziggurat**:
  1. Pay 2 green Resources (1 required).
  2. Select White, Brown, or Yellow section.
  3. Take leftmost House from matching color row (or bottom) and place on Ziggurat space.
  4. Gain bonus if covered.
  5. Place Claim marker on leftmost available space of corresponding Ziggurat tile.

#### Free Actions (any time during turn)
- Flip 1 Crate tile face up by returning any 1 Resource to supply.
- Score any number of Decree cards by meeting requirements.

#### Scoring Trigger
Occurs when a Barge becomes empty:
1. Active player gains 1 Gold from Time Track.
2. All players return all local Resources to supply.
   - For each yellow/brown/white Resource lost: advance 1 step on matching Mastery track.
   - For each blue Resource lost: gain 1 Water tile.
   - For each green Resource lost: gain 1 Garden tile.
   - (Only 1 advancement/tile per player, regardless of quantity lost)
3. Score District:
   - **Common Districts (1-3)**: Each Building scores VP = (size + adjacent Garden tiles) Ã— VP value at Mastery marker position.
   - **Port**: 1 VP per Claim marker in same row/column as your House (2 VP if in both).
   - **Ziggurat**: Score rightmost Claim marker on each Ziggurat tile using tile's scoring rule.
4. Return all dice to Barge and roll new values.
5. If Time Track empty, trigger Final Scoring.

#### Final Scoring
When last Gold is removed from Time Track:
1. Continue until all players have equal turns.
2. Play one additional full round (empty Barges trigger partial scoring: only steps 2 and 7).
3. Score all 5 Districts one final time using regular rules.
4. Reveal and score Urban cards.
5. Winner has most VP. Tiebreaker: most Gold, then shared victory.

### 6. Scoring & End Game
- **Victory Points**: Accumulated through building, gardening, Mastery tracks, Decree cards, Harbormaster tiles, and final scoring.
- **End Game Trigger**: Removal of last Gold from Time Track.
- **Final Scoring**:
  1. One final round with partial Barge scoring.
  2. Full scoring of all 5 Districts.
  3. Urban card scoring.
- **Win Condition**: Most VP. Tie: most Gold wins; otherwise shared victory.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Claim Markers**: Limited to 15 per player. Cannot perform actions requiring Claim markers if none available.
- **Resource Dice**: Values matter only for movement. Once in player area, they are generic Resources.
- **Gold**: Wild Resource. Can substitute for any Resource, but cannot be substituted.
- **Crate Tiles**: Can pay cost by flipping face down. Can flip back up by spending any Resource.
- **Mastery Bonuses**: Triggered when lowest marker advances. Benefits include Gold, Claim markers, VP, or Resources.
- **Ziggurat Scoring**: Each tile scores independently based on rightmost Claim marker.
- **Urban Cards**: Each House can only count toward one Urban card requirement.
