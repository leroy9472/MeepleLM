### 1. Lore & Objective  
**Lore:**  
In the near future, the Solarus Corporation discovers an infinite source of rare minerals beneath Mars‚Äô surface. Players lead elite Pod pilot crews on a drilling expedition to extract these resources. Profits fund upgrades to their pilots‚Äô skills and equipment. The goal is to become the most celebrated employee in the corporation‚Äôs history.

**Objective:**  
Earn the most Victory Points (VPs) by purchasing Pilot Cards, claiming Achievement Cards, and collecting Artifact Tiles with VP value. The game ends immediately when the last Artifact on the Depth 4 Game Board is collected.

### 2. Components  
- 76 Tunnel Tiles (in 3 lengths: 1, 2, and 3 spaces)  
- 80 Double-Sided Mineral Tiles (6 types: Iron, Gold, Platinum, Emerald, Ruby, Diamond; each with a monetary value)  
- 4 Double-Sided Game Boards (labeled Depth 1 to Depth 4)  
- 4 Player Decks (23 Pilot Cards each; divided into 4 color stacks: Red, Blue, Yellow, Wild)  
- 20 Minor Achievement Cards  
- 10 Major Achievement Cards  
- 16 Bomb Tokens  
- 16 Artifact Tiles  

---

### 3. Setup  

#### Game Area Setup  
1. **Determine Starting Player:** The player who most recently dug a hole.  
2. **Place Game Board:** Put the "Depth 1" board in the center. The player to the right of the starting player chooses which side is face up.  
3. **Mineral Tiles:** Place near the board.  
4. **Artifact Tiles:** Shuffle and place face down near the board.  
5. **Major Achievements:** Shuffle the 10-card deck; deal 3 face up. Return the remaining 7 to the box.  
6. **Minor Achievements:** Shuffle the 20-card deck; place face down. Deal 3 face up below the Major Achievements.  
7. **Tunnel Tiles:** Place near the board.  
8. **Bomb Tokens:** Place in a supply pile.  
9. **Remaining Game Boards:** Stack accessible nearby (Depths 2‚Äì4).  

#### Player Area Setup  
1. **Draw Deck:** Remove the 7 Starting Pilot Cards (no cost in top-right corner). Shuffle and place face down as your Draw Deck.  
2. **Discard Area:** Leave space beside your Draw Deck.  
3. **Bomb Token:** Take 1 and place in your area.  
4. **Pilot Stacks:** Sort remaining 16 Pilot Cards into 4 stacks by Drill Icon color (Red, Blue, Yellow, Wild). Within each stack, order by ascending cost ($10 on top, $25 on bottom).  
5. **Starting Hand:** Draw 4 cards from your Draw Deck.

---

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
Players take turns clockwise. Each turn consists of **2 actions** (can repeat the same action twice).  

**Available Actions:**  
1. **Draw:** Draw 2 cards from your Draw Deck.  
   - If the Draw Deck is empty, shuffle your Discard Area to form a new Draw Deck.  
2. **Drill:** Use matching Drill Icons to collect from the board.  
3. **Bomb:** Use a Bomb Token and matching card to collect via a pattern.  

At the end of your turn, if you have more than 5 cards in hand, discard down to 5.

### 5. Core Mechanics  

#### Drill Action  
1. **Discard Cards:** Play any number of cards with matching Drill Icons (üü•, üü¶, üü®, or üåê).  
   - Wild (üåê) icons can substitute for any color.  
   - If using only Wilds, all must represent the same color.  
2. **Place Tunnel Tiles:** Use Tunnel Tiles totaling length equal to the number of Drill Icons played.  
   - Must be placed in a **straight line**, edge-to-edge.  
   - Must connect to existing Tunnel Tiles or the surface line.  
3. **Collect:** Take all resources (Minerals, Artifacts, Bomb Tokens, Draw Bonuses) from covered spaces.

#### Bomb Action  
1. **Discard Bomb Token and Card:** Spend 1 Bomb Token and discard a Red Pilot Card with a Bomb Pattern (e.g., 2√ó2 square).  
   - Cannot Bomb without a Bomb Token.  
2. **Place Tunnel Tiles:** Form the exact shape of the Bomb Pattern.  
   - May rotate or flip the pattern.  
   - Must connect to existing Tunnel Tiles or surface.  
3. **Collect:** Take all resources from covered spaces.

#### Placing Tunnel Tiles ‚Äì Rules  
- Must connect edge-to-edge to existing tunnels or surface.  
- May connect along any edge of the placed tiles.  
- May progress in any direction (including upward).  
- Cannot hang off board edges, but may cross seams between boards.  
- May combine multiple tiles to form needed shapes.  
- No overlapping tiles.  
- No ownership: any player may connect to any tunnel.  
- Once placed, tiles cannot be moved or removed.  
- **Drill:** Must be straight line; no omitting spaces.  
- **Bomb:** Must exactly match the pattern; no omitting spaces.

#### Terrain Types  
- **Dirt:** No restrictions. Can be Drilled or Bombed. All dirt colors are equivalent.  
- **Steel Plate:**  
  - Cannot be Bombed.  
  - To Drill: Drill Icon color must match the border color of the Steel Plate.  
- **Rock:**  
  - Cannot be Drilled.  
  - Can be Bombed.

#### Collecting  
When covering spaces:  
- **Mineral Tiles:** Take from supply and place **all** on the **same** topmost Pilot Card stack. Value is used as currency.  
- **Bomb Tokens:** Take 1 from supply.  
- **Artifacts:** Take 1 face-down Artifact Tile; keep in play area (secret).  
- **Draw Bonuses:** Immediately draw cards as shown (e.g., +1, +2).

#### Buying Cards  
- At any time, if the total Mineral value on a topmost Pilot Stack meets or exceeds a card‚Äôs cost:  
  - Return all Minerals to supply.  
  - Place the card into your **Discard Area**.  
  - **No change** is given.  
- Buying triggers **Buy Bonuses** (immediately, not an action).

#### Buy Bonuses  
Triggered when purchasing a card:  
- Card goes directly into hand (not discard).  
- Perform an additional action this turn.  
- Draw 1 or 2 cards.  
- Gain a Bomb Token.  
- Duplicate a Mineral (take matching one from supply and place on any stack; restrictions apply).  

#### Drill Bonuses  
Triggered when discarding a card with Drill Bonus during a Drill Action:  
- Draw 1 or 2 cards.  
- Gain a Bomb Token.  
- Drill Rock spaces this action.  
- Collect Minerals and split across stacks.  
- Attach tunnels corner-to-corner.  
- Replace a Minor Achievement card (send one to bottom of deck, draw new one).

#### Artifact Tiles  
- Collected during Bomb or Drill actions.  
- Can be used **at any time during your turn** (not an action).  
- Discard face up to supply to gain bonus.  
- **No limit** on number held or used per turn.  
- **Bonuses:**  
  - Additional Action  
  - Draw 2 cards  
  - Gain Bomb Token + draw 1 card  
  - Take Platinum and place on any stack  
  - Take Gold + draw 1 card  
  - Buy a card ‚Üí place in hand instead of discard  
  - Worth 3 VPs (no effect)  
  - Take a Mineral matching one on any stack and place on any stack  

#### Achievements  
- **Claiming:** If you fulfill a visible Achievement goal **during your turn**, take it immediately.  
- Max **1 Major** and **1 Minor** per turn.  

**Major Achievements:**  
- Awarded for purchasing **all 4 cards** in a single Pilot Stack (color).  
- Not replenished after claimed.  

**Minor Achievements:**  
- Specific goals (e.g., ‚ÄúBomb 2 Rock spaces in one action‚Äù).  
- When claimed, draw a new one from the deck (until deck is empty).

#### Adding a Game Board  
- When the **last Artifact space** on the current board is covered:  
  1. Take the next board (Depth = current +1).  
  2. Attach it below the current board, same orientation.  
  3. If **two boards are already in play**, remove the **topmost (lowest Depth)** board and return its Tunnel Tiles to supply.  
  4. Hanging tunnels remain in play.  
  5. The player who triggered the board change chooses the new board‚Äôs face-up side.  

**2-Player Game Note:** Skip Depth 3. Use only Depths 1, 2, and 4.

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
**End Game Trigger:**  
- When a player Bombs or Drills the **last Artifact** on **Depth 4**, the game ends **after that player‚Äôs turn**.

**Scoring:**  
Each player counts:  
- **Victory Points (VPs)** from all Pilot Cards in **Draw Deck** and **Discard Area** (value in white hexagon).  
- **VPs** from all **Achievement Cards** claimed.  
- **VPs** from **Artifact Tiles** (only those with VP value, e.g., 3-VP Artifact).  

**Winner:** Player with the most VPs. Tiebreaker not specified.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Q:** Can I use Artifact Tiles during another player‚Äôs turn?  
  **A:** No. Only during your own turn.  

- **Q:** Can I split Mineral Tiles from one action across multiple stacks?  
  **A:** Only if a Drill Bonus allows it. Otherwise, all must go on one stack.  

- **Q:** What happens when the Minor Achievement deck runs out?  
  **A:** No replacements. Players can still claim the remaining face-up cards.  

- **Q:** Can I Bomb Steel Plates?  
  **A:** No. Steel Plates cannot be Bombed.  

- **Q:** Can I Drill Rock?  
  **A:** No. Only Bombing can collect from Rock.  

- **Q:** Do I get change when buying a card?  
  **A:** No. Excess Minerals are lost.  

- **Q:** Can I use a Buy Bonus if I buy a card via Artifact effect?  
  **A:** Yes. The Buy Bonus triggers whenever a card is purchased, regardless of how it was acquired.  

- **Q:** Can I use multiple Artifact effects in one turn?  
  **A:** Yes. No limit on number used per turn.
