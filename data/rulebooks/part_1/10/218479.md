## Comprehensive Reference Manual  

### 1. Lore & Objective  
Cerebria is a team-based area control game where players represent opposing forces—Bliss and Gloom—competing to shape the mindscape of Cerebia. Spirits invoke Emotions to gain control over Realms and Frontiers, pursue Aspirations, and build the Identity with Fragments. The goal is to score the most points by fulfilling Aspirations during Revelations and completing Intentions.  

### 2. Components  
- **Main Board**: Features five Realms (Valley of Motives, Willow of Values, Network of Thoughts, Cradle of Senses, Land of Desires) and five Frontiers.  
- **Origin Board**: Central hub with five Spheres, each starting with 7 Willpower (WP).  
- **Spirits**: 4 Bliss and 4 Gloom Spirit Figures and Boards (A/B sides).  
- **Emotions**: Mild and Strong Emotion cards (Bliss and Gloom), including starting cards (Brightness, Bleakness).  
- **Tokens**: Willpower, Essence, Vibe, Intensity, Ambition, Capping Fragment, and Identity Fragments (Minor/Major).  
- **Team Boards**: Track Hidden Aspirations, Ambition, and Intentions.  
- **Aspiration Cards**: 9 Common and 9 Hidden per team.  
- **Operation Cards** (Solo mode): 12 cards (Defiance, Endeavor, Judgment) for Ego AI.  

### 3. Setup  
1. **Board**: Attach Origin board to main board. Place 7 WP in each Sphere.  
2. **Teams**: 2 players per team (Bliss/Gloom), seated together. Use A-side for basic game.  
3. **Team Boards**:  
   - Place 3 Ambition tokens (inactive side up).  
   - Shuffle Hidden Aspirations deck; peek at top card (≠ current Common Aspiration).  
   - Lay out 7 Common Aspirations face-up (basic game).  
4. **Spirits**:  
   - Choose Spirit; place board, figure, 2 Essence, 4 WP (6 for first player).  
   - Place 4 wild Vibe tokens to unlock Move, Invoke, Quell, Fortify.  
5. **Emotion Decks**:  
   - Build 16-card decks (≤2 copies per card). Use starter decks for basic game.  
   - Shuffle; draw 2 cards, reveal top card.  
6. **Starting Emotions**:  
   - Place Spirit on Frontier.  
   - Place Brightness/Bleakness in adjacent Realm slot. Add 1 Essence.  
7. **Control Markers**: Set based on starting Emotions.  
8. **Order**: Randomize Player Order markers (GBGB). First player gets +2 WP, Absorb disabled turn 1.  

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
On your turn:  
1. **Actions**: Take 3 Actions (Spirit or Realm). Actions can be repeated.  
2. **Ambition Abilities**: Use before/after any Action (once per turn):  
   - Add Vibe token (1 Ambition).  
   - Rotate Origin (1 Ambition).  
   - Gain extra Action (2 Ambition).  
3. **Absorb Ability**: Use once per turn:  
   - Flip Action Tracker.  
   - Choose adjacent Sphere. Gain 2 WP +1 per adjacent controlled Frontier.  
   - Optional Sphere bonus (pay cost).  
   - If Sphere empties → Revelation.  
   - Rotate Origin.  
4. **End Turn**:  
   - Flip 1 Ambition token OR draw 2 Emotion cards.  
   - Gain 1 WP if at 0.  
   - Flip Action Tracker to enable Absorb.  

### 5. Core Mechanics  
- **Spirit Actions** (unlocked with Vibe tokens):  
  - **Move** (1 WP): Move to adjacent space. Cannot end on opponent (unless Surmount).  
    - Haste: +1 WP to move extra space.  
    - Surmount: +1 WP to end on opponent.  
    - Determination: -1 WP cost.  
  - **Invoke** (2 WP): Play Emotion from hand to adjacent empty slot. Pay 1 Essence from board.  
    - Bolster: +1 Ambition to add extra Essence (from supply).  
    - Inner Force: +2 WP to take Essence from supply.  
    - Determination: -1 WP.  
  - **Quell** (2 WP +1 Ambition): Remove 1 Essence from adjacent opposing Emotion. Must reveal matching Vibe card (except Brightness/Bleakness).  
    - Subdue: +2 WP to remove 2 Essence.  
    - Extinguish: +1 WP to quell without revealing card.  
    - Determination: -1 WP.  
  - **Fortify** (3 WP): Place Minor Fragment in uncontrolled Realm (empty Fortress). Gain +1 Intensity.  
    - Exalt: +1 Ambition to replace Minor with Major Fragment (+2 Intensity).  
    - Raze: +1 WP +1 Ambition to downgrade opponent’s Fragment. Gain extra Action.  
    - Determination: -1 WP.  
  - **Empower** (3 WP): Replace adjacent Mild Emotion with Strong counterpart (if meets threshold). Discard Mild, transfer Essence.  
    - Channeled Power: +1 Ambition to add 1 Essence pre-Empower.  
    - Emotional Outburst: +1 WP to Empower non-adjacent Emotion.  
    - Determination: -1 WP.  
- **Realm Actions** (always available):  
  - **Valley of Motives**: Pay 1 WP → gain 4 WP.  
  - **Cradle of Senses**: Pay 1 WP → draw 1 card; pay 2 WP → draw another (repeatable).  
  - **Network of Thoughts**: Pay 2 WP → move any team Emotion to adjacent slot.  
  - **Land of Desires**: Pay 1 WP → add 1 Essence to adjacent team Emotion; pay 1 WP per additional Essence.  
  - **Willow of Values**: Pay 1 WP → exchange any WP on board for equal Essence.  
  - **Discount**: -1 WP if team controls Realm.  

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
- **Revelation**: Triggered when Absorb empties a Sphere.  
  1. Reveal Hidden Aspirations.  
  2. Evaluate Aspirations:  
     - **Common Aspiration**: Leftmost face-up card.  
     - **Hidden Aspiration**: Top of team deck.  
     - Win if team has more of the required (e.g., Realms, Essence, Vibes).  
  3. **Score**:  
     - 1 Aspiration → Minor Fragment.  
     - 2 Aspirations → Major Fragment.  
     - Add to Identity.  
  4. **New Common Aspiration**: Flip current face-down; next card becomes active.  
  5. **New Hidden Aspiration**:  
     - Accomplished: Use top card (≠ Common).  
     - Not accomplished: Draw 3, choose 1 (≠ Common).  
  6. **Add Fortress**: If adjacent Fortress built/exalted earlier, add to Identity.  
  7. Refill Sphere with 7 WP.  
  8. Rotate Origin.  
- **Intentions** (Side A/B): Score during turn for:  
  - A: Quell Essence, Absorb ≥4 WP, fill Emotion, fully upgrade Action.  
  - B: Quell Essence, add Fortress in Revelation, Empower, Vibe milestones.  
  - First: 1 point; subsequent: 2 points.  
- **End Game**: Triggers if:  
  - No more Common Aspirations.  
  - No Fragments to add → add Capping Fragment.  
  - Team reaches 20 points on Wheel (full game).  
- **Final Scoring**:  
  - Minor Fragment: 3 points.  
  - Major Fragment: 5 points.  
  - Capping Fragment: 4 points.  
  - Intentions: Points on Wheel.  
  - **Tiebreaker**: Team with Capping Fragment wins.  

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Adjacency**: Each Emotion slot adjacent to 2 others. Realm slots adjacent within Realm.  
- **Control**:  
  - Frontier: Highest total Intensity in Triad (3 slots).  
  - Realm: Sum of unblocked Emotions + Fortress bonus.  
- **Vibe Tokens**:  
  - Cannot have two of same color per Action row.  
  - Wild tokens have no color.  
- **Absorb**:  
  - Spirit in Realm → 1 Sphere. On Frontier → choose between 2.  
  - Bonus: +1 WP per adjacent controlled Frontier.  
- **Revelation**:  
  - Fortress added only if not built/exalted this turn.  
  - Rotate Origin after.  
- **Solo vs. Ego**:  
  - Ego uses Operation cards to determine Actions.  
  - Target Aspiration guides choices.  
  - Failures → Special Operations.
