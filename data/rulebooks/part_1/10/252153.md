## Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective  
Tang Garden is a tile-laying and decoration placement game set in a classical Chinese garden. Players take turns building the garden and placing decorative elements to earn coins through track advancement, character abilities, and endgame scoring. The goal is to amass the most coins by the end of the game through strategic tile placement, character use, and decoration management.

### 2. Components  
- **Main Board**: Central play area with slots for garden tiles and landscape placements.
- **Garden Tiles (4 stacks)**: Categorized by back design (Rock, Water, Greenery, Footpath); each stack contains face-down tiles with one face-up.
- **Landscape Tiles**:  
  - 12 Small Landscape Tiles (e.g., Ghost Village, Desolate Land, Phoenix)  
  - 12 Large Landscape Tiles (e.g., Dragon, Sun/Moon, Village)  
- **Landscape Tokens**: 16 tokens (8 small, 8 large) placed on the board at setup.
- **Decoration Cards & Items**:  
  - 54 Decoration Cards (red back) including Trees, Birds & Fish, Pavilions, Bridges, Lotus & Peonies, Bonsai, Ninfea, Vase, Duck, Butterfly, Cat, Swan, Apricot Tree, Spectral Tree.
  - 36 Physical Decoration Items matching card types.
- **Player Boards**: One per player, with three colored tracks (Water, Greenery, Rock).
- **Cubes**: 3 per player (Water, Greenery, Rock), used to track progress.
- **Lantern Tokens**: 4 per player (6 in Golden Age expansion), used for special actions.
- **Character Cards & Miniatures**:  
  - Base: Architect, Child, Empress, Hermit, Poet, Student  
  - Ghost Stories: Demon, Fox Spirit, Medium, Soldier  
  - Golden Age: Emissary, Gardener, Geomancer, Literate, Musician, Nobleman, Tao Priest, Princess  
  - Neutral: Emperor, Lady
- **Character Tokens**: Orange square tokens to delay character influence.
- **Engineer Token**: Marks starting player.

### 3. Setup  
1. Place the central 4-space Garden Tile in any orientation.
2. Position the 4 Garden Tile stacks in board corners; flip top tile face-up.
3. Shuffle Decoration deck; place nearby with all Decoration items.
4. Each player receives:
   - Player board
   - 3 cubes (placed at start of tracks)
   - 4 Lantern tokens (face-up on board)
   - 1 starting Character card (randomly dealt from Architect, Child, Empress, Hermit, Poet, Student)
   - Matching Character miniature placed on card
5. Advance the cube matching the symbol on the upper-right of the starting Character card.
6. Shuffle remaining Character cards into a deck; reveal top 2 face-up.
7. Shuffle Small and Large Landscape tile stacks separately.
   - Insert top 4 Small Landscape tiles into inner side slots of board.
   - Place top tile of each stack beside it (2 visible).
   - Do same for Large Landscape tiles (outer side slots).
8. Place 16 Landscape tokens on board:
   - 3–4 players: Diamond pattern, 4 per side
   - 2 players: Square in middle ring (no corners)
   - 1 player: 8 tokens in non-outer corner spaces
9. First player takes Engineer Token.

*Expansion Setup Notes:*
- **Ghost Stories**: Roll d10 to select scenario.
- **Golden Age**: Roll d6 to select scenario.
- **Decorations Showcase**: Draw 2 face-up Decoration cards as showcase.
- **Market of the Pear Garden**: Create 5th stack of 16 tiles; draw 3 to form market.
- **For the Glory of the Emperor**: Place Emperor miniature on starting tile; return card to box.

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
On your turn, perform **one mandatory action**, then up to **three optional actions** (each only once per turn):

#### Mandatory Action (Choose One):
**A. Build the Garden**  
- Choose a face-up Garden tile from any stack.
- Place it orthogonally adjacent to an existing tile.
- All adjacent sides must match terrain type (Rock, Water, Greenery, Footpath), except Walls, which match any.
- **Scoring:**
  - Advance your track 1 space for each matching terrain side.
  - Advance 1 space for each enclosed terrain area (fully surrounded, including by walls).
  - Gain 1 coin per matching Footpath.
  - Optionally, gain 1 track advancement for every 2 matching Footpaths (instead of 2 coins).
- **Special Tiles:**
  - **Temple Tiles (Rock, Water, Greenery)**: Gain 1 coin for placing; +1 coin per matching adjacent side. Future players gain 1 coin when matching its wall.
  - **Bamboo Garden (Golden Age)**: Gains 1 coin for placement and 1 coin for each adjacent environment type.
  - **Haunted Temple (Ghost Stories)**: Gains 1 coin for placement and 1 track advancement per adjacent Footpath.
- If tile covers a Landscape token:
  - Take the token.
  - Place one of the two visible Landscape tiles of matching size into an empty slot.
  - Reveal next Landscape tile.
- If fewer than 2 face-up Garden tiles remain, flip top tiles face-up.

**B. Place a Decoration**  
- Draw 2 + (1 per face-down Garden tile stack) Decoration cards.
- Choose one to play; discard others face-up.
- Place the corresponding Decoration item on a valid placement indicator (symbol on tile).
- Claim one-time bonus (if shown: track advancement, token removal, etc.).
- If no valid placement, discard all and end action.
- Refresh all face-down Garden tiles at end of turn.

#### Optional Actions (Max One Each, Any Time During Turn):
**C. Influence a Character**  
- Triggered when all three of your tracks have passed a Character threshold (marked on tracks).
- Choose: take one of the two face-up Character cards or draw blindly from deck.
- Then, place either your **current active Character** or the **newly acquired one** onto the board on any empty Character spot (Pavilion, Bridge, or Garden tile).
- If placing the new one, slide its card under your stack (only endgame scoring visible).
- If placing the old one, place the new card on top (new in-game skill active).
- Replace face-up card if taken; reveal next if needed.
- If unable or unwilling, take a Character token as IOU.
- *Note:* If next threshold is reached without using token, it is lost.

**D. Use a Lantern**  
- Flip one Lantern face-down to activate its power. Can be done at any time.
- **Lantern Powers:**
  - **Place Two Tiles**: During Build action, place 2 face-up tiles (both must be face-up at turn start).
  - **Place Two Decorations**: During Decorate action, keep and place 2 cards, claim both bonuses.
  - **Move/Reorient Character**: Move one of your influenced Characters to any valid space and/or change facing.
  - **Search Deck**: When influencing a Character or placing a Landscape, look through deck to choose one; shuffle after.
  - **Draw 5 Tiles**: When building, draw 5 from any stack, choose 1 to place, return others in any order.
  - **Exchange Active Character**: Swap your current Character with one of the two face-up display cards.

**E. Refresh a Lantern**  
- Spend 3 of the same type of Landscape token (4 in 2-player game) to flip a used Lantern back face-up.
- **Emissary Lantern (Golden Age)**: May use different types of tokens.

### 5. Core Mechanics  
- **Line of Sight (LoS)**: Orthogonal direction a Character faces. Includes tile they’re on and all tiles in front until blocked.
- **Active Landscape**: The 5 Landscape tiles (3 small, 2 large) in the direction a Character is facing.
- **Terrain Enclosure**: A region fully surrounded by matching terrain or walls. Grants track advancement.
- **Character Skills**: Active only for your **current active Character** (top card). Used during main actions.
- **Endgame Triggers**:
  - One Garden tile stack is empty, OR
  - 3 or fewer Landscape tokens remain on board.
  - Game continues to end of current round.

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
After final turn, score:

#### A. Decoration Scoring:
- **Trees**: 1/4/9/16/25 coins per set of 1/2/3/4/5 different Tree types. Extra Trees start new sets.
- **Birds & Fish**: 6 coins per pair.
- **Lotus & Peonies**: 6 coins per pair.
- **Pavilions**: 12 coins to player with most, 6 to second. Tie for first: split 18 coins. No second awarded.
- **Bridges**: 2 coins each.
- **Bonsai, Ninfea, Sancai Vase (Golden Age)**: 7 coins each, minus 1 coin for each such item in the garden (including yours).
- **Swan (Ghost Stories)**: At endgame, choose one Decoration type for it to count as (resolve in turn order).
- **Spectral Tree (Ghost Stories)**: Decorations in 8 surrounding tiles lose LoS value (not visible to Characters).
- **Cat (Ghost Stories)**: Protects one Character from Demon’s effect.

#### B. Character Scoring (for Characters on Board):
- **Base Scoring**: 1 coin per Decoration in LoS (including own tile).
- **Sight Preferences**:
  - **Architect**: +3 per Construction icon on Active Landscape.
  - **Child**: +2 per Animal icon.
  - **Empress**: +9; -3 if Emperor in LoS; -3 if Lady in LoS.
  - **Hermit**: +2 per Rock tile in LoS (max 10).
  - **Poet**: +2 per Water tile in LoS (max 10).
  - **Student**: +2 per Greenery tile in LoS (max 10).
  - **Emperor**: +5 per Dragon icon on Active Landscape.
  - **Lady**: +6 per Sun icon; +6 per Moon icon (max 12).
  - **Merchant**: +2 per Village icon.
  - **Monk**: +3 per Buddhist Temple icon.
  - **Officer**: +1 per icon (any type) on Active Landscape.
  - **Sword Dancer**: +2 per Waterfall icon.
  - **Demon**: +6; other players’ Characters lose 1 (or 3 if in LoS), unless protected by Cat.
  - **Fox Spirit**: Copies skill and sight of targeted Character.
  - **Medium**: +8; surrounding Characters (8-tile radius) lose 1.
  - **Ghost Soldier**: +3 (or +7 if at least one Wall icon in Active Landscape).
  - **Emissary**: +3 per side with at least one Village icon.
  - **Gardener**: +2 per Decoration in LoS (max 10).
  - **Geomancer**: +2/5/8 for 1/2/3 terrain types in LoS.
  - **Literate**: +3 per side with at least one Animal icon.
  - **Musician**: +3 per Character in LoS.
  - **Nobleman**: +2 per different icon on Active Landscape.
  - **Tao Priest**: +12; -1 per different icon on Active Landscape.
  - **Princess**: +4 per pair of identical icons on Active Landscape.
  - **Herbalist**: +5 per Temple tile in LoS.
  - **Wayfarer**: +1 per Decoration in LoS (all four directions).

#### C. Final Scoring:
- Total coins from all sources.
- **Winner**: Highest total.
- **Tiebreakers**:
  1. Most Landscape tokens
  2. Most advanced cubes (furthest along tracks)

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Can I use a Lantern after drawing tiles/cards?** Yes, at any time during your turn.
- **Can I use multiple Lanterns in one turn?** No, only one per turn.
- **What if I can’t place any Decoration?** Discard all drawn cards; turn continues.
- **Can I influence a Character if no spot is available?** No; you must either place one or take a token.
- **Can I use Butterfly to refresh Lantern without paying Demon?** Yes, Butterfly bypasses Demon’s tax.
- **Can I move a Character onto a tile with a Decoration?** Yes, as long as there’s a Character icon.
- **Can Spectral Tree block its own LoS?** Yes, all 8 surrounding tiles (including diagonals) lose LoS value.
- **Can I use Emissary to refresh with mixed tokens?** Yes, any combination of Landscape tokens.
- **Can I use Wayfarer to retreat a track on a bonus space?** No, only tracks not on coin or character spaces.
