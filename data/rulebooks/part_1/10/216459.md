## THE LOST EXPEDITION ‚Äî Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective  
In *The Lost Expedition*, players lead a team of three explorers through the Amazon jungle in search of the mythical lost city of Z‚Äîinspired by Percy Fawcett‚Äôs final, ill-fated expedition. The game simulates the real dangers of the Brazilian jungle, including wildlife, disease, terrain, and indigenous tribes.  

**Objective**: Be the first to advance your pawn to the final **Expedition card**, the Lost City of Z. In cooperative and solo modes, this results in a win. In head-to-head, it triggers a win condition depending on tiebreakers.

### 2. Components  
- **6 Explorer cards** ‚Äì 2 each for Jungle, Navigation, and Camping expertise  
- **9 Expedition cards** ‚Äì Numbered 1‚Äì9, with card 9 being the Lost City of Z  
- **56 Adventure cards** ‚Äì Core encounter cards with symbols and effects  
- **28 Health tokens** ‚Äì Represent explorer vitality (max 4 per explorer)  
- **21 Food tokens**  
- **12 Ammunition tokens**  
- **2 Pawns** ‚Äì For head-to-head mode; one per player  
- **1 Morning/Evening token** ‚Äì Tracks time of day  
- **1 Expedition Leader token** ‚Äì Determines turn order and final say in decisions  
- **2 Player Aids** ‚Äì Reference sheets for symbols and rules

### 3. Setup  

#### Cooperative Mode (2‚Äì5 players)  
1. Choose **one explorer of each expertise** (Jungle, Navigation, Camping). Place them in the team area.  
2. Add to team area:  
   - 3 Ammunition tokens  
   - 4 Food tokens (5 in Hard mode)  
3. Assign Health:  
   - **Easy/Normal**: 4 Health tokens per explorer  
   - **Hard**: 3 Health tokens per explorer  
4. Lay out **Expedition cards** in a row, ending with the Lost City (card 9). Use only cards 3‚Äì9 (7 total) for Easy mode.  
5. Place **pawn** on the first card (opposite the Lost City).  
6. Place **Morning/Evening token** on "Morning".  
7. Shuffle **Adventure deck**. Deal:  
   - 4 cards to each player (2‚Äì4 players)  
   - 6 cards to each player (2 players only)  
8. Remaining deck is placed face-down. Discard pile starts empty.  
9. Give **Expedition Leader token** to starting player.  
10. In **5-player games**, remove 1 Food token from the team area after setup.

#### Solo Mode  
- Use same team: one of each expertise  
- 3 Ammunition, 3 Food, 3 Health per explorer  
- Lay out all 9 Expedition cards  
- Deal 6 Adventure cards to hand  
- Shuffle deck; play follows modified Morning/Evening rules

#### Head-to-Head Mode (2 players)  
- Remove **6 Adventure cards** with exclamation mark in title: #1, 8, 17, 33, 41, 52  
- Each player has:  
  - Own team of 3 explorers (one per expertise)  
  - 3 Ammunition, 3 Food, 4 Health per explorer  
  - Own pawn  
- Use **7 Expedition cards** (cards 3‚Äì9), both pawns start on first card  
- Deal 6 Adventure cards to each player  
- Flip leader token to determine first leader

### 4. Gameplay Flow  

A game consists of **rounds**, each representing one full day. Each round has two phases: **Morning** and **Evening**.

#### Cooperative & Solo Flow  
1. **Morning Phase**  
   - Starting with leader, players play **2 cards each** (3 in 2-player) face-up into central area.  
   - Cards are **rearranged in ascending numerical order**.  
   - Resolve cards **left to right**.  
   - After resolution, **discard** all cards unless kept for Expertise.  
   - **Spend 1 Food** (or lose 1 Health if no Food).  
   - Flip Morning/Evening token to **Evening**.

2. **Evening Phase**  
   - Players play **all remaining cards in hand**.  
   - Cards are added **in play order** (no reordering).  
   - Resolve left to right.  
   - **Spend 1 Food** (or lose 1 Health).  
   - Flip token back to **Morning**.

3. **End of Round**  
   - Pass **Expedition Leader token** clockwise.  
   - Each player draws **4 cards** (6 in 2-player).  
   - If deck is empty:  
     - First time: shuffle discard pile into new deck, **lose 1 Food** (or 1 Health).  
     - Second time: **players lose immediately**.

#### Solo Mode Specifics  
- **Morning**:  
  - Draw 2 cards from deck ‚Üí play 2 from hand ‚Üí draw 1 ‚Üí play 1.  
  - Total: 6 cards, **reordered by number**.  
- **Evening**:  
  - Play 1 from hand to start path.  
  - Then alternate: play 1 from hand **or** draw 1 from deck, placing it **left or right** of current path.  
  - Build 6-card path. Resolve in order.  
- Draw 6 cards at end of round.

#### Head-to-Head Mode Specifics  
- **Two Paths**: One above, one below Expedition row.  
- **Morning & Evening**:  
  - Players alternate playing 1 card at a time.  
  - Can join existing path or start new one.  
  - After all 6 cards played (3 per player), **reorder each path by number**.  
  - **Leader chooses which path to take**, OR lets opponent choose (opponent then chooses Evening path).  
  - Players resolve their path **independently**, but **simultaneously complete** their hike.  
- At end of round:  
  - Draw 6 cards.  
  - Pass leader token.

### 5. Core Mechanics  

#### Adventure Cards  
Each card has:  
- A **number** (bottom)  
- **Caption boxes** at top: Events (yellow), Choices (red), Options (blue)  

**Resolution Order**:  
1. **Events (Yellow)** ‚Äì Mandatory. All must be triggered, left to right.  
2. **Choices (Red)** ‚Äì Mandatory. Choose **exactly one** red box.  
3. **Options (Blue)** ‚Äì Optional. Any number may be triggered, in any order.

#### Symbols  

##### Resource Symbols  
- **Black Food (‚ñ†)**: Gain 1 Food  
- **White Food (‚ñ°)**: Spend 1 Food  
  - If no Food, lose 1 Health (any explorer)  
- **Black Bullet (‚óè)**: Gain 1 Ammunition  
- **White Bullet (‚óã)**: Spend 1 Ammunition  
  - Cannot spend if no Ammunition  
- **Black Cross (‚úö)**: Gain 1 Health on **any** explorer (max 4)  
- **White Cross (‚úó)**: Lose 1 Health from **any** explorer  

##### Expertise Symbols  
- **Black Skill (e.g., ‚ñ† Jungle)**: Gain Expertise ‚Üí Keep card in team area  
- **White Skill (e.g., ‚ñ° Jungle)**: Spend Expertise ‚Üí Choose one:  
  1. Discard a card with matching gain symbol  
  2. Lose 1 Health from explorer with matching expertise  
  3. Lose 2 Health from any **other** explorer (both from same)  
  - Cards with multiple gain symbols can be used once for **any** matching spend  
  - Cannot reuse a card

##### Path & Progress Symbols  
- **‚Üí (Right Arrow)**: Discard next card in path  
- **‚ü∑ (Crossed Arrow)**: **Optional** ‚Äì Swap any two cards in the path ahead of current card  
- **‚Äì (Minus)**: Discard last card in path (not current card)  
- **+ (Plus)**: Draw 1 card from deck, add to end of path  
- **üö∂ (Walking Figure)**: Move pawn to next Expedition card  
  - If this reaches Lost City ‚Üí **immediate win**  
  - Resolves **before** other symbols on card  
- **üíÄ (Skull)**: Remove **any one living explorer** from game  
  - Resolves **immediately**, before other symbols

#### Immediate Effects  
- **üö∂ and üíÄ** happen **before** other symbols on the same card.  
- This means you can win (via üö∂) even if later symbols would kill an explorer.

#### Health & Death  
- Max **4 Health** per explorer  
- If an explorer reaches **0 Health**, they die and are removed  
- If **all three explorers die**, players lose immediately

#### Deck Management  
- When deck is empty:  
  - First time: shuffle discard pile ‚Üí new deck, **lose 1 Food** (or 1 Health)  
  - Second time: **immediate loss**

### 6. Scoring & End Game  

#### End Conditions  
The game ends when **any** of the following occur:  
1. **Pawn reaches Lost City of Z** ‚Üí **Win** (cooperative/solo); **triggers win check** (head-to-head)  
2. **All three explorers die** ‚Üí **Lose**  
3. **Adventure deck runs out a second time** ‚Üí **Lose**

#### Head-to-Head Win Conditions  
- **If one player reaches Lost City**: that player wins  
- **If both reach in same phase**:  
  - Most surviving explorers wins  
  - Tie? Most cards left in current path wins  
  - Tie? Player who **did not start as leader** wins  
- **If all explorers die in same phase**:  
  - Player **closest to Lost City** wins  
  - Tie? Player who **did not start as leader** wins  
- **If deck runs out second time**:  
  - Player **closest to Lost City** wins  
  - Tie? Most surviving explorers wins  
  - Still tie? Player who **did not start as leader** wins

#### Solo Scoring  
- Score points only at end:  
  - 1 point per **remaining Food**  
  - 1 point per **remaining Ammunition**  
  - 1 point per **Expertise card in team area**  
  - **5 bonus points** if deck was never shuffled  
- Multiply total by **number of surviving explorers**  
- Higher score = better performance

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Can I spend Expertise instead of Health when out of Food?**  
  No. Only Health can substitute for Food.

- **Can I split 2 Health loss across two explorers when spending Expertise?**  
  No. Both Health tokens must come from **one** explorer.

- **Can I trigger a ‚Äòspend Ammunition‚Äô option if I have none?**  
  No. You cannot trigger any action requiring Ammunition if you have 0.

- **When do I lose Food?**  
  At the **end** of Morning and Evening phases, after resolving all cards.

- **Do I lose Food if the game ends during Morning/Evening?**  
  No. If a win/loss condition is met during card resolution, **stop immediately**. Do not spend Food.

- **Can I swap cards with the crossed arrow symbol after resolving a card?**  
  No. The swap must be done **when the symbol is triggered**, during resolution of its card.

- **Can I use a card with multiple Expertise gains more than once?**  
  No. Even with multiple symbols, it can only be used **once** when spending Expertise.

- **Does ‚Äòdiscard next card‚Äô skip the entire next card‚Äôs effects?**  
  Yes. The next card is removed from the path and not resolved.

- **Can I add a card to the path that causes a loop?**  
  No. The **+** symbol adds one card from the **deck** to the **end** of the path. No loops possible.
