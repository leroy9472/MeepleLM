### 3. Setup
- **General Setup**: When a creature tile is revealed, set up 1 creature on that tile. Some tiles may grant bonuses during setup.
- **War Banner**: Place the War Banner on the map tile where the creature is set up.
- **Creature Template & Stat Bar**: Use the matching Creature Template and Stat Bar. Abilities are generated during setup.
- **Minions**: Minions have 1 health, do not attack, and trap workers. They are immune to fire and poison. Any basic or special attack from a hero kills them.
- **Temporary Rules Cards**: Apply only when instructed. Examples:
  - **Card 001**: Place specified resources during setup. When defeating a creature, place 3 base resources + bonuses (1 per ●, 2 per ▲, 3 per ■) on the tile.
  - **Card 003/004**: Place red cube items on tiles or creature templates. Workers can pick up, move, and deliver items.
  - **Card 005**: After defeating a creature, immediately set up the next creature on the same tile.

### 4. Gameplay Flow
1. **Round Summary**:
   - Resolve story events.
   - Move Time Tracker.
   - Begin player turns.

2. **Player Turn**:
   - Resolve impairments (e.g., poison, curse).
   - Activate creatures.
   - Perform hero and worker actions.

3. **Creature Activation**:
   - Resolve impairments (e.g., Rupture, Enrage).
   - Apply +Heal (e.g., Lifeblood).
   - Perform basic attack and attack abilities (e.g., Knockback, Whirlwind).
   - Activate special abilities (★).
   - Resolve death effects (e.g., Call to Arms, Heart of Fire).

4. **Hero Attack/Heal**:
   - Choose a target within range.
   - Calculate damage: base attack + bonuses – defense.
   - Apply creature defend abilities (e.g., Green Blooded, Reflect All).
   - Deal damage.
   - Resolve effects (e.g., Blood Boil, Fury).

### 5. Core Mechanics

#### Universal Rules
- **Minions**: Immune to fire/poison; killed by any hero attack; trap workers.
- **Targeting**: If multiple targets are equidistant, players choose.
- **Hero Forced Movement**: Heroes stop on the first unexplored tile when moved across unexplored tiles. Moving over a pit causes the hero to fall in. Heroes take fire/poison damage along the path.

#### Creature Abilities (Alphabetical)
- **Ambush**: Set up a second creature; place its War Banner 2 tiles north of The City of Kings.
- **Bell Ringer**: Perform Darkvine All, then War Cry.
- **Berserk**: Perform Grab All, then Panic, then Move.
- **Blizzard**: Place strong Frost tokens on adjacent tiles, then Move.
- **Blood Boil**: When damaged, place a +1 Attack token on the creature.
- **Call to Arms**: When this creature dies, give +1 Attack and +1 Heal to all creatures.
- **Chain Lightning**: Hits priority target and all heroes on the same tile. Total damage based on player count (2: 4, 3: 6, 4: 8). Divided equally (rounded up).
- **Charge**: Teleport to priority target, then Knockback.
- **Curse**: Place 1 Curse token on priority target (limits healing to 50%). Max 1 per hero.
- **Darkvine**: Place 1 Darkvine token on priority target's tile.
- **Darkvine All**: Place 1 Darkvine on all hero tiles within range.
- **Darkvine Counter**: When damaged, place 1 Darkvine on attacker’s tile.
- **Eerie Howl**: All heroes within range must move to the closest tile outside the creature’s range.
- **Elemental Lance**: Place 1 strong Fire, Frost, and Poison token on each tile between creature and edge of map in Time Tracker direction.
- **Enrage**: Place +1 Attack token on creature. Occurs every activation.
- **Fire Ball**: Place 1 strong Fire token on priority target’s tile. Overrides Fire Bolt.
- **Fire Ball All**: Place 1 strong Fire token on all hero tiles within range. Overrides Fire Bolt and Fire Ball.
- **Fire Bolt**: Place 1 weak Fire token on priority target’s tile.
- **Fire Breath**: Grab All, then place strong Fire tokens around the creature.
- **Fire Trail**: Draw Position card, move creature. Place strong Fire tokens on all tiles traveled (including start and end).
- **Fire Lance**: Place 1 strong Fire token on each tile between creature and edge of map in Time Tracker direction.
- **Fire Wall**: Place strong Fire tokens in a pattern around the creature (see reference images).
- **Frost Bolt**: Place 1 weak Frost token on priority target’s tile.
- **Frost Lance**: Place 1 strong Frost token on each tile between creature and edge of map in Time Tracker direction.
- **Frost Storm**: Place 1 strong Frost token on priority target’s tile. Overrides Frost Bolt.
- **Frost Storm All**: Place 1 strong Frost token on all hero tiles within range. Overrides Frost Bolt and Frost Storm.
- **Fury**: When damaged, draw and place a green ability token on the creature.
- **Grab**: Pull priority target to creature’s location. Hero takes damage from fire/poison and may fall into pits.
- **Grab All**: Pull all heroes in range to creature’s location. Same movement rules apply.
- **Green Blooded**: Reduce incoming damage by 1. Attacker loses 1 health.
- **Heart of Fire**: When creature dies, deal damage equal to its Attack to all heroes in range.
- **Heart of the Phoenix**: Heart of Fire, then Phoenix effect.
- **Horde**: Draw Position card. Set up lowest stat bar from discard pile at indicated location (creature with Horde is X).
- **Hounds**: If none exist, spawn Hounds token using Position card. If exist, move 1 tile toward closest worker (ignore City of Kings).
- **Hunting Party**: Set up a second creature with same War Banner. No abilities given.
- **Knockback**: Push priority target back number of tiles shown. If same tile, use Position card to determine direction.
- **Lifeblood**: Place +1 Heal token on creature. Occurs every activation.
- **Lightning Bolt**: Deal 1 damage to priority target.
- **Move**: Draw Position card, move creature. Takes fire/poison damage. Immune to pits. Can move between unexplored tiles.
- **Overlord**: Activate Hounds, Shredders, Witch Doctor, Vilelings in order.
- **Panic**: All heroes in range draw Position card and move to indicated spot.
- **Patrol**: Move 1 tile toward Time Tracker direction.
- **Phoenix**: Creature revives with 50% max health. Remove all Phoenix tokens. Lose all tokens/impairments. Gain XP on both kills. Quests require second death.
- **Poisoned Blade**: Place 1 weak Poison token directly on priority target’s Character Sheet.
- **Poison Bolt**: Place 1 weak Poison token on priority target’s tile.
- **Poison Cloud**: Place strong Poison tokens around the creature.
- **Poison Lance**: Place 1 strong Poison token on each tile between creature and edge of map in Time Tracker direction.
- **Poison Spit**: Place 1 strong Poison token on priority target’s tile. Overrides Poison Bolt.
- **Poison Spit All**: Place 1 strong Poison token on all hero tiles within range. Overrides Poison Bolt and Poison Spit.
- **Poison Trail**: Draw Position card, move creature. Place strong Poison tokens on all tiles traveled.
- **Red Hot**: Basic attack deals +50% damage (rounded up) if target is on a Fire token.
- **Reflect All**: When damaged, all heroes in range take damage equal to value on token.
- **Rupture**: Reduce creature’s health by 3. Occurs every activation. XP awarded if death results.
- **Search Party**: Place 1 Attention token on each Resource tile within range that has at least 1 worker.
- **Shredders**: If none, spawn using Position card. If exist, move 1 tile toward closest creature. If on same tile as creature, sacrifice and give +1 Attack to all creatures on tile.
- **Swipe**: Deal 2 damage to priority target and all heroes on same tile.
- **Teleport**: Creature instantly moves to priority target’s location. Ignores board effects.
- **Temptation**: All workers in range move 1 tile toward creature (including trapped). Workers in pits unaffected.
- **Tracking**: Move 1 tile toward closest hero (ignore City of Kings). If all heroes there, do not move.
- **Vilelings**: If none, spawn using Position card. If exist, move 1 tile toward closest hero. If lands on hero tile, explode: deal damage (2p:4, 3p:6, 4p:8). Does not explode on spawn or when hero moves in.
- **War Cry**: If no priority target, place 1 Notice token on creature’s tile.
- **Whirlwind**: Basic attack hits priority target, then hits all heroes within Whirlwind range for 50% damage (rounded up). Range = value on token.
- **Witch Doctor**: If none, spawn using Position card. If exist, move 1 tile toward closest creature. If on same tile, sacrifice and give +1 Heal to all creatures on tile.

#### Minion Behavior
- **Hounds/Shredders/Witch Doctor/Vilelings**: Spawned and moved by abilities. Trap workers. Killed by any hero attack.
- **Vilelings Explosion**: Only when Vilelings move into hero, not vice versa.

#### Map Tile Effects
- **Buff**: Temporary stat bonus shown on tile.
- **Farm**: Workers spend wood for linen (Phase 1), then upgrade worker stats (Phase 2), then heroes trade resources (Phase 3).
- **Shrine**: Workers spend fish to add skill cards (1), then take skill discs (2), then heroes gain +3 Attack/Heal (3).
- **Stronghold**: Workers spend ore to add equipment (1), then kill creature (2), then heroes trade for equipment (3).
- **Creature Tile**: Reveal → set up creature; workers can scavenge.
- **Pit**: Characters fall in, cannot move. Freed by adjacent hero Interact action or hero self-sacrifice (critical damage).
- **Quest Tile**: Hero reveal or Interact → draw 1 Quest card.
- **Resource Tile**: Workers gather resources.
- **Shop**: Reveal → add 3 Equipment cards to Trade District. Hero Interact → buy with resources.
- **Stable**: Heroes treat all Stables and City of Kings as adjacent for movement.
- **The City of Kings**: Starting area; safe zone (creatures don’t move if all heroes are here).

#### Tokens
- **Fire**: Weak = 1 damage, Strong = 2. Triggers on contact/start of turn.
- **Frost**: Weak = -1 movement, Strong = -2. Affects movement only.
- **Poison**: Weak = 1 damage/turn, Strong = 2. Placed on Character Sheet.
- **Curse**: -50% healing. Max 1 per character.
- **Attention**: 4 tokens → spawn creature. Reset on spawn.
- **Attack/Heal/Shield**: Modify creature stats.

### 6. Scoring & End Game
- **XP**: Awarded when defeating creatures. Phoenix creatures grant XP twice.
- **Quest Completion**: Rewards given when quest conditions met (e.g., kill, escort, scavenge).
- **Loss Conditions**:
  - All heroes defeated.
  - Last item destroyed (Temporary Rule 004).
- **Win Conditions**:
  - Complete main quest objectives.
  - Survive story arc.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Priority Target Conflicts**: If multiple heroes equidistant, player chooses.
- **Token Limits**: Only one Fire/Frost/Poison token per tile. Strong replaces weak.
- **Phoenix Revival**: Creature returns with 50% health, no tokens. Original token removed even if multi-ability.
- **Vilelings Explosion**: Only triggers when Vilelings token moves into hero, not when hero moves.
- **Grab/Knockback over Pits**: Hero falls in and stops.
- **Unexplored Tiles**: Forced movement stops at first unexplored tile.
- **Simultaneous Poison Exposure**: Each affected hero takes a token.
- **Overlapping Abilities**: Stronger versions override (e.g., Fire Ball All > Fire Ball > Fire Bolt).
