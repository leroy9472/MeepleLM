## PART 1 — Comprehensive Reference Manual

1. Lore & Objective  
Each player leads a 19th-century railroad company competing to expand their rail network and contribute to the transcontinental telegraph. The goal is to earn the most Victory Points (VP) by building stations, extending railroads, constructing telegraph offices, completing company milestones, advancing on performance tracks, and leveraging train tiles.

2. Components  
- 1 Game Board (with city map, telegraph line, performance tracks)  
- 4 Player Boards  
- 64 Workers: 16 White, 12 Purple, 12 Orange, 12 Grey, 12 Turquoise  
- 52 Buildings (13 per player color)  
- 68 Rails (17 per player color)  
- 12 Performance Markers (3 per player)  
- 48 Telegraph Shares ($150 value each)  
- 6 Telegraph Tiles  
- 13 City Tiles  
- 24 Train Tiles (including 4 "Promote 2 Workers" types)  
- 38 Milestone Tiles (sorted by A1/A2, B, C, D)  
- 9 Deal Tiles  
- 4 Setup Tiles (double-sided)  
- 1 First Player Tile  
- 52 Banknotes: $50 (10), $100 (26), $500 (12), $1000 (4)  

3. Setup  
- General Board Setup:  
  - Place Game Board centrally.  
  - Sort and randomly place City Tiles face-up on matching-numbered city slots.  
  - Shuffle Deal Tiles, place face-down stack, flip top tile face-up.  

- Player Setup:  
  - Each player chooses a color, takes matching Player Board, 12 Buildings, 15 Rails.  
  - Place all Buildings and Rails on top slots of Player Board.  
  - Place 1 extra Rail (from reserve) between Washington and Charlotte.  
  - Each player receives: $600, 4 White Workers, 3 Telegraph Shares, 1 "Promote 2 Workers" Train Tile.  
  - Place one Performance Marker per player on bottom space of each of the three Performance Tracks.  

- Milestones:  
  - Sort Milestone Tiles by letter/number. Shuffle each stack.  
  - Each player takes one A1 and one A2 Milestone, places face-up. Return extras to box.  

- Trains:  
  - Separate 4 "Promote 2 Workers" Train Tiles. Give one to each player; return extras.  
  - Form 4 stacks of remaining Train Types:  
    - 4P: 5 per stack  
    - 3P: 4 per stack  
    - 2P: 3 per stack  

- Telegraph:  
  - Shuffle 6 Telegraph Tiles, place one face-up on each Telegraph section without a Deal icon.  
  - 3P: Block 1st Office slot in 2nd and 5th sections with dummy building.  
  - 2P: Block 1st Office slot in 1st, 4th, and 7th sections.  

- Setup Tiles:  
  - Randomly select number of Setup Tiles equal to player count. Randomize face-up side.  
  - Randomly assign one Worker (one of each color) to each Setup Tile.  
  - Starting with player to right of First Player (counter-clockwise), each picks a Setup Tile, takes its reward and Worker.  
  - First Player receives First Player Tile.  

- Special Blocking (for 2P/3P):  
  - 3P: Place dummy buildings on 1st Station slots of San Francisco, Bismarck, Houston.  
  - 2P: Place dummy buildings on 1st Station slots of San Diego, Salt Lake City, Duluth, Little Rock.  

4. Gameplay Flow  
- Play proceeds clockwise starting with First Player.  
- On your turn:  
  1. If no Workers in personal supply, retrieve all from your board (free action).  
  2. Take 1 Worker from personal supply.  
  3. Place it on one of the four action spaces on your Player Board.  
  4. Perform the Main Action.  
  5. Optionally use the Additional Effect based on Worker color.  
- Effects may be resolved in any order (e.g., gain money before paying cost).  
- After action, if a Deal Icon was activated (via Rail or Telegraph action), resolve the current Deal Tile at end of turn.  

5. Core Mechanics  
- Main Actions:  
  - STATION:  
    - Take leftmost Building, place in connected city.  
    - Pay city cost, take primary reward (right side).  
    - If first to build: place in 1st Station slot, pay cost (if any), take 1st Station bonus (left side).  
    - Max 1 station per player per city.  

  - RAILROAD:  
    - Take two leftmost Rails, place in connected rail spaces.  
    - Pay $400 + $100 per Difficult Terrain (triangle) icon adjacent to placement.  
    - Exception: If only 1 Rail remains, may place it paying full $400 + terrain cost.  
    - Rails need not connect to each other, but each must connect to your network.  
    - Deal Icon: Triggers Deal resolution if rail placed there. Only one Deal per turn.  

  - TELEGRAPH:  
    - Take leftmost Building, place in available Telegraph section.  
    - Gain primary reward (Telegraph Shares, shown in brown).  
    - If first: place in 1st Office slot, gain bonus Shares (white number).  
    - May discard any 1 Worker to claim bonus on Telegraph Tile in that section.  
    - Deal Icon: Triggers Deal resolution if office placed there.  

  - TRADE:  
    - Discard 1 asset (rail or building) from leftmost occupied section of Player Board.  
    - Gain money shown above that section.  
    - May flip one Train Tile before or after sale.  
      - Face-up → receive bonus, flip face-down.  
      - Face-down → flip face-up (no immediate bonus).  

- Worker Abilities (Additional Effects):  
  - White (Non-specialized): Promote 1 Worker (move from supply/board to Milestone vacancy).  
  - Purple (Foreman): Grants VP or bonuses.  
  - Orange (Accountant): Reduces cost or gives money.  
  - Grey (Negotiator): Grants in-game benefits.  
  - Turquoise (Engineer): Enhances main action.  
  - Rule: Any Worker can be used as White (for promotion).  

- Deals:  
  - Triggered when a player places a Rail or Telegraph Office on a space with a Deal Icon.  
  - At end of turn, resolve face-up Deal Tile.  
  - Active player may take one or both deals.  
  - Other players, in clockwise order, may take one of the two deals.  
  - Each deal costs 1–4 Shares, gives a reward (e.g., Worker exchange, rail placement, milestone progress).  
  - After resolution, discard tile and flip next. Reshuffle discard if stack empty.  

- Milestones:  
  - Each has 1–3 conditions and requires 1–2 Managers (promoted Workers).  
  - Promote a Worker by using White Worker ability or specific action rewards.  
  - Promoted Workers are fixed on tile until completion.  
  - Completion: At end of turn, if conditions met and Managers placed, flip tile face-down, return Managers, draw new Milestone:  
    - Draw 3 from next letter stack (e.g., A → B), choose 1, return others to bottom.  
    - D Milestones: No replacement.  
  - Conditions include: connected cities by level, stations built, rails on Deal spaces, terrain types.  

- Performance Tracks (3):  
  - Level 5 City Track: VP multiplier for number of connected Level 5 cities.  
  - Station Track: VP multiplier for number of stations built.  
  - Telegraph Track: VP multiplier for number of telegraph offices built.  
  - Advancing: Use brick-with-arrow icons from action bonuses.  
    - May split advancements across tracks.  
    - To pass a cost icon (money, Shares, Worker), pay required cost.  

- General Rules:  
  - Minimum 4 Workers: Cannot perform any action or effect that reduces available Workers (on board + supply) below 4.  
  - Selling Shares: At any time, sell Telegraph Shares for $150 each.  
  - Hiring Workers: At any time, pay $800 to gain 1 Worker of any color.  
  - Train Tiles: Unlimited ownership; duplicates allowed. Place face-up (8 VP side) when gained.  
  - Order of Resolution: Effects may be resolved in any order, including paying after gaining funds.  

6. Scoring & End Game  
- Game End: Triggered when any player removes all 13 rails and 10 buildings from left of company logo.  
  - Complete current round (until player to right of First Player).  
  - Then each player takes one final turn.  
- Final Scoring:  
  - 8 VP per face-up Train Tile.  
  - VP for each completed Milestone (value on tile).  
  - Performance Tracks:  
    - Level 5 Cities: # connected × track VP level  
    - Stations: # built × track VP level  
    - Telegraph Offices: # built × track VP level  
  - Telegraph Connections:  
    - 5 or 8 VP per bar between sections if you have offices in both adjacent sections.  
    - Offices can be reused for multiple connections.  
- Total VP. Highest wins.  
- Tiebreaker: Most money (cash + $150 per Share). If still tied, shared victory.  

7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- Can I use a colored Worker as White? Yes, always.  
- Can I resolve effects out of order? Yes, including gaining money before paying.  
- Can I trigger two Deals in one turn? No, only one Deal per turn even with multiple triggers.  
- Can I promote a Worker without completing Milestone conditions? Yes.  
- Can I have more than one station in a city? No, only one per player.  
- Can I place rails not connected to each other? Yes, as long as each connects to your network.  
- What if a Worker color is out of supply? Gain a Worker of any other color.  
- Can I sell a face-down Train Tile? No, only flip or gain VP at end.  
- Can I use Telegraph Tile bonus if not first? Yes, as long as you build in that section.  
- Can I complete a Milestone after my final turn? Yes, if Deals in final round enable it.
