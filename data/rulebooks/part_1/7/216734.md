## Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective  
While no specific lore is provided, the objective of Dice Throne is to defeat all opposing players by reducing their Health to 0. The last player standing wins. In a 1v1 game, each player starts with 50 Health.

### 3. Setup  
1. Place Hero Board, Hero Leaflet, and tokens in front of you.  
2. Set Health Dial to 50 (1v1).  
3. Set CP Dial to 2.  
4. Shuffle your deck and draw 4 cards for your starting hand.  
5. Roll 1 die; highest roller becomes Start Player and skips their first Income Phase.  
6. Check Hero Leaflet for any hero-specific setup (e.g., placing Companion tokens).

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
Each turn follows these phases in order:  
1. **Upkeep Phase** â€“ Resolve upkeep effects (e.g., Burn, Poison).  
2. **Income Phase** â€“ Gain 1 CP and draw 1 card. *Start Player skips this on their first turn.*  
3. **Main Phase (1)** â€“ Play Upgrade or Main Phase Action cards, or sell cards for 1 CP each.  
4. **Offensive Roll Phase** â€“ Roll dice up to 3 times; activate one Offensive Ability if possible.  
5. **Targeting Roll Phase** â€“ *Only in games with >2 players:* Roll to determine target.  
6. **Defensive Roll Phase** â€“ If an Attack was made, the Defender rolls to activate their Defensive Ability.  
7. **Main Phase (2)** â€“ Same as Main Phase (1).  
8. **Discard Phase** â€“ Sell cards until you have 6 or fewer in hand.

#### **Status Effects**  
- Represented by tokens placed on the Hero Board.  
- **Gain/Inflict**: Take token and place it on the affected playerâ€™s Hero Board.  
- **Spendable**: Can be spent for an effect, then discarded. Usable at any time unless restricted.  
- **Persistent**: Lasts until removed by another effect.  
- **Stack Limit**: Maximum number of a given Status Effect on one hero (e.g., Evasive has a limit of 5).  
- **Unique**: Break standard rules; defined on the Hero Leaflet.

#### **Companions**  
- Hero-specific components (not Status Effects).  
- Immune to effects that alter Status Effects.  
- Rules found on Hero Leaflet.

#### **Dice Mechanics**  
- Roll up to 3 times per Offensive Roll Phase.  
- Final Dice Result = final state after all rerolls and modifications.  
- Key combinations:  
  - **2/3/4/5-of-a-kind**: Matching numbers (not symbols).  
  - **Small Straight**: 4 sequential numbers (e.g., 1-2-3-4).  
  - **Large Straight**: 5 sequential numbers (e.g., 2-3-4-5-6).  
- **On [symbol]**: Trigger effect if symbol appears (once per roll, even if multiple).

#### **Key Phases & Interruptions**  
- **Then**: Creates a pause. Players may spend Status Effects or play Instant/Roll Phase cards. Effects after "Then" resolve afterward.  
- **Additionally**: Effects after this word resolve after all others.  
- **Or**: Choose one option.  
- **Timing Conflicts**: Resolve in clockwise order starting with Active Player. Can be interrupted by Instant/Roll Phase cards or Spendable Status Effects.

#### **Ultimate Ability**  
- Cannot be interrupted, prevented, or responded to.  
- Ignores Status Effects that would reduce its effectiveness.  
- Can be enhanced (e.g., with Attack Modifiers).  
- Only way to stop: alter die roll *before* activation.

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
- **Victory**: Be the last player with Health > 0.  
- **Draw**: All remaining players reduced to 0 Health simultaneously.  
- No traditional scoring; game ends when only one player remains.
