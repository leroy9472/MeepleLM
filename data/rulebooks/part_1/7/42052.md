## Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective  
Vinhos is a strategic board game set in the world of Portuguese winemaking. Players take on the role of vintners managing estates across Portugal’s wine regions over six years (rounds). The objective is to earn the most Victory Points (VP) through wine production, export, sales, fair performance, and end-game bonuses.

Victory is determined by:
- Points from exported wines (red shield VP)
- Points from foreign market majorities
- Bank account balance
- End-game manager multiplier bonuses
- Fair Points (tiebreaker)

### 2. Components  
- Game board showing action grid, wine regions, fair track, and turn order
- Player boards (each with 4 estates, 3 slots per estate for vineyards/wineries, and wine storage)
- Vineyard tiles (by region)
- Winery tiles
- Cellar tiles
- Enologist tokens
- Wine Expert tiles (4 decks by feature)
- Fair tiles (3 per player)
- Vintage tiles (numbered 0–6)
- Renown cubes (15 total)
- Porto tiles (Douro region)
- Discs (for VP, Fair Points, turn order, investment)
- Barrels (for exports, sales, manager relationships)
- Action markers (1 per player)
- Feature markers (for tracking fair trends)
- Bagos (currency)

### 3. Setup  
#### General Setup  
1. **Vintage Tiles**: Set aside tile 0. Shuffle 1–6, place them face-down, then put 0 on top.
2. **Wine Fair**: Place one feature marker in each row of column 0 on the fair track.
3. **Player Cash**:
   - First half (rounded up) of players: 11,000 Bagos
   - Remaining players: 12,000 Bagos
4. **Wine Expert Decks**: Create 4 face-up decks by feature type; shuffle each.
5. **Tiles on Board** (based on player count):
   - Cellars: 9 (4p), 7 (3p), 5 (2p)
   - Wineries: 16 (4p), 12 (3p), 8 (2p)
   - Vineyards: 8 (4p), 7 (3p), 6 (2p) — place in region spaces
6. **Porto Tiles**: Place in Douro region space.
7. **Renown Cubes**: Place 15 on the wine bottle region.

#### Each Player  
1. Take:
   - 1 player board
   - 9 discs (color-matched)
   - 8 barrels
   - 1 action marker
   - 3 fair tiles
2. Place 5 discs:
   - 0 VP disc at 0 on VP track
   - 1 disc in random turn order slot (top row)
   - 0 Fair Points disc at 0 on FP track
   - 1 disc at 5 on Banco do Vinho (bank account)
   - 1 disc at +1 on investment bar
3. Place action marker in center space (Pass / Press Release).
4. Buy 1 Vineyard:
   - Pay cost in Bagos
   - Place tile in estate
   - Place disc if first estate in region
   - Add 1 renown cube to region

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
Each year (round) consists of the following phases:

1. **New Vintage Tile**  
   - Discard old tile, draw new one
   - Advance feature markers as indicated

2. **Flip Wine Experts Face-Up**  
   - After fair phases, flip all spent Wine Expert tiles face-up

3. **Action 1 & Action 2**  
   - Players take turns in turn order
   - Move action marker to an action space
   - Pay movement costs:
     - 1,000 Bagos per piece of wood in target space
     - 1,000 Bagos extra for moving 2 spaces
   - Pay action cost
   - Perform action

4. **Maintenance**  
   - Adjust bank accounts (interest)
   - Pay Enologist salaries from bank accounts

5. **Wine Production**  
   - Shift existing wine tiles one slot right (discard if no space)
   - Move Enologists freely among estates
   - Each estate produces 1 wine in leftmost slot:
     - Production Value (PV) = # Vineyards + # Wineries + # Enologists
     - Wine Value (WV) = PV + weather modifier + regional effects

6. **Wine Fair (Years 3, 5, 6 only)**  
   - In turn order, players who haven't done a Press Release do so
   - Steps:
     a. Choose wine
     b. Determine final value (add renown cubes if desired)
     c. Send press release (place fair tile in slot)
     d. Gain bonus (bag, expert tile, or fair points)
     e. Gain fair points for matching features
     f. Optionally establish up to 2 manager relationships (place barrel)
     g. Discard wine
   - All players simultaneously:
     a. Secretly choose Wine Expert tiles (must match fair slot features)
     b. Reveal
     c. Advance feature markers per tile effects
     d. Gain fair points per feature column
     e. Award VP based on fair ranking
     f. Discard used Wine Expert tiles
     g. Last place (4p game): may take 1 free Wine Expert
   - Retrieve fair tiles

7. **Reset Turn Order**  
   - New order: last to first by Fair Points
   - Ties retain previous order

### 5. Core Mechanics  

#### Actions  
Available when moving action marker to corresponding space:

- **Buy Vineyard (1–2 times)**  
  - Pay cost (1,000–5,000 Bagos)
  - Take top vineyard tile from region
  - Place in estate
  - If new region estate, place disc and add renown cube

- **Build Cellar (1 time)**  
  - Pay 2,000 Bagos
  - Replace warehouse with cellar (holds 4 wines)
  - Transfer wines to same positions
  - Add 1 renown cube to region

- **Build Winery (1–2 times)**  
  - Pay 3,000 Bagos per winery
  - Place in empty estate slot
  - Add 1 renown cube to region

- **Hire Enologist (1–2 times)**  
  - Pay 1,000 Bagos each
  - Place in empty winery slot
  - Salary paid during Maintenance

- **Hire Wine Expert (1–2 times)**  
  - Pay 1,000 Bagos each
  - Take top tile from any feature deck
  - Place face-up in front
  - Max 6 experts; discard extras to bottom of decks

- **Export Wine (unlimited)**  
  - Choose wine
  - Optionally remove 1–2 renown cubes from region to increase value
  - Value must meet or exceed export slot value
  - Discard wine
  - Place barrel in slot
  - Gain red-shield VP

- **Sell Wine (unlimited)**  
  - Choose wine (color must match sale slot)
  - Optionally remove 1–2 renown cubes to increase value
  - Value must meet or exceed sale slot value
  - Discard wine
  - Add slot value to bank account

- **Bank (unlimited)**  
  - Withdraw: move bank marker down, take cash
  - Deposit: discard cash, move marker up
  - Invest: pay cost to move investment marker up

- **Pass / Press Release (1–2 times)**  
  - Move turn order disc to second row
  - Choose to pass or make press release (see Fair steps)

#### Optional Actions (Before/After Any Action)  
- **Reclaim Barrels**: If 2 same-color barrels in one hangout (Casa de Fados, Hotel, Enoteca), return both to hand; add 1 renown cube to each of 2 regions
- **Divest**: Move investment marker down; gain indicated cash
- **Use Manager Relationship**: Discard 1 wine; move 1 barrel (not on lock space) in same row; apply effect
- **Use Wine Expert Ability**: Turn face-down to activate ability (e.g., -1,000 to vineyard cost, free movement, +2,000 cash, etc.)

#### Regional Effects  
Each region provides a unique benefit when establishing first estate:

1. **Minho**: Cannot build cellars
2. **Trás-os-Montes**: +2 Fair Points when presenting wine at fair
3. **Dao**: Free cellar (no renown cube)
4. **Douro**: Receive 2 Porto tiles; spend 1 to boost Douro wine PV by +3
5. **Lisboa**: Free winery (no renown cube)
6. **Alentejo**: Renown cubes are worth double when boosting wine value
7. **Setúbal**: Take 2 free Wine Expert tiles
8. **Algarve**: Wines always +1 Wine Value

Note: Second estate in same region still triggers regional effect.

#### Aging & Spoilage  
- Before production, shift all wine tiles one slot right
- Wines with no space to shift are spoiled and discarded
- Cellars increase wine value over time: +1 WV per year aged (max +5 in fourth year)

#### Renown Usage  
- Remove 1–2 renown cubes from wine’s region to increase its value
- Only allowed if it enables:
  - Better fair tile
  - Meeting Beatrix’s requirement
  - Exceeding 9WV to gain Fair Points
- Cannot spend solely to block others

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
#### Game End  
After Year 6 fair and turn order reset:

1. **Final Manager Actions**  
   - Players take turns (current turn order)
   - Each may discard 1 wine to activate a manager relationship
   - Continue until all pass

2. **Final Scoring**  
   - **Export VP**: Red-shield points from export slots
   - **Foreign Markets**: Majority in each column scores VP (tie: split, round down)
   - **Bank Balance**: 1 VP per 1,000 Bagos
   - **Manager Multipliers**: Score VP based on end-game multiplier spaces

3. **Winning**  
   - Highest VP wins
   - Tiebreakers:
     1. Most barrels exported
     2. Most total money (cash + bank) after divesting

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Can I use renown cubes to exceed export slot value?**  
  No. Renown may only increase value to *match* the slot, not exceed it.

- **Can I use a Wine Expert ability after performing an action?**  
  Yes. You may use abilities at any point during your turn.

- **What happens if I can’t pay Enologist salary?**  
  You must pay from bank account during Maintenance. If insufficient funds, you cannot hire Enologists beyond your ability to pay.

- **Can I reclaim barrels from different hangouts?**  
  No. Must be two barrels of same color in *same* hangout.

- **Do regional effects stack for multiple estates?**  
  Yes. Each new estate in a region triggers the effect again.

- **Can I divest multiple times?**  
  Yes. Anytime, as often as desired.

- **Can I use Porto tile on non-Douro wine?**  
  No. Only on Douro wines.

- **Can I use renown from one region to boost wine from another?**  
  No. Only from the wine’s own region.
