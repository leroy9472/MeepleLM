## Comprehensive Reference Manual

1. Lore & Objective  
The Amazon Rainforest is one of Earth’s most biodiverse ecosystems. In *Life of the Amazonia*, players run conservation organizations to restore and cultivate a thriving jungle. The goal is to earn the highest Environmental Score by strategically placing animals, trees, flowers, and terrain, and managing resources. The player with the most points at game end wins, earning the Good Comet Society’s Grand Prize.

2. Components  
- Animal Meeples:  
  5 Jaguars, 12 Leaf Frogs, 5 Giant Otters, 12 Tamarins, 6 Macaws, 8 Toucans, 6 Caimans, 1 Anteater, 1 Amazon River Dolphin, 1 Anaconda, 1 Hoatzin, 1 Sloth, 1 River Turtle, 1 Iguana, 1 Armadillo, 12 Woodpeckers  
- Player Components:  
  16 Player Markers, 4 Resource Bags, 4 Discard Pile Boats  
- Tiles & Cards:  
  4 Starting Terrain Tiles, 36 Terrain Tiles, 21 Special Terrain Tiles, 16 Base Animal Cards, 8 Unique Animal Cards, 48 Insect Cards, 42 Scenery Cards  
- Tokens:  
  42 Trees, 42 Aquatic Flowers, 16 Seeds  
  Resource Tokens:  
  - Currency: 12 (1), 20 (2), 12 (3), 8 (4)  
  - Leaf: 8 (1), 14 (2), 12 (3), 10 (4)  
  - Water: 8 (1), 14 (2), 12 (3), 10 (4)  
  - Fruit: 18 (2), 12 (3), 10 (4)  
  Bonus/Tracking Tokens:  
  8 "Automa Tokens", 1 End Game Bonus Token, 5 Complete Tokens  
- Game Board & Accessories:  
  1 Waterfall of Life (assembled from supports and waterfall piece), 2 Token Volumes (vaults), 1 Scorepad, 4 Player Aids

3. Setup  
- Place the Waterfall of Life and Token Volumes centrally.  
- Select a Base Animal Card set (A–D); place 8 cards face-up. Return others to box.  
- Place corresponding Animal Meeples near each Base Animal Card.  
- Place Complete Tokens and End Game Bonus Token near Animal Refuge.  
- Shuffle Terrain Tiles (excluding 4 Starting Tiles); place face-down as deck. Draw 3 to form Terrain Tile Pool.  
- Place Seeds, Trees, Aquatic Flowers, and Special Terrain Tiles in Supply.  
- Shuffle Insect and Scenery Card decks. Draw 3 of each to form face-up pools.  
- Each player:  
  - Takes a Starting Terrain Tile (random), places it in front.  
  - Chooses a color: takes matching Resource Bag, Discard Pile Boat, and 4 Player Markers.  
  - Places 10 starting tokens in Resource Bag: 2× (2 Currency), 1× (1 Leaf), 1× (1 Water), 2× (2 Fruit).  
  - Places 4 Player Markers on first space of each Waterfall of Life track (Terrain, Tree, Flower, Storage).  
  - Draws 2 Unique Animal Cards, chooses 1, places it face-up. Returns unchosen to box.  
  - Takes corresponding Unique Animal Meeple, places on card.  
  - If chosen Unique Animal grants a Starting Seed, takes 1 Seed.  
- In 3–4 player games, third and later players receive 1 Seed.  
- First player is the one who most recently watched a nature documentary.  
- Each player draws 5 tokens from their Resource Bag to their Play Area.

4. Gameplay Flow  
- Players take turns clockwise.  
- Each turn has two phases:  
  1. Action Phase: Perform any number of actions in any order, paying required costs.  
  2. Clean-up Phase: Store up to Storage Track limit in Play Area; discard excess. Draw 5 new tokens from Bag (reset Bag if empty).  
- Game ends when 5 of 8 Base Animal types are depleted. The player who places the last of the 5th type takes the End Game Bonus Token and becomes Final Player. All other players take one more turn. Then scoring begins.

5. Core Mechanics  
- Resource Tokens:  
  - Stored in Resource Bag; drawn to Play Area each turn.  
  - 4 types: Currency, Leaf, Water, Fruit; values 1–4.  
  - Seeds can substitute for any 1-value resource (worth 1).  
- Actions (8 types, pay to perform):  
  1. Purchase a Resource Token: Pay cost; take token of desired type/value from Vault to Discard Pile Boat.  
  2. Place a Terrain Tile: Move marker right on Terrain Track; pay cost; take 1 of 3 face-up tiles; place connected by ≥2 hexes; refill pool.  
  3. Place a Tree: Move marker right on Tree Track; pay cost; place on unoccupied Forest hex.  
  4. Place an Aquatic Flower: Move marker right on Flower Track; pay cost; place on unoccupied River hex.  
  5. Place an Animal: Pay cost shown; place Base Animal from Refuge or Unique Animal (if conditions met).  
  6. Purchase a Nature Card: Pay cost; take 1 face-up Scenery or Insect Card.  
     - Scenery Cards: Keep, score at end. Max 4.  
     - Insect Cards: Use immediately, discard.  
  7. Expand Storage: Move marker right on Storage Track; pay cost. Increases max tokens saved in Play Area (1→3).  
  8. Purchase a Bonus: Pay cost; gain 1 of: Relocate, Compost, Place Special Terrain Tile, Gain Seed, Draw, Renew.  
     - Can mix bonus types in a single payment.  
     - Only one of each bonus type per turn.  
- Bonuses:  
  - Relocate: Move a Life (not terrain or Unique Animal) to valid hex. No Placement Bonus.  
  - Compost: Remove a token (Play Area or Discard Boat) from game.  
  - Place Special Terrain Tile: Place from Supply; can replace another Special Tile.  
  - Gain Seed: Take 1 Seed (max 4).  
  - Draw: Draw 1 token from Bag to Play Area.  
  - Renew: Discard 1–2 cards/tiles from pools; refill.  
- Bag Reset: When Bag is empty and draw is needed, return Discard Boat tokens to Bag.  
- Placement Rules:  
  - Hex: Each hex holds one Life (Animal, Tree, Flower).  
  - Terrain: Forest (Trees), River (Flowers), Marsh.  
  - Habitat: Connected hexes of same terrain. Size matters for scoring.  
  - Grouping: Specific shape of Lives (e.g., L-shape). Shapes can be rotated/reflected.  
  - Community: Adjacent Lives of certain types. Shape irrelevant.  
- Unique Animals:  
  - Chosen at start; only owner can use.  
  - Placed without cost if terrain and unique conditions met.  
  - Abilities usable once per turn after placement.  
  - Examples:  
    - Hoatzin: Discount 1 Tree or Flower placement per turn.  
    - Iguana: 1 Seed per turn counts as 2.  
    - Anaconda: 1 Animal placement per turn costs 1 less.

6. Scoring & End Game  
- Game ends when 5 Base Animal types are depleted. Final Player takes End Game Bonus Token (5 pts). Others take one final turn.  
- Scoring:  
  1. Base Animals: Score per card’s criteria (Each Animal, Grouping, Community, The Jungle).  
  2. Scenery Cards: Score as described.  
  3. Terrain Tile Track: Points = space number.  
  4. Tree Track: Points = space number.  
  5. Aquatic Flower Track: Points = space number.  
  6. Unused Seeds: 1 pt each.  
  7. End Game Bonus: 5 pts (Final Player only).  
- Tiebreaker: Most Animals placed. If still tied, share victory.

7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- Can I use multiple Seeds in one payment? Yes, but only one can use Iguana ability per turn.  
- Can I relocate a Life to the same hex? No; must move to different valid hex.  
- Can I compost a token in my Bag? No; only from Play Area or Discard Boat.  
- Can I use a Unique Animal ability the turn it’s placed? Yes, if placed during Action Phase.  
- Can I score the same Life in multiple Animal cards? Yes.  
- Can I use a Life in multiple Groupings for the same Animal? No; each Life counts once per card.  
- What if I can’t refill a pool? Leave empty.  
- What if both Bag and Discard Boat are empty? Cannot draw tokens.  
- Can I use Insect Cards after Scenery limit? Yes; they are one-time use.  
- Can I decline a Bonus? Yes.  
- Can I use Hoatzin to discount a Tree placed via Insect Card? No; only “Place a Tree” action.
