### 3. Setup  
1. Place the battlefield between players.  
2. Prepare supplies of damage, boost, and magic tokens.  
3. Each player:  
   - Chooses a faction or builds a custom deck.  
   - Places their **summoner**, **2 starting units**, and **starting gate (10 life)** on the battlefield as shown on the back of their summoner card.  
   - Shuffles remaining cards into a **draw pile**.  
   - Draws 5 cards.  
4. Randomly determine first player.  
   - First player places magic token on **2** of magic track.  
   - Second player places magic token on **3** of magic track.  

---

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
Each turn has 6 phases, completed in order:  
1. **Summon**  
2. **Move**  
3. **Build**  
4. **Attack**  
5. **Magic**  
6. **Draw**  

After all phases, turn ends and opponent begins their turn.

---

### 5. Core Mechanics  

#### Phase 1: Summon  
- Summon **any number** of units from hand.  
- Pay cost in **magic** (move token down magic track).  
- Place unit on **empty space adjacent to a gate you control**.  
- Cannot summon if no valid space exists.  

#### Phase 2: Move  
- Move **up to 3 different units** you control.  
- Each can move **1 or 2 spaces**.  
- Movement rules:  
  - Move into or through **empty adjacent spaces** (sharing an edge).  
  - **No diagonal movement**.  
  - Cannot move through other cards.  
  - Units that are also **structures** cannot move unless an effect allows it.  
- A unit may move 1 space and return to original space (e.g., to trigger "after moving" abilities).  

#### Phase 3: Build  
- Build **any number** of structures (gates, walls, etc.) from hand.  
- Pay cost in magic.  
- Place on:  
  - Empty space **adjacent to your summoner**, OR  
  - Empty space in your **back 3 rows** (closest to you).  

#### Phase 4: Attack  
- Attack with **up to 3 different units** (not necessarily moved this turn).  
- Steps per attack:  
  1. **Declare Target**:  
     - **Melee unit** (‚öîÔ∏è): Target adjacent enemy.  
     - **Ranged unit** (üèπ): Target up to **3 clear straight spaces** away (horizontal/vertical, no diagonals, no blocking).  
  2. **Roll Dice**: Roll number of dice equal to unit‚Äôs **strength**.  
  3. **Add Damage**: 1 damage per die showing symbol matching unit‚Äôs attack type (‚öîÔ∏è or üèπ).  
- **Damaging & Destroying**:  
  - Place **damage tokens** on target.

  - **Gain 1 magic** when a card you control destroys an enemy card.  
- **Cost of Inaction**:  
  - If **no attack targets enemy**, add **1 damage to your summoner** at end of Attack Phase.  

#### Phase 5: Magic  
- Discard **any number** of cards from hand.  
- Gain **1 magic per card discarded**.  

#### Phase 6: Draw  
- If hand has **fewer than 5 cards**, draw up to 5.  
- **No reshuffling**: If draw pile is empty, you cannot draw.

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
- **Win Condition**: When only one player‚Äôs **summoner** remains on the battlefield, that player wins.  
- There is **no scoring system**; victory is achieved solely by eliminating the opponent‚Äôs summoner.  
- **Losing Condition**: Your summoner is destroyed.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Can I play an active event if I already have one with the same name?**  
  ‚Üí No. You cannot have two active events with the same name in play.  

- **Can a unit attack without moving?**  
  ‚Üí Yes. Attack phase is independent of movement.  

- **Can a unit attack twice?**  
  ‚Üí Only if an effect grants an **extra attack**. Otherwise, each unit can attack only once per turn.  

- **What happens when a card is under another card?**  
  - It is **not on the battlefield**, but still **in play**.  
  - Cannot be targeted unless specified.  
  - Discarded when the top card is destroyed, discarded, or returned.  

- **What happens if my draw pile is empty?**  
  ‚Üí You do **not** reshuffle discard pile. You cannot draw cards.  

- **Can I build a structure anywhere?**  
  ‚Üí Only **adjacent to your summoner** or in your **back 3 rows**.  

- **Can I summon a unit if I have no gates?**  
  ‚Üí No. You must control at least one gate with an adjacent empty space.  

- **Do I gain magic if my effect destroys my own unit?**  
  ‚Üí No. Magic is only gained when **enemy** cards are destroyed by your units, events, or abilities.  

- **Timing Conflicts**:  
  ‚Üí If multiple effects trigger simultaneously, **current player** chooses resolution order.  

- **Triggered Abilities (e.g., "After this unit moves")**:  
  ‚Üí Resolved **immediately after** the triggering event, before any other effects (except other "after" effects).  

- **"Instead of" Effects**:  
  ‚Üí Replace the normal action (e.g., "instead of moving" means no movement occurs, so "after moving" does not trigger).  
  ‚Üí Still counts toward phase limits (e.g., using "instead of moving" counts as using that unit‚Äôs move).  

- **Card Control**:  
  ‚Üí If you take control of a card, **rotate it 180¬∞** so it faces you.  
  ‚Üí You also gain control of any cards **under** it.
