2. Components  
- 67 Map Tiles (one side explored, one unexplored)  
- Player Boards (1 per player)  
- Main Dice (2 white dice per player)  
- Effort Dice (3 purple dice per player)  
- Destiny Cards (1 per player, with secret paths)  
- Character Miniatures (1 per player)  
- Skill Tokens (to mark skill levels)  
- Item Cards (various, with coin values and abilities)

- Coins (1 starting coin per player)  
- 3D Points of Interest Minis  
- App (required for gameplay, narrative, and scenario control)

3. Setup  
- Choose a scenario in the app and read the introduction aloud.  
- The Nature of the Beast is standalone; the other four form a campaign best played in order.  
- Each player selects a character (random if undecided).  
- Each player receives:  
  - 1 Player Board  
  - 2 Main Dice and 3 Effort Dice  
  - 1 Coin  
  - Characterâ€™s Destiny Card and Miniature  
- Place 2 Main Dice and 1 Coin in the supply area of the player board.  
- Place 3 Effort Dice off the board (exhausted).  
- Place skill tokens on skill tracks as directed by the app.  
- Privately read and place the Destiny Card face-down on the board (illustration visible).  
- Set up the map:  
  - Place starting explored tile.  
  - Add Points of Interest tokens and 3D minis as per app.  
  - Place all character miniatures on the starting tile.  

4. Gameplay Flow  
Players take turns in clockwise order, passing the app device. Each turn has three phases:  

1. Start of Turn  
- Refresh 1 exhausted Effort Die by returning it to your supply.  
- This is when most game events trigger (via app).  

2. Movement  
- Move up to 2 tiles orthogonally (no diagonals).  
- You may stay on your current tile.  
- If moving to an unexplored tile:  
  - Stop movement immediately.  
  - Explore the tile via the app: flip to explored side, place POIs, add adjacent unexplored tiles.  
- After moving to an explored tile, you may visit 1 Point of Interest (POI) on that tile.  

3. Visiting a Point of Interest  
Click the POI in the app and read the description aloud. You may choose any available options in any order (each only once per visit):  
- Interactions: Automatic actions with unknown consequences.  
- Tests: Skill-based challenges (Intelligence, Dexterity, Power).  
- Scanning Items: Use or discuss items with NPCs.  
- Scanning Destiny: Share your Destiny path with an NPC for hints (scan specific pathâ€™s QR code).  
- Scan or Test: Choose to use a correct item (automatic success/fail) or take a test (chance of success).  
- Trading: Buy/sell items at trade stacks (if available).  

5. Core Mechanics  
- **Tests**  
  - Required: Roll 2 Main Dice.  
  - Optional: Roll 0â€“3 Effort Dice (each used die is exhausted).  
  - Total all dice values.  
  - For each skill marker on the relevant track at a number â‰¤ roll total, gain 1 success.  
  - Each star (â˜…) on an Effort Die = 1 automatic success.  
  - Enter total successes into app (max 6 needed).  
  - Skill Types:  
    - Intelligence: Reasoning, social, focus.  
    - Dexterity: Stealth, perception, agility.  
    - Power: Strength, endurance, combat.  

- **Experience**  
  - Earned via interactions.  
  - Spend during your turn (not during a test).  
  - 1 XP = move skill markers 2 spaces left total (e.g., 1 marker 2 left, or 2 markers 1 left).  
  - No two markers may occupy the same space.  

- **Skill Gain/Loss**  
  - App may instruct skill gain (move markers left) or loss (move right).  
  - Player chooses how to distribute movement across markers on the track.  
  - Permanent; can be offset later with XP.  

- **Items**  
  - Inventory limit: 5 cards.  
  - Excess items: Abandon or use Discard ability.  
  - Abandoned items: Place near map with Trade Stack Tokens (one on item, one on tile).  
    - Any player visiting a POI on that tile may pick it up as a free action.  
  - Abilities:  
    - Always: Active while in inventory.  
    - Discard: Use ability, then return card to box.  
  - Use abilities freely during your turn unless specified.  

- **Trading**  
  - First-time trade: Create trade stack with app-specified items + matching Trade Stack Tokens.  
  - One token stays at POI, one on the item stack.  
  - Prices = printed coin value (buy/sell).  
  - Any number of items can be traded per turn.  
  - Trade stack persists; items sold by one player can be bought by others.  
  - Coins can also be used to bribe, repay, or throw (return to supply).  

- **Scanning**  
  - Item Scan: Represents using or discussing an item. Consequences vary.  
  - Destiny Scan: Share your Destiny path with NPC for hints (scan specific pathâ€™s QR).  
    - NPC responses are indirect to avoid revealing secrets.  

- **Events**  
  - Triggered at start of turn or via app.  
  - May alter POIs: move, remove, or change state.  
  - May involve NPC movement or world changes.  

- **Virtue**  
  - Hidden fourth skill tracked by app.  
  - Based on moral choices.  
  - Affects NPC interactions and states.  

6. Scoring & End Game  
- There is no traditional scoring.  
- Victory condition: Fulfill your personal Destiny.  
- Each Destiny has two paths; players may switch between them freely.

7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Inventory Overflow**: Must resolve immediately. Abandon or discard.  
- **Ambiguous Actions**: Resolve in the playerâ€™s favor.  
- **Item Use in Scan or Test**: Only one method (item or test) allowed per turn.  
- **Team Mode (Bound by Fate)**:  
  - 2 vs 2 mode. Teams sit alternately.  
  - Teammates can discuss openly.  
  - Can exchange items/coins if on same tile at turn start.  
  - Can summon teammate (within 2 tiles) during POI visit.  
  - Summoned teammate acts with summoner: shared actions, combined test successes, shared effects (except items: one gets item, other gets XP).  
  - Finale: Both must be present; they act together, and the non-triggering teammate skips future turns.  
- **Solo Mode**:  
  - Explorer Mode: No time pressure, story-focused.  
  - Challenger Mode: Time pressure, possible failure, replayable.  
- **Replayability**: Scenarios may be replayed only if all players have played them before.  
- **Submaps & Travel**: Use Travel POIs to switch maps. Destinations unlock over time. Hidden items may enable travel.  
- **Dungeons & Mazes**:  
  - Dungeons: Choose paths at intersections; remember routes.  
  - Mazes: Larger, with dead ends; solve via clues or luck.  
- **Destiny Hints**: Gain hints via murals, libraries, or NPC conversations (Destiny scans).
