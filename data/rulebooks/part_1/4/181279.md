## Comprehensive Reference Manual  

### 1. Lore & Objective  
In *Fury of Dracula*, one player controls Dracula as he travels across Europe, spreading darkness and creating vampires. The other players control a team of hunters (Lord Godalming, Dr. John Seward, Van Helsing, and Mina Harker) who must track him down and destroy him before his influence overruns the continent.  

- **Dracula wins** by advancing the influence track to space 13.  
- **Hunters win** by defeating Dracula (inflicting 15 damage).  

### 2. Components  
- Game Board (Europe map)  
- 5 Character Sheets (4 hunters, 1 Dracula)  
- 5 Plastic Figures (4 hunters, 1 Dracula)  
- 75 Event Cards (50 Hunter, 25 Dracula)  
- 70 Location Cards (60 Land, 10 Sea)  
- 28 Encounter Cards  
- 38 Item Cards  
- 12 Hunter Combat Cards (Dodge, Punch, Escape)  
- 13 Dracula Combat Cards  
- 16 Ticket Tokens  
- 3 Despair Tokens  
- 1 Influence Marker  
- 4 Bite Tokens  
- 31 Damage Tokens (24 × 1, 7 × 5)  
- 8 Roadblock Tokens  
- 6 Storm Tokens  
- 4 Fog Tokens  
- 2 Bats Tokens  
- 3 Heavenly Host Tokens  
- 1 Consecrated Ground Token  
- 5 Power Cards (Feed, Dark Call, Wolf Form, Misdirect, Hide)  
- 1 Time Marker  
- 4 Rumor Tokens  
- 4 Hunter Reference Cards  
- 1 Reference Map  

### 3. Setup  
1. Place the board and set influence marker to 0, time marker to Monday (day).  
2. Assign roles: One player controls Dracula; others control hunters.  
3. Shuffle and place decks:  
   - Item cards facedown.  
   - Hunter and Dracula event cards shuffled into one deck.  
   - Dracula’s encounter and combat cards into separate decks.  
   - Location cards sorted numerically; power cards placed beneath to form Dracula’s location deck.  
4. Create token pool: Randomize ticket tokens; organize other tokens.  
5. Dracula draws 5 encounter cards and takes 1 rumor token.  
6. Hunters place figures on starting cities:  
   - Lord Godalming: Constanta  
   - Dr. Seward: Marseilles  
   - Mina Harker: Brussels  
   - Van Helsing: Amsterdam  
7. Dracula secretly chooses a starting city (not a sea zone, Castle Dracula, or a city with a hunter), places its location card facedown on the first space of the trail, and places his figure on the red circle above it.  

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
**Each round consists of:**  
1. **Hunter Phase**  
   - **Dawn**: Advance time; combat if hunters are with Dracula.  
   - **Day**: Each hunter performs 1 action (in activation order: Godalming, Seward, Van Helsing, Mina).  
   - **Dusk**: Advance time; combat if hunters are with Dracula.  
   - **Night**: Each hunter performs 1 action.  
2. **Dracula Phase**  
   - **Movement Step**: Slide all hideouts right; place a new location or power card on space 1.  
   - **Encounter Step**: Place an encounter card on the new hideout; optionally place a rumor token on spaces 1–3.  

### 5. Core Mechanics  
#### Movement  
- **Hunters**:  
  - **Road**: Move to adjacent city.  
  - **Railway**: Spend a ticket token; move up to number on token (white for white railways, yellow for yellow or mixed).  
  - **Sea**: Move from port to adjacent sea zone or between adjacent sea zones.  
- **Dracula**:  
  - Moves only by road or sea (no railways).  
  - Sea movement: -2 damage (port → sea), -1 damage (sea → sea).  
  - Uses the trail to track movement; figure remains hidden until revealed.  

#### Actions (Hunters)  
- **Move**: As above.  
- **Supply**: In large city, draw 1 item card. Then draw 1 event card:  
  - **Day**: Top of deck; keep if hunter icon, discard Dracula icon facedown.  
  - **Night**: Bottom of deck; keep if hunter icon, Dracula takes if Dracula icon.  
- **Rest**: Recover 1 damage (Seward recovers 2 when resting with another hunter).  
- **Trade**: With a hunter in same city; trade item cards and ticket tokens secretly.  
- **Reserve a Ticket**: Draw 1 ticket token; max 2 held.  
- **Search**: In a hideout or lair, Dracula reveals and resolves encounter cards (ignoring matured effects) in hunter’s chosen order.  
- **Special**: Use character ability or event card effect.  

#### Combat  
- Triggered at **dawn or dusk** if a hunter is in Dracula’s city.  
- **Setup**:  
  - Each hunter adds 1 Dodge, Punch, Escape card to hand (do not count toward hand limit).  
  - Dracula draws 5 combat cards.  
- **Combat Rounds**:  
  1. Choose combat cards.  
  2. Dracula chooses **engaged hunter** (only this hunter blocks Dracula’s card).  
  3. Reveal cards.  
  4. **Compare Icons**: If Dracula’s card icon matches a highlighted icon on engaged hunter’s banner, Dracula’s card is canceled.  
  5. **Resolve Dracula’s Effect** (if not canceled).  
  6. **Resolve Hunter’s Effect** (all hunters, if not canceled/defeated).  
  7. **Check Status**: Combat ends if Dracula has played 6 cards, all hunters are out, or escape condition met.  
  8. **Refresh Hands**: Dracula draws 1 card (if <5); hunters get previous round’s card back.  
- **Combat Ends When**:  
  - Dracula plays 6 cards.  
  - Dracula resolves Escape as Bat/Mist (if allowed by Pride ability).  
  - All hunters are defeated, bitten, or escaped.  
  - Dracula is defeated.  
  - Influence track reaches 13.  

#### Dracula Combat Cards  
- **Claws**: Engaged hunter suffers 2 damage; at night, another hunter suffers 2 damage.  
- **Escape as Bat**: Combat ends; Dracula may move to city up to 2 roads away.  
- **Escape as Mist**: Combat ends.  
- **Fangs**: Dracula recovers 2 damage; engaged hunter suffers 2 damage (or is bitten if mesmerized at night). Then, advance influence by 1.  
- **Mesmerize**: Cancels hunter’s card; Dracula takes 1 less damage from that hunter’s Weapon cards.  
- **Plotting**: Draw 1 combat card; may cancel Punch/Dodge/Escape.  
- **Strength**: Engaged hunter suffers 2 damage; cancels Punch, Crucifix, or Weapon; discards them at night.  

#### Hunter Combat Items  
- **Punch**: Cancels Mesmerize/Plotting; Dracula suffers 1 damage.  
- **Dodge**: Cancels Fangs/Strength/Claws.  
- **Escape**: Cancels Strength/Plotting; hunter leaves combat.  
- **Crucifix**: Cancels Fangs/Mesmerize/Escape as Mist; if cancels Fangs/Mesmerize, Dracula suffers 3 damage.  
- **Garlic**: Cancels Fangs/Strength; if engaged, Dracula suffers 2 damage if Fangs/Claws resolved.  
- **Garlic Wreath**: Cancel if Claws resolved; otherwise, after suffering Fangs damage, Dracula suffers 4 damage.  
- **Heavenly Host**: Cancels Mesmerize; if Fangs played, hunter is bitten and removed; otherwise, Dracula suffers 4 damage.  
- **Holy Bullets**: After Rifle/Pistol, Dracula suffers 3 additional damage (1 per combat).  
- **Holy Circle**: Cancels all; choose hunters to remove from combat.  
- **Knife/Pistol/Rifle**: Weapon cards; cancel specific Dracula cards; deal 2–3 damage.  

#### Encounter & Power Cards  
- **Vampire Encounters** (New, Aristocratic, Reckless): Trigger combat; matured effect advances influence.  
- **Power Cards**:  
  - **Feed**: Recover 3 damage (no encounter).  
  - **Dark Call**: Suffer 2 damage; draw 5 encounter cards.  
  - **Wolf Form**: Move up to 2 roads away; suffer 1 damage.  
  - **Misdirect**: Clear a hideout; place Misdirect on that space.  
  - **Hide**: Stay in place; place encounter (cannot mature).  

#### Tokens  
- **Bats**: Dracula moves hunter to adjacent city on next action.  
- **Fog**: Hunters cannot enter, leave, or pass through fogged city; no search.  
- **Roadblock**: Blocks road/rail movement.  
- **Storm**: Blocks sea movement into storm zone.  
- **Consecrated Ground / Heavenly Host**: Dracula cannot enter; revealed if on trail.  
- **Rumor Tokens**: +3 influence if vampire matures from hideout with rumor.  

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
- **Dracula advances influence by**:  
  - Maturing vampire: +X (on card).  
  - Resolving Fangs against mesmerized hunter: +1.  
  - Defeating a hunter: +2 + number of despair tokens.  
  - After 3 despair tokens, placing city/Hide: +3.  
- **Despair Tokens**: Placed when time advances from Sunday night to Monday day.  
- **Win Conditions**:  
  - Dracula wins if influence reaches 13.  
  - Hunters win if Dracula takes 15 damage.  
- **Tie**: If both conditions occur simultaneously, hunters win.  

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Delayed Hunters**: Must stand upright instead of acting; participate in combat if with Dracula.  
- **Multiple Hunters in Combat**: Dracula chooses engaged hunter; only that hunter blocks.  
- **Sea Zones**: No combat; Dracula cannot be revealed.  
- **Lairs**: Created instead of maturing hideout; Dracula may move to lair later.  
- **Hand Limits**: Hunters: 3 item/event (Seward/Van Helsing: 4); Dracula: 4 event cards.  
- **Card Decks Empty**: Shuffle discard pile (keep facedown cards facedown).
