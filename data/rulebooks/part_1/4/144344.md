## Comprehensive Reference Manual

1. Lore & Objective  
In Rococo, players own dressmaking businesses competing to gain the most Prestige before the grand ball. The game centers on managing employees, crafting dresses, acquiring resources, and funding decorations. The player with the highest Prestige after 7 rounds wins. In the solo variant "Rococo - Masques Solo", the player competes against 1–4 AI-controlled Masques—faceless dressmakers aiming to outshine them.

2. Components  
- Double-sided game board (for 2–3 or 4–5 players)  
- 28 Employee cards (5 Masters, 5 Journeyman, 5 Apprentice per player color; 3 special level 6 cards)  
- 42 Dress tiles (draft and rental sides)  
- 48 Resource tiles (green, yellow, red, blue silk; thread/yarn; lace)  
- 48 Coins (Livre)  
- 48 Prestige Point tokens  
- 80 wooden Property markers (16 per player color)  
- 16 Thread/Yarn markers  
- 16 Lace markers  
- 1 Favor card  
- 1 Starting Player marker  
- 1 Cloth bag  
- 5 Player boards  
- 1 Overview card for final scoring  
- Spare token (used as Focus token for Masques)  

3. Setup  
- Choose board side based on player count.  
- Place Favor card next to the Favor space.  
- Shuffle Employee cards by level (6 to 1), forming a general stack.  
- Shuffle Resource tiles into face-down stacks by warehouse segments.  
- Place Dress tiles in the cloth bag.  
- Each player receives:  
  - 1 Player board  
  - 5 Base Employee cards (placed face-down under left space)  
  - 16 Property markers  
  - 15 Livre, 1 Thread/Yarn, 1 Lace  
- Determine Starting Player.  

Masque Setup (Solo Variant):  
- Place spare token as Focus token on Hire space tabs.  
- Each Masque receives:  
  - Starting materials and hand (as per normal player)  
  - Examination card  
  - Specialised Apprentice (face-up)  
- Shuffle 5 starting cards, place face-down in Employee supply.  
- Draw top card to Discard pile (first Action card).  
- Next 3 cards form hand.  
- Masques never pay for actions.  
- Masques count 10 money = 1 VP.  
- Masques never Depute red bonus Employees.  

4. Gameplay Flow  
Each round consists of 4 phases:  
A. Prepare for New Round  
- Transfer Starting Player marker if Favor card was taken.  
- Remove leftover face-up Employee cards from Hire spaces.  
- Reveal 4 new Employee cards from general stack.  
- Refill empty warehouse segments with Resource tiles.  
- Refill Workshop windows:  
  - Discard rightmost two Dress tiles (marked with ⊙).  
  - Shift remaining tiles right.  
  - Fill empty spaces from cloth bag.  

B. Select Hand Cards  
- Players draw 3 cards from Employee supply.  
- If supply < 3 cards:  
  - Take all cards.  
  - Flip discard pile to form new supply.  
  - Draw needed cards.  
- Exactly 3 cards? Take them; do not refresh supply until next round.  

C. Take Actions  
- Starting Player plays first, clockwise order.  
- Play one card per turn until all hand cards are used.  
- Main Action depends on Employee type:  
  - Master: Any of 6 actions  
  - Journeyman: All except Hire  
  - Apprentice: Only Acquire Resources or Depute  
- After main action, optional bonus action may be used.  
- Card goes to Discard pile.  

Masque Turn:  
- Draw top card from Masque’s hand.  
- Compare with top Discard card (Action card) to determine action.  
- Perform action.  
- Place Employee card on Discard pile (becomes new Action card).  
- If Discard pile exhausted, shuffle into new supply, leaving top card in Discard.  

D. Collect Income  
- Base income: 5 Livre.  
- Upper Fountain marker: +1 Livre per Decoration funded.  
- Lower Fountain marker: +1 Livre per Dress on board.  
- "All halls" bonus: Immediately claim when present in all 5 halls.  

5. Core Mechanics  

Main Actions:  
1. Claim Queen’s Favor (Master/Journeyman):  
- Take Favor card, gain 5 Livre, become next Starting Player.  
- Worth 3 VP at game end.  

2. Acquire Resources (Master/Journeyman/Apprentice):  
- Pay based on number of tiles in segment:  
  - 3–4 tiles: 2 Livre  
  - 2 tiles: 1 Livre  
  - 1 tile: free  
- Choose: keep tile for silk OR discard for Thread/Yarn and/or Lace.  

3. Make a Dress (Master/Journeyman):  
- Pay listed cost and required resources (silk, Thread/Yarn, Lace).  
- Only Masters can make thimble-marked dresses.  
- Use multiple silk tiles; excess silk lost.  
- Decide: Rent or Sell.  
  - Rent: Flip and place on Guest space (Master Guest spaces require Master-made dress).  
    - Place Property marker.  
    - Claim space bonus if present.  
  - Sell: Gain Livre shown, discard tile.  

4. Hire Employee (Master only):  
- Pay based on number of available cards:  
  - 4 cards: 5 Livre  
  - 3: 3 Livre  
  - 2: 1 Livre  
  - 1: free  
- Add to hand; may play same round.  

5. Depute Employee (All types):  
- Remove card from game. Gain:  
  - Master: 10 Livre  
  - Journeyman: 7 Livre  
  - Apprentice: 4 Livre  
- May use bonus before removal.  
- Cannot depute if ≤4 cards in deck.  

6. Fund a Decoration (All types):  
- Pay cost to place Property marker on Fireworks, Musician, Statue, or Fountain.  
- Fountain: max 1 marker per row.  
- Prestige Points awarded at end.  

Masque Actions (determined by acting Employee + Action card):  
- Hire a new Employee (Master):  
  - Draw Resource tile.  
  - Move Focus token down by number of items.  
  - Wrap around if needed.  
  - If space empty, move to next available Employee down.  
  - Hire that Employee.  
  - If no Employee, Make a Dress instead.  
  - Specialised Apprentice: Hire if available instead.  

- Make a Dress (Master/Journeyman):  
  - Draw Resource tile.  
  - Master: Rent first Dress from Left matching any silk.  
  - Journeyman: Rent first Dress from Right matching any silk.  
  - If no match, take first from respective side.  

- Placing the Dress:  
  - Master: First free Master Guest space in hall with fewest own dresses, top hall first. Leftmost first.  
  - Journeyman: First free bonus space in hall with fewest own dresses, bottom hall first. Leftmost first.  
  - If no matching space, use normal space in same order.  
  - If no space, sell dress.  

- Bonus Jewellery: If Jeweller has matching color jewellery, Masque gains lowest-priced one.  

- Fund a Decoration:  
  - Order: Jeweller → Fountain (top row first) → Speciality → Statue → Hall → Fireworks  
  - Speciality:  
    - Master: Fireworks  
    - Journeyman: Halls  
    - Apprentice: Statues  
  - Always place in lowest-cost available space in category.  
  - If no space:  
    - Apprentice → Acquire Resources  
    - Others → Make a Dress  

- Acquire Resources:  
  - Draw Resource tile.  
  - Journeyman: Search top-left to bottom-right, row by row.  
  - Apprentice: Search bottom-right to top-left, row by row.  
  - Take first matching tile.  
  - If no match, take first available by search order.  
  - Discard drawn tile.  

- Take Examination Action:  
  - If Masque completed tasks, next Employee of that type takes Examination.  
  - Shuffle relevant stack, add top card to hand, shuffle hand.  

- Master X Bonus (Master cards with X):  
  - If Queen’s Favor unclaimed: Claim it, gain 5 Livre.  
  - If claimed: Perform Hire a new Employee.  

- Bonus Action Clarifications:  
  - "Give up something for Prestige": Masque gains 1 Prestige, keeps resources.  
  - Depute bonus: Depute top card of Employee supply, or bottom of Discard if supply empty.  
  - Choose card from supply: always top card.  
  - Copy bonus: Always copies top card of player’s Discard pile (can be X bonus).  

6. Scoring & End Game  
Game ends after 7 rounds. Final scoring:  
1. Money: 1 VP per 10 Livre (remainder breaks ties).  
2. Employee Bonuses: Score crown/red-background cards.  
3. Favor Card: 3 VP if held.  
4. Board Scoring (in order):  
A. Majority in each hall:  
- 1st: points before /  
- 2nd: points after /  
- Tiebreak: more dresses on Master Guest spaces → Musician space → all tied get 1st place points.  
- 2-player: only 1st place scored.  

B. Fireworks Majority:  
- Varies by player count (e.g., 7/3 or 6/2).  
- Tiebreak: rightmost (higher-cost) space wins.  
- 2-player: only 1st place scored.  

C. Watch Fireworks from Terrace:  
- For each Fireworks marker, move one Dress from King’s Hall to adjacent Terrace space.  

D. Statues:  
- Per Statue marker: score 1 set of different-colored dresses (2 VP per color, max 8).  
- Multiple markers: must use different dresses for each set.  

E. Score Property markers:  
- Remove one by one.  
- Add Prestige values on markers.  
- Terrace dresses: multiply value by 2 or 3.  

Winner: Most Prestige. Tie: more money wins.  

7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- Can I depute my last Master? No, if only 4 cards remain in deck. Strongly discouraged.  
- Can I make a dress with no guest space? Yes, but must sell it.  
- Can Masque acquire resources with no tiles? No, action skipped.  
- If Masque Discard pile reshuffled, is top card preserved? Yes.  
- Can Masque hire if no Employees available? No, defaults to Make a Dress.  
- Can Masque claim Favor if already held? No, gains 5 Livre instead.
