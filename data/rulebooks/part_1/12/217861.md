## Comprehensive Reference Manual

1. Lore & Objective  
- **Lore**: Paper Tales is set in a forgotten era of heroic wars and fantastic creatures. Players rewrite history by building their kingdoms through units and buildings.  
- **Objective**: Earn the most Legend Points (LP) over 4 game turns by deploying units, constructing buildings, winning wars, and using card abilities. The player with the highest LP wins. In case of a tie, the player with the most gold wins. If still tied, players share victory.

2. Components  
- 1 Score Track  
- 1 wooden Time Token  
- 30 Gold tokens (value 1, yellow)  
- 10 Gold tokens (value 5, gray)  
- 30 Age tokens  
- 5 Player Aid Cards  
- 5 wooden Score Tokens  
- 25 Building cards (5 per player: Town, Tavern, Mine, Temple, Barracks)  
- 81 Unit cards  

3. Setup  
- Place the Score Track in the center.  
- Place the Time Token on space "1" of the Turn Track.  
- Form pools of Gold and Age tokens.  
- Each player:  
  - Chooses a color and takes matching Player Aid Card and Score Token.  
  - Places Score Token on 0 of the Score Track.  
  - Takes 3 Gold (visible to all).  
  - Takes one set of 5 Building cards (Town, Tavern, Mine, Temple, Barracks).  
- Shuffle Unit cards into a face-down draw pile.  
- Leave space for a discard pile.  

4. Gameplay Flow  
- The game lasts 4 turns, each with 6 phases:  
  1. Recruitment  
  2. Deployment  
  3. Wars  
  4. Income  
  5. Construction  
  6. Aging  
- All players complete each phase simultaneously before proceeding.  
- After Aging, advance the Time Token. After turn 4, proceed to end game.

5. Core Mechanics  
- **Kingdom Structure**:  
  - 4 unit slots: 2 Front, 2 Back.  
  - With at least one Level 2 Building, gain a 5th unit slot (Front).  
  - Buildings are placed near the kingdom; unused buildings are kept aside.  

- **1. Recruitment**:  
  - Each player is dealt 5 Unit cards.  
  - Keep 1, pass the rest to the left (turns 1 and 3) or right (turns 2 and 4).  
  - Repeat until each player has 5 cards.  
  - Add any card saved from the previous turn to hand (now 6 cards possible).  
  - If draw pile is empty, shuffle discard pile to form a new draw pile.  

- **2. Deployment**:  
  - Players choose units from hand to deploy face-down into available slots.  
  - Can discard or reposition existing units to free space.  
  - After all players are ready, reveal new units simultaneously.  
  - Pay gold cost (top-left of card) for each newly deployed unit.  
  - If cannot pay, discard the unit(s).  
  - Of unplayed cards, keep 1 for next turn; discard the rest.  
  - Resolve all Phase 2 abilities (indicated by Phase 2 symbol).  

- **3. Wars**:  
  - Sum strength (shield value) of Front row units. * cards may modify strength via abilities.  
  - Compare strength with left and right neighbors independently.  
  - Win a war if strength ≥ neighbor’s. Each win = 3 LP.  
  - Ties: both players win and earn 3 LP.  
  - Resolve all Phase 3 abilities.  

- **4. Income**:  
  - Each player earns 2 gold from the bank.  
  - Plus any gold from Phase 4 abilities of in-play cards.  
  - Resolve all Phase 4 abilities.  

- **5. Construction (Optional)**:  
  - Each player may perform **one** of:  
    - **Construct a New Building**:  
      - Choose an unconstructed building from your set.  
      - Play as Level 1 or Level 2.  
      - Pay **Land Cost**: 2 gold × number of buildings already in play.  
      - Pay **Resource Cost**:  
        - Level 1: produce resources shown on Level 1 icon.  
        - Level 2: produce resources for **both** Level 1 and Level 2 icons.  
    - **Upgrade a Building**:  
      - Flip a Level 1 building to Level 2.  
      - Pay Resource Cost shown on Level 2 icon.  
      - No Land Cost.  
  - **Level 2 Building Bonus**: Grants a 5th unit slot (Front).  
  - Resolve all Phase 5 abilities.  

- **6. Aging**:  
  - Discard all units with 1 or more Age tokens (they die).  
  - Add 1 Age token to each surviving unit.  
  - Resolve all Phase 6 abilities.  

6. Scoring & End Game  
- Game ends after Turn 4 Aging phase.  
- Final score = Legend Points from:  
  - All in-play Buildings (value shown on card).  
  - Wars won (3 LP each).  
  - Card abilities (e.g., Phase 3 effects).  
- Highest total LP wins.  
- Tiebreaker: Most gold. If still tied, players share victory.

7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Simultaneous Abilities**:  
  - Resolved in player order:  
    1. Player furthest ahead on Score Track.  
    2. If tied, player with least gold.  
    3. If still tied, random order.  
- **Unit Death**: Units die only when discarded during Aging phase.  
- **Card-Specific Clarifications**:  
  - **Golem**: If no Level 2 Building when deployed, only 4 unit slots available this turn.  
  - **Grand Architect**: Can "buy" any combination of resources (via ability).  
  - **Kraken & Palm Reader**: Apply ability to **each** unit that dies this turn, including self.  
  - **Monkey**: Earns 1 gold upon deployment; can be used immediately for payment.  
  - **Mystical Healer**: Can save itself; add Age token to saved unit and all others.  
  - **Ogre**: Base 2 gold from Income phase counts toward its strength bonus.  
  - **Shapeshifter**: Discard immediately when replaced; add Age token and resolve abilities of new unit.  
  - **Temple**: Level 1 or 2 resource cost can be paid with required number of , or with gold instead (as specified on card).  
- **2-Player Rules**:  
  - **Recruitment**: Each player gets 9 cards. After keeping one, must discard one before passing rest to opponent.  
  - **Wars**:  
    - Win 1 war if strength ≥ opponent.  
    - Win 2 wars if strength ≥ 2× opponent’s strength.
