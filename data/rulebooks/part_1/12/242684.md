## Comprehensive Reference Manual

1. Lore & Objective  
In Reavers of Midgard, players lead Viking clans, recruiting reavers, raiding villages and keeps, battling at sea, and subduing territories to gain glory. The game lasts 6 rounds. The player with the most glory at the end wins. Glory is earned through artifacts, spoils, fulfilled prophecies, controlled territories, and other achievements.

2. Components  
- 1 Game Board  
- 4 Player Boards  
- 4 Score Trackers (1 per color)  
- 8 Longship Pawns (2 per player)  
- 1 Start Player Marker  
- 1 Honor Ship Token (3-player games)  
- 62 Village Spoils Cards  
- 76 Keep Spoils Cards  
- 66 Reaver Cards  
- 24 Sea Journey Cards  
- 45 Sea Battle Cards  
- 34 Prophecy Cards  
- 60 Crew Dice (20 red, 20 yellow, 20 blue)  
- 6 Combat Dice (black)  
- 30 Small Food Tokens, 10 Large Food Tokens (worth 5)  
- 20 Small Favor Tokens, 5 Large Favor Tokens (worth 5)  
- 14 Terror Tokens  
- 15 Wall, Tower, and Farm Tokens each  
- 12 Ship Upgrade Tiles (4 Shield, 4 Chest, 4 Ship/Hammer)  
- 54 Conquered Territory Tiles (Food, Favor, Dice types; levels 1–3)  
- Round Tracker  
- Reference Cards

3. Setup  
1. Place the Game Board in the center. Use the 2-player side for 2 players, 3–4 player side otherwise.  
2. Place the Round Tracker on Round 1.  
3. Determine the starting player (e.g., most recent real-life victor or random). In a 3-player game, give the Honor Ship token to the starting player.  
4. Give each player:  
   - 1 Longship pawn (2 in 2-player games)  
   - 1 Score Tracker (placed on 0)  
   - 1 Player Board  
   - 1 Favor Token  
5. Distribute starting food:  
   - Player 1: 4 Food  
   - Player 2: 5 Food  
   - Player 3: 6 Food  
   - Player 4: 7 Food  
6. Shuffle and place all card decks (Reaver, Village Spoils, Keep Spoils, Sea Journey, Sea Battle, Prophecy) on their board spaces.  
7. Each player draws 2 Reaver cards. For each:  
   - Take and roll the dice shown in the top-left corner, placing them on their Player Board.  
   - Choose to Rally, Specialize, or Promote each Reaver.  
8. Set up Action Locations:  
   - Recruit Reavers: Place 4/6/8 Reaver cards face-up (2P/3P/4P), in pairs.  
   - Trade with Villages: Place 3 Prophecy cards face-up. Roll and place 2 dice of each color in the dice spaces.  
   - Battle at Sea: Place 2/3/4 Sea Battle cards face-up.  
   - Raid Villages: Place 4/6/8 Village Spoils cards face-up. Place all 12 Ship Upgrade tiles nearby.  
   - Raid Keeps: Place 4/6/8 Keep Spoils cards face-up.  
   - Subdue Territories: Sort Territory tiles by type (Food, Favor, Dice) and level (1, 2, 3). Shuffle each of the 9 stacks and place face-down on the board.  
9. Place remaining components (dice, food, favor, terror, wall, tower, farm tokens) in a shared supply.

4. Gameplay Flow  
Each round consists of 4 turns, one per available Action Location.  
1. Active Player Selection: The current player places their Longship pawn on an unoccupied Action Location.  
2. Activate Specialized Reavers: All players activate Reaver cards in the Specialize slot matching the chosen action (from top to bottom). This occurs even if the player later chooses to Rest.  
3. Active Player Action: The active player pays the cost (if any), performs the action, and gains the position 1 bonus.  
4. Other Players Choose: In clockwise order, each player chooses to:  
   - Follow: Pay the cost and perform the action, gaining the bonus based on their position relative to the active player (1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th).  
   - Rest: Gain 2 Food or 1 Crew die of any color on a side of their choice.  
5. Repeat: The next player in clockwise order takes a turn until all 4 Action Locations are used.  
Special:  
- 2-player: Each player takes 2 turns.  
- 3-player: Each player takes 1 turn; the player with the Honor Ship takes an extra turn.  
- 4-player: Each player takes 1 turn.  
End of Round: Proceed to Cleanup Phase.

5. Core Mechanics  
- Actions: Each action has a cost (except Recruit Reavers and Trade with Villages). Bonuses are based on player order relative to the active player, not activation order.  
- Reaver Cards: When gained, take dice from the top-left corner. Then choose:  
  - Rally: Take dice from top-right, set to desired side, discard Reaver to Glory Pile.  
  - Specialize: Place under corresponding slot on Player Board (max 3 per slot). The ability activates when that action is taken.  
  - Promote: Place in Leader slot. Discard previous Leader to Glory Pile. Gain clan bonus:  
    - Blue: 4 Food  
    - Red: Draw 1 Keep or Village Spoils card  
    - Yellow: 2 Favor  
  - Leader Effect: Any die showing your Leader’s clan icon can be used as a Shield, Chest, Ship, or Hammer.  
- Combat:  
  - Discard Crew dice showing Hammer(s). For each Hammer, roll 1 Combat die.  
  - Total axes rolled must meet or exceed the target’s Defense Value.  
  - Spend 1 Favor token to re-roll any number of Combat dice. Can be used multiple times.  
- Glory Pile: Cards discarded to the Glory Pile are kept face-down. Used for end-game scoring. Other discards go face-up to their deck’s discard pile.  
- Action Locations:  
  - Recruit Reavers (Free): All activating players take 2 Reaver cards (from face-up stacks or deck). Active player gains 2 Favor.  
  - Trade with Villages (Free): Active player takes Start Player marker (pass if already held). Players gain rewards: 3/2/1/1 times (1st/2nd/3rd/4th). Rewards: 2 Favor, 3 Food, 2 dice from the location, or 1 Prophecy card.  
  - Battle at Sea (4 Food): Active player gains 2 Glory. Each player reveals a Sea Journey card (pay cost or suffer effect), then selects a Sea Battle card. Defeat it by:  
    - Discarding the dice shown, or  
    - Winning Combat against its Defense Value.  
    - Success: Gain rewards, add card to Glory Pile.  
  - Raid Villages (1 Food, 2 Chest dice): Active player gains 2 extra cards, 2nd player gains 1. All take 2 linked face-up cards or 2 from deck. Card types:  
    - Artifact: Attach to Player Board (max 4). Store matching die to activate ability.  
    - Ship Upgrade: Take tile (use once per round as die) or gain 3 Glory.  
    - Farm: Gain 3 Food. Optionally Pillage: gain 1 Food, 1 Farm Token, 1 Terror Token.  
    - Village Wall: Gain 2 Favor. Optionally Pillage: gain 1 Favor, 1 Wall Token, 1 Terror Token.  
    - Weapon Bundles (Swords/Bows/Axes): Take 2 dice or gain 5 Glory + 1 Terror.  
    - Runic Carvings: Discard 1 Terror to give another player 1 Terror, or gain 10 Glory + 2 Terror.  
  - Raid Keeps (2 Food, 2 Shield dice): Active player gains 2 extra cards, 2nd player gains 1. All take 2 linked face-up cards or 2 from deck. Card types:  
    - Treasure/Art/Armor: Add to Glory Pile. Scored in sets at game end.  
    - Tapestry: Add to Glory Pile. Scored in sets of 3 different types.  
    - Keep Wall: Gain 2 Favor. Optionally Pillage: Combat vs. Defense 2. Success: gain 1 Favor, 1 Wall Token.  
    - Tower: Gain 2 dice. Optionally Pillage: Combat vs. Defense 3. Success: gain 3 Glory, 1 Tower Token.  
  - Subdue Territories (Variable): Players may attempt: 3/2/1/1 times (1st/2nd/3rd/4th). For each attempt:  
    - Force: Combat vs. tile’s Defense Value. Success: claim tile.  
    - Peace: Discard resources shown on tile. Success: claim tile.  
    - After all attempts, flip claimed tiles and gain rewards.  
    - During Cleanup, gain 1 Glory per total subdued territory.

6. Scoring & End Game  
After Round 6:  
1. Transfer all cards and tiles from Player Board and play area to Glory Pile.  
2. Score:  
   - Terror Tokens:  
     - Lose 1/3/6/10/15/21 Glory for 1–6 Terror Tokens.  
     - Lose 6 Glory for each additional Terror Token beyond 6.  
   - Artifacts: Gain Glory value on each card.  
   - Keep Spoils:  
     - Treasure: 1=2, 2=4, 3=9, 4+=16 Glory  
     - Art: 1=3, 2=6, 3+=10 Glory  
     - Armor: 1=1, 2=2, 3=6, 4=12, 5=20, 6+=30 Glory  
     - Tapestries: 1=2, 2=4, 3 different=15 Glory  
   - Farm/Wall/Tower Tokens:  
     - Farms: 1–3=1 each, 4–5=2 each, 6+=3 each. Majority: +3 Glory.  
     - Walls: 1–3=2 each, 4–5=3 each, 6+=4 each. Majority: +4 Glory.  
     - Towers: 1–3=3 each, 4–5=4 each, 6+=5 each. Majority: +5 Glory.  
     - Ties for majority: all tied players gain bonus.  
   - Prophecies: Gain Glory as indicated (e.g., 1 per Artifact, 1 per Tapestry, etc.).  
3. Total Glory. Most Glory wins. Tiebreaker: most Favor tokens.

7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- Q: If a player Rests, can they still activate Specialized Reavers?  
  A: Yes. Specialized Reaver abilities trigger before the decision to Follow or Rest.  
- Q: Can I use my Leader’s wild ability during Combat?  
  A: Yes. Any die showing your Leader’s clan icon can count as a Hammer for Combat.  
- Q: What if I can’t pay an action’s cost?  
  A: You must Rest.  
- Q: Do I score Territory tiles during Round 6’s Cleanup?  
  A: Yes. Gain 1 Glory per subdued territory, including those from Round 6.  
- Q: Can I discard a die from an Artifact to use its ability if I’m Resting?  
  A: Yes, if the action matches the Artifact’s requirement.  
- Q: What if all Specialize slots are full?  
  A: You cannot Specialize new Reavers. Must Rally or Promote.  
- Q: Can Ship Upgrades be used more than once per round?  
  A: No. Each is flipped face-down when used and reset during Cleanup.
