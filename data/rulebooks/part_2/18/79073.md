## Comprehensive Reference Manual

### 1. Lore & Objective
The game simulates survival horror scenarios inspired by the *Resident Evil* universe. Players take on the roles of characters battling waves of infected enemies across different game modes. The primary objective varies by mode:

- **Story Mode**: Survive by defeating infected, managing resources, and escaping.
- **Mercenary Mode**: Earn the most Decorations (points) by defeating infected efficiently.
- **Outbreak Mode**: Survive infection while defeating enemies; avoid reaching Infection Level 10.
- **Versus Mode**: Attack and reduce opponents' health to win.
- **Speed Mini-Game**: Defeat infected faster than your opponent to earn the most Decorations.
- **Nightmare Mode**: Face enhanced enemies and unique challenges.

Victory conditions depend on the selected mode and scenario.

### 2. Components
- **Infected Cards**: Enemy characters with Health, Damage, and effects. Examples: Majini, Licker, Nemesis.
- **Token Cards**: Special items like Rocket Launcher Case and Gatling Gun Case.
- **Mansion Item Cards**: Items such as Yellow Herb, Kevlar Jacket, Antivirus.
- **Event Cards**: Environmental hazards or triggers (e.g., Explosive Barrel, Laser Trap).
- **Bonus Cards**: Time Bonus (+1, +2, +3) and Combo Bonus.
- **Weapon Cards**: Firearms and melee weapons with Ammo Requirements and Damage.
- **Ammunition Cards**: Ammo x10, Ammo x20, Ammo x30.
- **Action Cards**: Cards like Reload, Deadly Aim, Item Management.
- **Character Cards**: Player characters with unique abilities.
- **Skill Cards** (Mercenaries Mode): XP-based upgrades.
- **Resource Piles**: 18 default piles of cards from which players Buy.
- **Counters**: For tracking Reloads, Decorations, Turns, and Infection Level.

### 3. Setup
**General Setup (Story Mode Example)**:
1. Choose a game mode (Story, Mercenary, Outbreak, etc.).
2. Build the Mansion Deck:
   - Use specified Infected, Token, Item, and Event Cards per mode.
   - Shuffle together and place face down.
3. Set up Resource Area:
   - Create 18 Resource Piles (or as specified) from available cards.
   - Include Basic Resources unless modified.
4. Starting Inventory:
   - **Story Mode**: 7x Ammo x10, 1x Handgun, 2x Combat Knife.
   - **Mercenary Mode**: 7x Ammo x10, 1x Custom Standard Sidearm, 2x Reliable Blade.
5. Each player draws 5 cards to begin.

**Speed Mini-Game Setup**:
- Two players.
- Use 20 specified infected cards; split into two 10-card decks.
- Each player gets:
  - 5x Ammo x10, 4x Ammo x20, 2x Ammo x30
  - 2x Custom Full-Bore Machine Gun, 1x Custom Lightning Hawk, 2x Custom Pump-Action Shotgun, 4x Custom Standard Sidearm
- Shuffle and place in Inventory Area.

### 4. Gameplay Flow
**Turn Structure (Standard Game)**:
1. **Draw Phase**: Draw 5 cards (or to hand size if less).
2. **Action Phase**: Perform any number of actions in any order:
   - Play Ammunition/Item cards.
   - Play Action cards.
   - Explore the Mansion (use weapons to defeat infected).
   - Buy cards from Resource Area.
   - Activate character/skill effects.
3. **Explore Sequence**:
   1. Announce Explore.
   2. Move weapons to Play Area.
   3. Pay Ammo Requirements.
   4. Total Damage: Weapon + Character + Effects.
   5. Apply weapon/character effects.
   6. Reveal top card of Mansion.
   7. Apply "When Revealed" effects.
4. **End Phase**: Discard down if needed; turn ends.

**Speed Mini-Game Flow**:
- Simultaneous turns.
- Both players reveal top card of each of two Mansions.
- Use ammo and weapons to defeat revealed infected.
- First to play all inventory wins.
- Scoring: +1 Decoration per 10 Health of defeated infected, -1 per Reload Counter and leftover card.

### 5. Core Mechanics
- **Defeat**: Damage ≥ Health of revealed infected.
- **Gain**: Move card to Discard Pile.
- **Obtain**: Add card to Inventory, Hand, or Discard.
- **Trash**: Move card to bottom of its Resource Pile.
- **Remove from Game**: Card is out of play.
- **Attach**: Place card under Character/Partner.
- **Combo Pile** (Mercenary Mode): Team shares; defeating infected each turn builds combo. When broken, score: (Sum of Decoration values) × (Number of cards in combo).
- **Infection Level** (Outbreak Mode): Tracked separately. Reaching 10 turns character into Infected Character.
- **XP & Skills** (Mercenary Mode): Earn XP by exploring. Spend on Skill Cards for permanent upgrades.
- **Simultaneous Play** (Speed Mini-Game): No turns; act as fast as possible.

### 6. Scoring & End Game
**Story Mode**:
- Game ends when all players are defeated or scenario goal met.
- No formal scoring; survival is victory.

**Mercenary Mode**:
- Ends when turn counter reaches 0.
- Team with most Decorations wins.

**Speed Mini-Game**:
- Ends when one player plays all inventory cards.
- Score:
  - +1 Decoration per 10 Health of defeated infected.
  - -1 per Reload Counter.
  - -1 per card left in Hand/Inventory.
- Highest score wins.

**Outbreak Mode**:
- Game ends when all non-Infected players are dead or all objectives met.
- Winner: Highest Health among survivors (Infected Characters do not count).

**Versus Mode**:
- Game ends when one player’s character is permanently defeated (Max Health ≤ 0).
- Last player standing wins.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Can I choose not to perform part of a card effect?**  
  No. Must do as much as possible.

- **Tied for highest/lowest Decorations (e.g., Hunter, P.R.L. 412)?**  
  No effect triggers if tied.

- **Infected reveals an item and unkillable infected in same explore?**  
  Yes, you can take the item.

- **Can I use a weapon without ammo?**  
  Yes, but no damage unless ammo is paid.

- **Does extra ammo carry over?**  
  No. Ammo resets each explore.

- **Can I mulligan in Speed Mini-Game?**  
  Yes, if hand is all ammo or all weapons.

- **Does “when revealed” effect go before or after damage?**  
  After damage calculation, before defeat check.

- **Can I use Leon’s level 2 ability on non-pistols?**  
  No. Only weapons with “Pistol” type.

- **Do I revive at 0 Health in Professional Difficulty?**  
  No. Permanently defeated.

- **Can Skill Cards be shared in Partner Mode?**  
  No. Each character has separate XP and Skills.
