### 4. Gameplay Flow (Round Structure)  
Each round has 4 phases:

#### I) Select and Reveal Action Cards  
- All players **simultaneously** choose 1 Action card and place it face-down above their Lair.
- All reveal simultaneously. Cards stay face-up.

#### II) Collect Income from Investments  
- For **each Minion** a player has in an Investment Area:
  - Gain **$1** for each **adjacent player** who played the **matching Action** this round.
- **Adjacent** = players directly to left and right.
- **In 2-player games**: opponent counts as both neighbors â†’ **double income**.
- **You do not gain income from your own Action**.
- Example: You have 2 Minions in Invent. Left neighbor played Invent â†’ gain $2.

#### III) Resolve Actions (in numerical order)  
Actions resolve in this fixed order:
1. **Speculate**
2. **Invent**
3. **Research**
4. **Work**

All players who took the same Action resolve it **simultaneously**.

##### Speculate  
- Pay cost to place 1 Minion from reserve to an Investment Area:
  - **Speculate**: $0
  - **Research / Work**: $1 each
  - **Invent**: $2
- You **cannot move** existing Minions. Only place from reserve.
- Cannot Speculate if you canâ€™t afford the cost or have no Minions left.

##### Invent  
- Choose 1 Invention card from hand. Place it **face-down** above your Lair.
- All players who chose Invent **reveal simultaneously**.
- Pay the **cost** (top-left corner) to the bank.
  - If you **canâ€™t pay**, return the card to your hand. Nothing else happens.
- **Resolve effects**:
  1. Each player resolves **their own Inventionâ€™s effects on themselves first**.
  2. Then, each player resolves **othersâ€™ Invention effects that affect them**, in **clockwise order from their left**.
- Effects occur **once**, when played. After resolution, Invention is moved **below Lair** to show VP.
- If multiple Inventions affect you, order matters: your own first, then clockwise.

##### Research  
- Draw **1 Invention card** from the deck.
- Gain **$2** from the bank.
- If deck is empty: shuffle discard pile into new deck, then draw. If still no cards, no draw.

##### Work  
- Gain **$4** from the bank.

#### IV) Check for Winner / Retrieve Action Cards  
- **Total Victory Points** = sum of VP values (lower-left corner) of all Inventions you've built.
  - Some Twists or Inventions may modify scoring.
- If **one player has â‰¥20 VP and more than all others**, they win.
- If **tied at â‰¥20 VP**, game continues.
- Otherwise, all players retrieve their Action cards and start a new round.
