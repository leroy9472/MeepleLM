## Comprehensive Reference Manual  

### 1. Lore & Objective  
In 2083, humanity is extinct. Nature has reclaimed Earth, and apes have evolved into intelligent tribes. As a tribal leader, you guide your apes to develop collective intelligence by gathering resources, attracting advanced apes, and mastering human relics.  

**Objective**: Be the first to reach **80 victory points** to win. In solo mode, you must score 80 victory points **before the King of the Apes** does.  

---

### 2. Components  
- 1 main board  
- 6 player boards (each with a rage track and x3 resource space)  
- 12 player tokens (2 per player: 1 for score track, 1 for rage track)  
- 24 action discs (4 per player, each showing a bonus and an ape type)  
- 48 starting Tamarin cards (8 per player)  
- 120 tribe cards (Chimpanzees, Gorillas, Mandrills, Orangutans; levels 1 and 2)  
- 120 wooden food tokens: 40 flowers, 40 fruits, 40 grains  
- 47 cardboard energy tokens  
- 7 object tiles (only 3 used per game)  
- 6 player aids

### 3. Setup  
#### Multiplayer (2‚Äì6 players):  
1. Place main board in center.  
2. Split tribe cards into **8 decks**: each ape type (Chimpanzee, Gorilla, Mandrill, Orangutan) at levels 1 and 2. Shuffle each deck separately; place on matching spaces on main board.  
3. Create general supply: unlimited flowers, fruits, grains, and energy.  
4. Shuffle object tiles; reveal **3 randomly** next to board.  
   - First game recommended: **Computer, Ghetto Blaster (or Boombox), Moped**.  
5. Each player:  
   - Choose a color.  
   - Place player board in front of them.  
   - Shuffle 8 Tamarin cards; form face-down draw pile to the left.  
   - Place 4 action discs face down on player board.  
   - Place 1 score token on **0** of main board‚Äôs score track.  
   - Place 1 rage token on **0** of their player board‚Äôs rage track.  

#### Solo Mode (vs. King of the Apes):  
1. Assign a color to the **King of the Apes** (automa).  
2. Set up King‚Äôs components:  
   - Player board, 8 Tamarin cards (shuffled draw pile), 2 tokens (score and rage at 0).  
   - Reveal **3 of his 4 action discs** randomly in a row above his board; **return 4th to box**.  
   - Give King **2 resources** matching the attraction cost of the **leftmost revealed disc**:  
     - Mandrill ‚Üí 2 flowers  
     - Orangutan ‚Üí 2 fruits  
     - Gorilla ‚Üí 2 grains  
     - Chimpanzee ‚Üí 2 of any one food type (based on missing ape)  
   - Shuffle King‚Äôs draw pile.  
3. If **Computer** is not among the 3 revealed objects, **give it exclusively to the King**.

### 4. Gameplay Flow  
Each round has **3 phases**, played in order:  

#### P1: Assembling the Tribe  
- **King (solo only)**:  
  - Gains **3 flowers, 3 fruits, 3 grains, 2 energy**.  
  - Reveals top 4 cards from his draw pile (no arrangement).  
  - Resolves all revealed cards **immediately**:  
    - Each **Tamarin**: gain resource shown in rage bonus (top right).  
    - Each **level 1 ape**: +3 VP  
    - Each **level 2 ape**: +6 VP  
    - Each **Gorilla**: +2 rage  
    - Each **Orangutan**: +2 energy  
    - Each **Mandrill**: +2 VP  
    - Each **Chimpanzee**: gain **all** Tamarin rage bonuses from this round  
- **All players (including you)**:  
  - Reveal top 4 cards from draw pile. Shuffle discard into draw if needed.  
  - Arrange 4 cards left to right into **Primate Assembly** (continuous line).  
  - Resolve **top ‚Üí center ‚Üí bottom row**, each **left to right**.  
  - **Open frames** (on card edges) must be **closed** by adjacent card to activate.  
  - Activate frames fully or skip. Each frame activates **once** unless Chimpanzee ability used.  
  - **Chimpanzee ability**: Each üîÑ symbol lets you activate **one other closed frame** (condition must be met).  
  - Collect resources/VPs immediately.  
  - **In solo mode**: Collect resources from **King‚Äôs personal supply** first; use general supply only if King lacks them.  

#### P2: Attracting New Apes  
- **King (solo only)**:  
  - Chooses action disc allowing him to attract **highest-level ape possible**.  
  - In case of tie, picks **leftmost disc**.  
  - If cannot afford any, picks **leftmost disc**.  
  - Applies disc bonus (e.g., Chimpanzee: gains all Tamarin rage bonuses from P1).  
  - Pays cost to general supply; takes **top card** of matching ape/level deck; places on **top of draw pile**.  
- **All players**:  
  - Secretly choose 1 action disc; place face down.  
  - Reveal all discs simultaneously.  
  - Gain **bonus** at top of own disc (no cost).  
  - Optionally attract **1 ape** of type shown on disc:  
    - Level 1: **3 resources**  
    - Level 2: **6 resources**  
    - Resource type:  
      - Mandrill ‚Üí flowers  
      - Orangutan ‚Üí fruits  
      - Gorilla ‚Üí grains  
      - Chimpanzee ‚Üí any one food type (all same)  
  - Place attracted card on **top of draw pile**.  
  - **Copy neighbor‚Äôs bonus (multiplayer)**: Spend **2 identical resources** to gain another player‚Äôs disc bonus (only adjacent players; once per round).  
  - **In solo mode**: You may copy **King‚Äôs disc bonus** by spending 2 identical resources.  

#### P3: Resting  
- **King (solo only)**:  
  - If **4+ rage**: spend in sets of 4 to **remove cards** from game.  
    - Priority: **Tamarins ‚Üí level 1 apes ‚Üí level 2 apes**  
    - Among same level: remove **most recently drawn**  
    - Gain **rage bonus** (top right) of each removed card immediately  
  - If **5+ energy**: spend 5 energy to **activate Computer**, gain **5 VP** (once per round)  
  - Discard all 4 revealed cards to his discard pile  
- **All players**:  
  - Discard Primate Assembly to discard pile (right of player board)  
  - Return action disc to player board  
  - Carry over all resources

### 5. Core Mechanics  

#### Frame Activation  
- Resolve rows: **top ‚Üí center ‚Üí bottom**  
- Within row: **left to right**  
- **Closed frames only** (inner or edge-closed by adjacent card)  
- Conditions (‚Üí):  
  - Cost: pay resources to activate  
  - Requirements: e.g., ‚Äú3+ ape types‚Äù or ‚Äú# of Tamarins‚Äù ‚Äì no cost, just meet condition  
- **Chimpanzee**: Each üîÑ = activate **one other closed frame** (condition must be met)  

#### Rage  
- Tracked on player board. Gorillas generate rage.  
- Spend **4 rage** to **permanently remove** one card from Primate Assembly.  
  - Card must be in current Assembly  
  - Not replaced; gaps remain  
  - Gain **rage bonus** (top right) immediately  
  - Can trigger mid-resolution  
  - Minimum 4 cards in deck at all times  

#### Objects (Each usable once per round)  
| Object | Phase | Cost | Effect |  
|-------|-------|------|--------|  
| **Computer** | 1, 2, or 3 | 5 energy | Score 5 VP |  
| **Moped** | 2 | 6 (L1) or 9 (L2) energy | Attract any ape (place on draw pile) |  
| **Ghetto Blaster / Boombox** | 1 | 2 energy | Discard 1 drawn card; draw replacement from draw pile |  
| **Minibar** | 1 or 2 | 1 energy | Swap 1 resource with general supply |  
| **Game Console** | 3 | 3 (L1) or 5 (L2) energy | Save 1 discarded card to top of draw pile |  
| **Pinball Machine** | 1 | 4 energy | Draw 5th card this round |  
| **Mobile Phone** | 1 | 2 (L1) or 3 (L2) energy | Return 1 drawn card to deck; draw replacement from any deck (same level) |

### 6. Scoring & End Game  
- Score VP via:  
  - Frame effects (center/bottom rows)  
  - Mandrills (high VP potential)  
  - Computer (5 VP)  
  - Removing cards with rage (rage bonus may include VP)  
  - King‚Äôs level 1/2 apes (3/6 VP each when revealed)  
- **Game ends** when **any player reaches 80+ VP** at the end of a phase.  
  - If during **P1**, game ends **immediately**  
- **Solo mode**: You win only if you reach 80 VP **before or when** the King does.  
- **Tie at 80+**: highest total VP wins; if still tied, **shared victory**  
- **Premature end**: If not enough cards to satisfy all attractions, game ends after current round.  
  - **Priority**: player with **highest ID number** in Primate Assembly chooses first, then next highest, etc.

### 7. FAQ or Edge Cases  
- **Can I skip frames?** Yes. You may skip any frame, even if activatable.  
- **Can Chimpanzee activate same frame twice?** Yes, as long as it‚Äôs closed and condition met.  
- **Can I remove a card not in current Assembly?** No. Only cards in current Primate Assembly.  
- **Do rage bonuses stack for Chimpanzees?** Yes. Each Chimpanzee gives **all** Tamarin rage bonuses from that round.  
- **What if King lacks resources I need?** Take from general supply.  
- **Can I use object effects in solo mode?** Yes, same as multiplayer. King only uses **Computer** (if not in general pool).  
- **Can I copy King‚Äôs disc if I can‚Äôt attract ape?** Yes. Copying only gives bonus, not attraction right.
