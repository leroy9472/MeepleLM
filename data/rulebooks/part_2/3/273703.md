# Comprehensive Reference Manual

## 1. Lore & Objective
The goal of Altar Quest is to complete the quest defined by the current quest setup. This typically involves exploring rooms, overcoming threats, and completing specific objectives such as finding artifacts or defeating enemies. Players lose if any hero is defeated or if a game effect causes defeat.

## 2. Components
- Hero decks (character, action, feat, reaction, equipment, ally cards)
- Quest, feature, threat, villain, lurker, and search decks
- Hero dice (teal six-sided dice with success, focus, success+focus, and critical symbols)
- Altar dice (six-sided dice with rhune symbols: Wathe, Volkris, Brase, Lowek, Aluna, Nethander)
- Miniatures (heroes, enemies, villain, features, lurker)
- Tokens (focus, supply, armor, threat, damage, search, quest)
- Play areas (hero, threat, villain, quest)
- Game board with rooms, door spaces, feature spaces, and shadow spaces

## 3. Setup
1. Choose heroes and place their character cards, forming hero play areas with equipment/ally cards to the right.
2. Create threat, villain, and quest areas.
3. Place quest setup card in quest area, then add altar card to its right.
4. Roll 5 altar dice to form the altar pool.
5. Prepare threat deck and place reference card nearby.
6. Place villain card "Scheme" side up.
7. Shuffle search and lurker decks.
8. Construct feature deck: set aside "Altar Found" and 2 random feature cards, shuffle remaining 5, then place the 3 set-aside cards underneath.
9. Place stairs tile in a room's feature space and heroes on the stairs tile.

## 4. Gameplay Flow
Each round consists of:
1. Hero Turn (each hero performs act phase)
2. Threat Turn (each hero resolves react phase)
3. Villain Turn (resolve villain area, draw villain card)
4. Quest Turn (resolve quest area)

## 5. Core Mechanics

### Hero Actions (each costs 1 action unless noted)
- **Move**: Move up to 3 spaces. Can be taken multiple times.
- **Card Action**: Resolve an Action effect on a card in play.
- **Interact**: Resolve an Interact effect on a card in play if within range.
- **Play Action Card**: Play an Action card from hand.
- **Draw 1 Card**: Draw one card from your deck.
- **Rest**: Discard 1 supply to heal 2 damage.
- **Channel**: Gain 1 focus token and change 1 altar die to any result.

### Free Actions (no action cost)
- **Open a door**: When in a door space, open a door to reveal a room (once per act phase).
- **Trade**: Trade any number of search cards with an adjacent hero.
- **Play Feat Card**: Play during action step without spending an action.
- **Exhaust/Use effects**: Resolve Exhaust or Use effects at appropriate times.

### Room Reveal (when opening door to unrevealed room)
1. Place door mini.
2. Draw feature card, place in quest area, place corresponding miniature.
3. Draw quest card, read narrative, resolve "When Revealed" effect, place in quest area (attach to feature if required).
4. Each hero draws and resolves 1 threat card:
   - Minion spawns in shadow space (nearest empty space if occupied)
   - Trap token placed adjacent to feature, nearest active hero
   - If no threat cards available, draw lurker card instead (spawns like minion)

### Tests
Types: Test, Attack, Inlict, Resist, Search, Task.

#### Test Procedure:
1. **Gather Dice**: Roll number of hero dice equal to tested attribute value.
2. **Modify Dice**: Apply effects that add/remove dice (e.g., discard supply for +1 die).
3. **Roll Dice**: Roll the dice pool.
4. **Resolve Critical Results**: Each critical counts as success and grants +1 die. Continue until no criticals.
5. **Use Focus**: Spend focus tokens to convert focus results to successes.
6. **Cancel Successes**: Apply test-specific cancellation (e.g., search tokens cancel search successes).
7. **Apply Results**: Apply remaining successes based on test type.
8. **Gain Focus**: Gain 1 focus token per remaining focus result (max 5 per hero).

### Enemy Mechanics
- **Engage**: Enemy moves to optimal position to target nearest character.
- **Spawn**: Minions spawn in shadow space (nearest empty space if occupied). Villain spawns in shadow space nearest most characters.
- **Defeat**: Remove miniature, clear tokens, discard card. Heroes gain 1 supply.

### Card Types
- **Action**: Play during action step using "Play Action Card" action.
- **Feat**: Play during action step without spending an action.
- **Reaction**: Play in response to specific triggers (only 1 per timing instance).
- **Ongoing**: Remains in play until discarded.
- **Event**: Resolved and discarded.

### Keywords
- **Action**: Resolved during hero act phase using action.
- **Exhaust**: Resolve by exhausting card (turn sideways); if followed by rhune, must spend corresponding altar die.
- **Interact**: Resolved during interact action when in range.
- **Use**: Resolved at any time; discards card.
- **Activate**: Resolved during appropriate phase (threat, villain, quest).
- **Trigger**: Resolves when character enters range.

### Rhune Effects
- On hero cards: Optional when resolving.
- On non-hero cards: Mandatory when resolving.
- When resolving rhune effect: Re-roll corresponding altar die, then resolve effect text.

## 6. Scoring & End Game
- **Win**: Complete quest objectives (specific to each quest).
- **Lose**: Any hero defeated or game effect causes loss.
- **Supply**: Gain 1 supply when defeating minion or overcoming trap.
- **Progress**: Task tests place progress tokens on cards to complete objectives.

## 7. FAQ or Edge Cases
- **Focus tokens**: Max 5 per hero. Gain after test resolution based on focus results.
- **Supply tokens**: Shared resource. Unlimited unless specified.
- **Armor tokens**: Spend to cancel damage from attack or inflict tests.
- **Reaction cards**: Only 1 may be played per timing instance.
- **Lurker cards**: Not minion threat cards. Drawn when no threat cards available.
- **Room reveal limit**: Only 1 room revealed per hero act phase.
- **Hand size**: No maximum hand size.
- **Diagonal movement**: Allowed except through doorways (must move orthogonally between rooms).
- **Feature blocking**: Features block movement but not range or line of sight.
- **Line of sight**: Requires same room OR adjacent room with open door, and unobstructed line (walls and closed doors block).
- **Card placement**: Place cards in threat, villain, and quest areas left to right in order drawn.
- **Exhausted cards**: Can still resolve non-Exhaust effects; refresh during hero turn.
- **Mulligan**: One-time opportunity to replace starting hand.
